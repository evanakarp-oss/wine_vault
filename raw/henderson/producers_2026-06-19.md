# Henderson Selections — Producer Roster (2026-06-19)

Source: https://www.hendersonselections.com/wine-producers — pasted by Evan on 2026-06-19.
Structure: COUNTRY (UPPERCASE) → producer · region (mixed-case), as listed on the site.

Henderson Selections is a Texas-based (Austin) artisanal wine distributor/importer,
founded 2012. Tagline "Place Over Process." Low-intervention wines from small
vignerons; one of the more stylistically diverse portfolios in the US. Markets:
Texas (via Classified Wines & Spirits), expanding to NY/NJ.

Spellings are preserved as scraped (a few are off vs. the producers' own labels —
e.g. "Cinncinato" → Cincinnato, "Capolio Perlingieri" → Capolino Perlingieri,
"Fruili" → Friuli, "Elian Daros" → Elian Da Ros, "Maranones" → Marañones).
