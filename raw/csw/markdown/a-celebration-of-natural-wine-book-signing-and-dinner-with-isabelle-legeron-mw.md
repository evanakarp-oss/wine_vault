# A Celebration of Natural Wine: Book Signing and Dinner with Isabelle Legeron MW!

*10/22/14 -*

**

*I am French, I taste for a living, and I recently rediscovered wine. After years learning about wine and tasting bucket loads of the stuff - from the cheap and cheerful to bottles worth a building in some parts of the world - I seem to have come full circle. All I want is real , delicious, natural wine. -Isabelle Legeron MW*

MW stands for "Master of Wine" and it’s a really big deal - the highest honor in the wine world, like being a Cardinal of the Church or winning a no-hitter (coincidentally there are just a few more MWs than there have been no-hitters). An MW candidate must be invited and mentored to even be considered for the honor of taking the three-part exam, which includes blind tasting and successfully identifying 36 wines from throughout the world. This might sound easy, but it requires an encyclopedic knowledge of wine and a savant-like tasting ability to pinpoint three dozen wines from among the constantly expanding possibilities. Isabelle Legeron is the first French female MW in history, and a dynamic advocate of the kind of natural wines that we love!

Isabelle has won a following through *That Crazy French Woman* on the Travel Channel UK, organizing RAW (the hugely successful annual natural wine festival in London) and now through the recent publication of *Natural Wine: An Introduction to Organic and Biodynamic Wines Made Naturally* , which includes tasting notes on over 140 of the world's most exciting natural wines.

We are delighted to welcome Isabelle on **Thursday, November 13th** , from 5:00 to 6:30pm for an in-store tasting and book signing of *Natural Wine* , which already graces our shelves. As always, these preliminary celebrations are open to all and free of charge. The ** festivities will continue at acclaimed downtown restaurant Contra, where a custom six-course tasting menu will be paired with some of the most influential producers of natural wines featured in Isabelle's book. We’ve had a couple of other dinners at Contra and they were a huge hit - expect delicious, satisfying food from Chefs Jeramiah Stone & Fabian Von Hauske and the warm, expert hospitality of Wine Director Jorge Riera.

Gossamer-winged dinner tickets are $180, which includes meal, wine, taxes, and gratuity. Dinner begins at 7:00pm. Contra is located at 138 Orchard Street (between Rivington and Delancey Streets); New York, NY 10002. We hope to see you there!
