# Jean Foillard Morgon "Cote de Py" 2010 - The Second Coming!

*﻿2/16/12 -*

For the second year in a row, the distribution of Jean Foillard's beautiful Morgon Cote de Py has been, well, irregular. We apologize for any panic we may have caused among Beaujolais lovers by our initial two case inventory - we have received a second delivery and can now offer this great wine in case quantities, even though our allocation remains well below last year's level. Foillard's 2010 is a superb wine with firm acidity and lower alcohol than in 2009. Balanced and lovely, with beautiful medium-bodied fruit, we would recommend holding it for a few years, although a bottle opened at our staff party the other night disappeared almost immediately...
