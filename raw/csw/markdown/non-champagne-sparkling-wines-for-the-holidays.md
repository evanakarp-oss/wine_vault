# (Non-Champagne) Sparkling Wines for the Holidays

12/20/25 -

![](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0602/3790/6145/files/NonChampersPic.jpg?v=1766805573)

With Christmas right around the corner, it seemed like it was time to start suggesting some sparkling wines for the end of the year. For today's offer, here is a collectiion of our favorite non-Champagne sparkling wines, be they Methode Traditionelle or Methode Ancestrale (aka Pétillant Naturel) or "Charmat" as in Prosecco and Lambrusco. As much as we love Champagne, we're very fond of our non-Champagne sparkling section and hope that you will be too!

Some highlights from local New York wineries are the Pet-Nats from Chëpìka (a collaboration between Nathan Kendall and our good friend Pascaline Lepeltier) and the Claro Que Si! Pet-Nat Riesling from another Finger Lakes project, Barbichette. The Laurent Barth Petillant Naturel from Alsace is back and as delicious as ever! We also have plenty of fantastic Crémants, great values like the Terre di Marca Prosecco and Castellroig Corpinnat for any upcoming parties, and many other delicious and fizzy options ready for delivery or pick-up!
