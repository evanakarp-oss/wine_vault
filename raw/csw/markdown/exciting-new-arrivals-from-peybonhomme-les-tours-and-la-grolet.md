# Exciting New Arrivals From Peybonhomme-les-Tours and La Grolet!

*﻿4/10/2009 -*

![](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0602/3790/6145/files/525_600x600.jpg?v=1694661193)

We were fortunate to taste again with Jean-Luc Hubert of Chateau Peybonhomme-les-Tours and Chateau La Grolet last February at a Biodynamic event in the Loire Valley. We're happy to report that the new releases from these two great organic and biodynamic estates were consistently superb! Our new order arrived on April 15th and included their great new rosé, Le Clairet de Peybonhomme, as well as new vintages of four of our favorite wines. These are, without a doubt, the finest values in Bordeaux today and we are proud to be importing them directly - and passing on the savings to you! Only natural products are used at these estates, all chemical fertilizers, pesticides and herbicides are banned, and biodiversity is encouraged in the vineyard. This is natural (and affordable!) winemaking at it's best - we urge you to try these elegant wines!
