# New Arrivals from the Jura -

*﻿9/26/15 -*

![](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0602/3790/6145/files/25_45da9546-e146-4726-9fa2-a2513b2bd0d0_480x480.jpg?v=1697076388)

*(Pont de Breux, near Salins-les-Bains)*

Given the scarcity and popularity of the best wines from the Jura, we're very happy to offer new arrivals and returning favorites from some of the regions best producers. And we are very excited to be collaborating with our friends down the street at Racines NY where they will pour a special selection of Jura wines by-the-glass on Tuesday September 29 th from 6 pm until closing, with Chambers Street's Tim Gagnon in attendance! We will feature bottlings from the always fantastic Peggy and Jean-Pascal Buronfosse in Rotalier, delicious wines from Arbois made by Céline and Steve Gormally (Les Dolomies), the recently-arrived wines of Geraud and Pauline Fromont of Domaine des Marnes Blanches in Ste-Agnès, Domaine Saint-Pierre, Domaine Pont de Breux and a couple of interesting surprises from the cellar. Chef Fréderic Duca will be serving up his elegant cuisine as always, including a special Jura dish of "poularde au vin jaune, chanterelles sautees et pomme puree" so join us for dinner, snacks, or just to taste some of the most compelling wines this region has to offer! **
