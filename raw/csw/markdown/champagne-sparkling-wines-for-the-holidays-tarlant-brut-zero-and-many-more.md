# Champagne & Sparkling Wines for the Holidays: Tarlant Brut Zero and Many More!

12/21/2024

![](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0602/3790/6145/files/e8e0fb1c-21d4-4bed-aa93-1d970ba33a43_1_480x480.jpg?v=1735317424)

**As it's time to get together with friends and family for the end of year holidays, we offer a tasty array of superb grower Champagnes and unique sparkling wines to accompany your celebrations!**

**We'd like to feature the beautiful Champagnes of Benoît and Mélanie Tarlant** - Benoit's visit in November went so well that we sold out of everything except **Brut Zero** white & rosé and the marvelous **Etincelante 2002** - these are gorgeous wines for these special occasions! (Tarlant vines pictured above) (Sold out again, now arriving 12/23)

**The two pioneers of organic farming in Champagne are here - Laval and Beaufort** , along with favorites like Ponson, Mouzon, Huré, Ruppert-Leroy and many more…

**And we present a delicious assortment of our favorite non-Champagne sparkling wines** , be they Methode Traditionelle (we can't say "Champenoise" anymore) or Methode Ancestrale (aka Pétillant Naturel) or "Charmat" as in Prosecco and Lambrusco…

**Magnums** can be found at the end of the list…
