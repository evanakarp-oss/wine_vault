# Welcome Mosel Growers: Ulli Stein and Matthias Knebel!

*﻿3/18/2009 -*

![](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0602/3790/6145/files/518_600x600.jpg?v=1694661940)

*(Ulli Stein)*

We are unbelievably excited and honored to have Ulli Stein and Matthias Knebel pouring their wines in the store this Friday. They are two of our favorite small Mosel estates — 5 and 7 hectares; and both produce distinctive, intelligent, breath-taking wines.

Also joining them will be Mosel Wine Merchant’s Lars Carlberg and Dan Melia, who will be pouring some other selections from their portfolio – wines from Steinmetz, Clemens Busch and Von Nell.

These wines are mostly dry or off-dry in style and beautifully articulate of Mosel terroirs.
