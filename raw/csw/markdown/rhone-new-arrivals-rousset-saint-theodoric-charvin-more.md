At any rate, today's Rhône new arrivals offer contains some truly great wines!! And with  a 15% discount today only, hoping to get your attention in the pre-holiday rush. We lead off with the superb Crozes-Hermitages from **Stéphane Rousset** .  We have a small allocation of the extraordinary **2023 "Les Picaudières,** " from a steep terraced vineyard, rivaling  Hermitage in quality - it's perhaps the best value in the Northern Rhône. The wonderful **2024 Crozes-Hermitage Marsanne Vieilles Vignes** is a unique and beautiful wine that deserves your attention along with the **2023 Saint-Joseph and the 2023 Crozes-Hermitage Blanc** . **Châteauneuf-du-Pape** We're proud to offer 2022 and 2023s from three of the very finest estates: Saint Théodoric, Domaine Charvin and Domaine Pierre André as well as a superb value from the organic/Biodynamic Domaine Lafond. (Please note: the Saint Théodoric "Les Sablons," from younger vines and not made in every vintage, offers the same elegance and freshness from the sandy/clay soils as "Le Grand Pin" and "la Guigasse" at a more moderate price. The 2014 was particularly delicious...) *Pierre André vineyards on the sandy soils of "La Guigasse," near Saint Théodoric*

**Gigondas** The brilliant organic wines of the Clos du Joncuas are back, including new releases from 2016 and 2017. These are complex, full-bodied traditional Gigondas, from great farming, fermented with whole-clusters, and aged in cement or foudres. Drink the 2016 and 2017 now or in ten years, and enjoy the more forward 2022 until 2035 - 2040. The 2023 Vacqueyras is one of the best wines of this under-appreciated region, and is a great value. Don't overlook the beautiful 2024 Gigondas Rosé which will drink beautifully over the next three years. **Tavel** Domaine Lafond is "a leading edge domaine at Tavel" says Drink Rhone, organic since 2012 and now Biodynamic. We retasted the 2024 Tavel Roc-Epine a few days ago which is beginning to open up and is drinking sensationally with deep berry flavors and a full supple palate with terrific length. Enjoy now and over the next two to three years. Ridiculous price!
