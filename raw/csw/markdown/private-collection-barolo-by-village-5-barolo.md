# Private Collection - Barolo by Village, #5: Barolo

12/11/2024

![](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0602/3790/6145/files/f75b840d-6904-4a8e-886c-5a75d7e37315_1_480x480.jpg?v=1734455921)

There is a ton of compelling wine on this offer, but we'd be remiss not to draw extra attention to the amazing collection of Barolo from Francesco Rinaldi. We've been lucky to taste the wines back to the 1940's, and they stand out for their devotion to quality, tradition, and consistency that persists until the present day. For reasons we don't quite understand, the Francesco Rinaldi wines remain one of the best value : quality propositions in Barolo, and the recent vintages are a downright steal.

*-Jamie Wolff*
