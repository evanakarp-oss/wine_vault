We are once again thrilled to offer another magnificent collection of Produttori del Barbaresco on a pre-arrival basis from one of our oldest and most reliable Italian collector friends. We continue to find that the cooperative has been consistently producing outstanding wines, dating back to the late 50's. Along with the ‘straight’ Barbaresco, in the best vintages the Produttori bottle 9 different single vineyards as Barbaresco Riservas; in the not-so-great years all of the Riserva vineyards are blended together to make a single bottling of Barbaresco. This reliably produces a wine that will outshine the reputation of even the least sought after vintages.
