# 43rd Annual Louis/Dressner Tasting and Benefit for Partners-in-Health, Monday March 14th at The Ten Bells!

3/8/16 -

Please join us on Monday, March 14th as Louis/Dressner Selections and Chambers Street Wines present 22 of the finest estates in France and Italy at a tasting to benefit Partners-in-Health. "We go. We make house calls. We build health systems. We stay ." This is a great opportunity to taste and talk with these talented vignerons in optimal, uncrowded conditions, particularly for those who reserve an early time-slot. This will be a sensational tasting and should not be missed! Here is the list of estates represented and winemakers attending:

Rémi Branger , Domaine de la Pépière, Muscadet; Thierry Puzelat , Clos du Tue Bœuf, Touraine; Joseph Mosse , Domaine René Mosse, Anjou; Evelyne de Jessy , Domaine du Closel, Savennières; Eric Texier , Rhône; Georges Descombes , Morgon; Kevin Descombes , Morgon; Damien Coquelet , Beaujolais; François Cazin , Cheverny; Claude-Benoît & Claude-Emmanuelle Desvignes , Morgon; François Pinon , Vouvray; Jean-Paul Brun , Domaine des Terres Dorées, Beaujolais; Christelle Rénardat , Domaine Rénardat-Fache, Bugey; Julien Ilbert , Combel-la-Serre, Cahors; Ariana Occhipinti , Sicilia; Sebastiano de Bartoli , De Bartoli, Sicilia; Nico Sciackitano , La Stoppa, Emilia-Romagna; Francesca Padovani , Fonterenza, Montalcino; Saša Radikon , Radikon, Oslavia; Jeanne Dumas-Chalifour , Cascina Degli Ulivi, Ligure; Elisabetta Foradori , Trentino; Silvio Messana , Montesecondo, Toscana.

**Please call CSW at 212-227-1434 or email office@chambersstwines.com to reserve your time-slot, and pay at the door (cash only) when you arrive. You can also purchase tickets below for the time-slot desired and choose In-Store Pick-up - we'll have your receipt at the tasting** .

**The Ten Bells** , America's first natural wine bar, on the fashionable Lower East Side at **247 Broome Street, between Ludlow and Orchard.**

**Partners-in-Health** : "Whether to Liberia, Rwanda, or any of the countries we work and live in, we go where we’re needed most. We care for patients in their homes and communities. We work in close partnership with local government officials and the world’s leading medical and academic institutions to build capacity and strengthen health systems. And we stay, committed to accompanying the people and communities we serve for the long term." **For more information or to donate on-line please go to www.pih.org .**
