**Terre des Chardons**

Some notes from this unique estate…

"The Chardon family, originally from Azay-le-Rideau in the Loire Valley, settled in the Gard commune of Bellegarde in the early 1980s. Today, Terre des Chardons covers 9 hectares on the Costières plateau at the heart of their organic farm dedicated to vegetable growing and olive growing, producing **Demeter-certified Biodynamic** wines since the first harvest in 2002. All of the wines are in the Protected Costières de Nîmes Appellation and Protected Clairette de Bellegarde Appellation.

**Clairette de Bellegarde** is a micro-appellation of only 9 hectares giving birth only to a single-varietal white wine (100% white clairette). It's a superb and powerful dry white in the spirit of the great wines of the Rhône Valley, produced only in Bellegarde on the hectares bordering the medieval village.

Demeter certified since our first harvest, we use only natural products. Our philosophy is to help the vine reveal its terroir for a more personal expression of our wines. The soil is made up of siliceous stones, once deposited by the Rhone, locally called "gress". These stones, heated during the day by the sun, release this heat at night for better ripening of the grapes. They give the soil better filtering properties and therefore guarantee natural freshness (especially important for the Syrah.) They facilitate access of the roots to deeper water tables and thus offer the vines better resistance to summer drought.

Our work in the biodynamic vineyard is mainly based on the principle of dynamization and on the use of specific natural preparations in homeopathic doses. This allows for better vitality, a good balance of the vine and the soil and deeper rooting for a faithful expression of the terroir. The diversity of fauna and flora in the vineyard is also a key factor present within the domain…"
