Our *perception* , when tasting the wines, was that there may have been some microbiological issues in recent vintages. Without having analyzed the wines, that remains pure speculation, but after 2020 we have not found the same level of quality that we did in the 2016-2020 period. That’s a pity, because there is so much to like about these properties and the people behind them, and I have purchased their wines for my own cellar.
