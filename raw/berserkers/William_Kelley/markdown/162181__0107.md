> *Replying to :*  
> subject to tasting the 2008 Comtes It’s really very good, I’ve had it four times now. Score?

There’s a review of a preview disgorgement on our site, and one of the commercial disgorgement publishing this month (spoiler: there wasn’t much difference between the two).

In the context of some of the 2008 prestige cuvées released so far, I would rank the Comtes behind Cristal Rosé; on a similar level to Cristal; a touch ahead of Bollinger Grande Année and Pol Roger Churchill; and a couple of points ahead of the 2008 Dom Pérignon. But, this is to compare chalk and cheese, as all these bottlings have very different styles. Of course, there are a few 2008 releases to come, but I think it’s clear that Comtes numbers among the best of all the Grandes Marques’ offerings in this vintage. At some point, I’m looking forward to tasting them all side by side over an afternoon…
