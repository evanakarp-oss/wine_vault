I honestly think that an evolving market has contributed to the Collin price rises more than anything. What has happened is that Collin’s wines, and those of a handful of other Champagne producers (and they are my favorites, too), have gotten the attention of people (including rather new consumers) who also buy very expensive Burgundy et al. The people who drink Rayas and Lamy-Caillat in Shanghai are drinking Ulysse Collin, too, and I can’t fault their taste.
