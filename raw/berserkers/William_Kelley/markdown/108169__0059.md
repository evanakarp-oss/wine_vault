Had the '78 Latour three times and two out of three times were fantastic. 1978 La Chapelle is honestly still too young. Amazing isn’t it? 37 years old! 1990 La Chapelle probably in…

I honestly have my doubts about the 1990 La Chapelle, and sold the bottles I had. After getting half way through a case I concluded the wine was overripe and getting more pruney and oxidative with each bottle. To my mind the last great La Chapelle is actually the '88.

Back on topic, the '78 Jaboulet Cornas was absolutely fantastic at the end of 2014! One of the very greatest Cornas I’ve ever tasted. So that is well worth checking out if you ever see it!
