Interesting wine. I last had this about a year ago, and discussed it with Eric Baugher at Ridge. Apparently it’s one of the lowest tannin vintages of Monte Bello ever made. But it is still very vibrant and youthful for all that. Its unusually luscious style makes it rather singular.
