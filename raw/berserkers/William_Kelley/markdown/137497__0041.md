Cécile Tremblay makes a nice Beaux Monts. It seems otiose to mention Emmanuel Rouget’s.

Petit Monts is usually one of Maxime’s best wines at Georges Noellat.

Chaumes is often so facile that it is hard to take it seriously as a 1er cru. I’d rather drink Mugneret-Gibourg Vosne village.

Brûlées is arguably Méo’s best wine. Bruno Clavelier also makes a lovely rendition.

Reignots - Felettig is quite under the radar, think it all goes to Asia though. It used to be blended into the Vosne 1er cru. Cathiard’s vines here are young.

Croix Rameau - not that I know of, and I do not really have an idea of what this vineyard’s identity is

Les Rouges - Tremblay, Grivot, but let’s not talk about this!
