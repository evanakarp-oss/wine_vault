> *Replying to :*  
> In any event, there appears to be some disagreement between critics about which of these wines is superior, as the 2008 Cristal has slightly higher overall scores than the 2008 Cristal Rose, and both have multiple 100 point scores, 99 point scores, etc.

Not really, there are just some commentators who didn’t taste the rosé and gave the regular Cristal 100. I can’t believe that anyone who tasted them both would give them the same score. (Edit: I stand corrected, I see Suckling, Wine Enthusiast and Wine Spectator all did this. All I can say is that they’re mistaken.)

Your point about rosé being a different beast holds in some cases, but Cristal rosé is in fact barely pink in color: more importantly, it’s a selection of the very best blocks that go into Cristal, in 2008, four out of the 45 that inform the Cristal blend. So it might make sense to think of it as a sort of “super Cristal”. If it wasn’t a step up, it would mean someone somewhere had made a mistake.
