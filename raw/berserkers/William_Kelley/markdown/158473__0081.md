> *Replying to :*  
> So it is difficult to know what exactly I can do in the pages of The Wine Advocate , short of saying, for example, “these young wines from producer x taste great, but anecdotal evidence from collector friends suggests that they may well oxidize”. And if I were to write that, I can readily…

That is more or less how I do approach the subject. In the ‘producer profiles’ that accompany each set of tasting notes I generally try to indicate if producers are analyzing dissolved oxygen, what their élevage practices are, and what kind of bottling lines and closures they are using. And I give a much shorter drinking window, starting sooner, to producers that make wines that are known to evolve rapidly vs those that don’t. But this thread makes me resolve to ask more questions and give more information more systematically, for sure.
