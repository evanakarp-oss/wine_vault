Nice—and accurate—note.

One decade-plus is the rule for the more backward vintages of Claude Dugat’s Gevrey AOC. 2002 and 2001 are drinking beautifully now, for example.

My estimate would be that 2013 needs another 5 years or so to reveal some of its potential.

2011 is good today with a decant, and transcends the vintage’s reputation.
