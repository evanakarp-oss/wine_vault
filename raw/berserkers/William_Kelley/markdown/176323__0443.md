Thanks for noticing this!

One challenge is that when resisting score inflation / compression, the alternative that is wheeled out is often scoring “conservatively”, which means in practice never giving 100/100 and never scoring lesser-known producers or appellations highly. What I am trying to do instead is to open up the scale, while using it fluidly to express differences in quality and preference. This wine from the Lafleur stable (actually from just outside Fronsac AOC, though on the same soils) I hope exemplifies that.
