Under-ripeness definitely isn’t part of the answer. All the celebrated vintages back then were the ripe ones, not the tart ones like today, and when you ask any of the old-timers how they decided when to pick, they will say words to the effect of, “when the grapes were golden, dummy”. The under-ripe vintages of the 1970s mostly make for pretty unfriendly drinking today.
