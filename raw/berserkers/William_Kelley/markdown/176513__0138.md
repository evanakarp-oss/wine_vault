I do! And there are more and more producers who are doing it (Lachaux, Lamy, Bouley etc). Of course, the corollary is really that they have to stop selling EP as well…

In some cases, e.g. de Montille and Barthod, where the cellars are cold and malolactics late, I started tasting from bottle irrespective. I do not consider myself a creative writer, and I prefer if my tasting notes have a real and unambiguous referent.
