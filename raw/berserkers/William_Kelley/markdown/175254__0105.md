Complex question. She shows the most emotion talking about the RSV, and on some level I think that’s her favorite. I think she admires the Musigny the most (“this might be the greatest grand cru”, was what she said last month when we tasted the 2019). But Chambertin is very important to her, as the 1955 Maison Leroy Chambertin is her declared inspiration for the wines she tries to make at Domaine Leroy, and she worked the 1955 vintage at the Domaine des Comtes Saint Quentin, who were important proprietors in Gevrey (they owned the parcel of CdB that went to Clair Dau, then divided between Jadot and Bruno Clair).
