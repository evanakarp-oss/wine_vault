> *Replying to :*  
> Reading from another source, the Cab Franc is actually a massale selection from Lafleur called “Bouchet” (a clone?). Now I am curious whether there is any notable difference here.

You can read that in TWA as well. “Bouchet” is just the traditional name for Cabernet Franc on the right bank, before ampelographers told them what it was. The team at Lafleur call it Bouchet to underline that they’re using pre-clonal massale selections.
