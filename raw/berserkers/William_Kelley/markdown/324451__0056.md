Aubert is actually quite explicit in saying that the wines can drink very well young. He often cites the 1969 de Vogüé Musigny, which he drank a bunch of in the early 1970s, as never having tasted better than out of the gates. And I think in riper, much higher pH vintages than we used to have in the past, that’s more likely to occur than it was in the 1980s or '90s. So in short, I think the website isn’t sending any kind of implicit message, it just hasn’t been updated for a while.
