> *Replying to :*  
> Gautier, I have a question for you if you happen to see this. Did you separately bottle Viré-Clessé and Quintaine in 2017 as distinct wines, or are these the same wine with different labels for different markets? I finally got around to trying this - am in NC like Nathan and Fred and ended up…

Not Gautier, but the AOC is Viré-Clessé (you will see this on the back label) but the hamlet whence the wine derives is Quintaine. On a legal level, the back label, which has the AOC on it, it actually the “front label”.
