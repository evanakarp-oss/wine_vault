Kalin stopped producing after the 2001 vintage. So there is a fair bit of unreleased wine from the late 1990s through 2001, and a bit of “library” stock going back to the very first days of the winery in the mid 1970s! There are also some sparkling wines, including late disgorged stuff from 1989, 1990, 1991 and 1992 which are some of the most interesting traditional method I’ve tried from outside Champagne.
