> *Replying to :*  
> I think the “boots on the ground” type of knowledge in Bordeaux is a bit less useful than in other regions since the big estates are fairly corporate and more transparent than, say, in Burgundy.

I can see why you might think that, but in reality there is a lot of interesting stuff going on in the vineyards that hasn’t been all that well reported on. You are still more likely to hear the name of the architect who designed the tasting room than the name of the vineyard manager from the wine press. So there’s definitely room to improve here.
