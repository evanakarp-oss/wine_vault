> Great notes. Interesting to see your report on the 2009 Criots. I passed up an opportunity to drink it earlier this year in favor of what turned out to be quite a boring 1999 Leflaive Bâtard, and I have been kicking myself ever since. Oh, and for “probably the best white wine being made in…

We didn’t give it five hours, though we gave it close to that in a decanter, but it just seemed inherently introverted and quite far stylistically from the great Leflaive vintages of the 70s and 80s, to an extent that made it hard for me to love.
