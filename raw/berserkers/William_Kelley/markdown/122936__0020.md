Seconded.

Roulot is also investigating the recycled glass question, in conjunction with a chemist if I recall correctly. Seems around the mid-1990s EU regulations mandated the inclusion of  a certain percentage of recycled glass in the raw materials for bottle production. The idea is that that may have resulted in traces of metal oxides, which catalyze oxidation, getting into the glass. Now that may sound far-fetched, but if only more producers took such a thorough interest in premox and preventing it!
