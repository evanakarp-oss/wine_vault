I was sorry to miss the pick-up party. But I did taste a nice line up of the Rhys 2007s the other day at a harvest party in Napa, and can report that all the wines were showing beautifully, and are still very youthful. The 2007 Alpine Chardonnay was vibrant and expressive, and all the Pinot Noirs were beginning to develop some lovely complexity but still have a way to go before they reach anything like their peak. I didn’t take any notes worthy of their own thread, but people who own these should feel very happy about the positive evolution going on in bottle.
