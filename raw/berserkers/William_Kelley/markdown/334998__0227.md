What’s described here isn’t manipulation, just the nature of presenting cask samples that have in some cases only been in barrel a couple of months! I think tasting this early during élevage is similarly difficult in most regions (tasting praedikat wine just after fermentation has been stopped would probably be harder). It is definitely useful and interesting, but it’s also important not to be naive about its limitations—or the fact that some vintages are more readable early than others.
