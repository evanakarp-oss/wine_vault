1995 Napa was pretty different to Napa 20+ years later. If you look at the USDA annual crush report, in 1995 the average recorded brix for Napa Cabernet was 23.6 (roughly 14.0%). By 2015, it was 26.1 brix (roughly 15.5%). And of course, plenty of wines are picked above that and watered back.
