I do not know where you are buying your asparagus, Larry, or what you are doing with it. I like asparagus and enjoy the variations between what the Belgians do in Mechelen, the sandy Landes in the south-west of France, the partially-earthed version from Provence, especially around Uzès, and what the locals do here in Burgundy. None of them have anything to do with what I tasted, but maybe that is because my asparagus is not actually all that vegetal.

Anyway, as I made clear in my comments, it was only with decanting that there was a big shift in the aromatics. So I can easily imagine people following from bottle for several days with similar results.
