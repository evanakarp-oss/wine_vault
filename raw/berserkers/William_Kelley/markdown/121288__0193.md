Just chiming in after a few days in Germany. Tasted all the Keller 15 GGs, which are frankly as good a set of young wines from any producer and vintage as I’ve ever tasted. Really phenomenal stuff. Also tried some terrific stuff from Seehof in the Rheinhessen and Schlossgut Diel in the Nahe.

I am beginning to feel pretty complacent about the quality of the vintage. Very happy to be stocking up. Keller GGs were, felicitously enough, offered in the UK today.
