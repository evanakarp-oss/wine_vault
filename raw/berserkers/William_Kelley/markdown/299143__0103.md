I actually think they will age pretty well, without ever loosing their broad-shouldered, fat, somewhat sweet and sloppy style. Certainly slower than the 2015s, which are generally aging quite fast. You just have to watch out for wines that are out of balance, with exaggerated abvs, or where people went to town with extraction.
