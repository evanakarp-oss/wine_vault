> *Replying to :*  
> I drank the 2005 Château Rayas Châteauneuf-du-Pape Pignan Reserve about 6 years ago and I couldn’t say why it wasn’t a perfect wine… This is an especially good wine and still today a lot more fun to drink than the 2005 Rayas. If it is any indication of how good the 2005 Rayas will be in 2035, well……

It’s still quite blocky and shy, with a lot of depth and muscle, whereas the Pignan is lighter, super perfumed and very charming.
