The Forêts is the richest and most complete wine (which makes sense given the size and location of the parcel). Clos de l’Arlot is the most ethereal. The Suchots is a touch broader-shouldered than the Clos de l’Arlot, but not as concentrated as the Forêts nor as complete. Maybe the vine genetics are not as optimal.
