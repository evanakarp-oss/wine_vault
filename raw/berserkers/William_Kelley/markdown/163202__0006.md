The University of California / Sotheby Book of California Wine (1984) is pretty good, with essays on various aspects of the early history of wine in California (the chapter on Chinese vineyard and cellar workers is especially interesting) including Sonoma County. It’s was a very nicely produced book and you can easily pick it up second hand.
