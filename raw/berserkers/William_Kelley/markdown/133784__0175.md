> *Replying to :*  
> Thanks! This is a great explanation. Does this stand to reason then that the best blocks of the Beckstoffer portion of the vineyard are the ones furthest west? Are those the blocks that Schrader gets? I would be interesting to see a breakdown of which parts of the vineyard the various wines made…

That is true as a rule of thumb, but the details are a bit more complicated, as water is channeled underground by formations of large cobbles / boulders from ancient river activity, which complicate the picture. And the variations in viticulture (head trained at one extreme, 4’x4’ low-prunded VSP at the other), crop levels (<2 tons to >5 per acre), vine material, etc between different blocks and owners are so great that differences deriving from ‘terroir’ would be hard to isolate.
