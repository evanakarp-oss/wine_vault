> Opened 2014 Dureuil-Janthial Puligny-Montrachet 1er Cru Les Champs-Gain last weekend and it was mind blowing. The wine of an absolute grace! I am curious how some of the Grand Crus would sing in Vincent’s cellars if he ever gets his hands on such grapes. Some vineyards are blessed with top…

It’s tempting to pose this question, but in so doing, we are actually validating the hierarchy that has been holding places such as Rully back! If the Meix Cadot Vieilles Vinges is better than most Bâtard-Montrachet, why assume that Vincent’s Bâtard-Montrachet, if he were to make one, would be better than his Meix Cadot Vieilles Vinges? Once one starts putting the question that way, instead, things get really interesting.
