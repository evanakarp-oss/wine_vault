> *Replying to :*  
> Recommendation of the winemaker: open 24 h in advance … my rec: + then decant 1-2 h in advance … this way it should be very enjoyable I have heard of this quite a bit with Rayas (and most Reynaud wines), but it scares me for some reason. I’m scared the wine will get a little too much air…

Don’t be scared of opening 24 hours in advance! It invariably works wonders for these wines. Just don’t let the wine get too warm while it’s open: I open them in the cellar and leave them there.
