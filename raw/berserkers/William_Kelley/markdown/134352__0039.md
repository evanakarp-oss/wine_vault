> *Replying to :*  
> Thanks, William. Interesting thoughts and helpful insights on all three vintages. 2013s could indeed turn out to be quite the bargain. Any particular villages or producers to look out for in 2013?

Well, it’s definitely a vintage to parse by producer. But at the village level, I have loved Domaine Pierre Guillemot’s Savigny VV, Berthaut’s Fixin Les Crais, and both Roumier’s and Barthod’s Chambolle AOC. Liger-Belair’s Nuits Aux Cras is excellent and was delicious from the beginning, and the Georges Noellat Nuits Boudots is also quite precociously attractive, maybe even at its best now. I have not opened many higher appellation bottles, but intend to make a concerted effort to taste more, professionally and personally, in 2018.
