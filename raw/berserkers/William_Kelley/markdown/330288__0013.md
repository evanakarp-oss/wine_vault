I’ve had a few Pinot Noirs from Hokkaido. Not sure I’d quite go as far as umami, which is of course a taste as opposed to a flavor, but all have had what I would describe as a sort of maritime/brine-y/kelp-y flavor, which is probably amplified by stem retention. They made me think of a hypothetical red Muscadet (and yes, I know Pinot Noir is actually grown in the Pays Nantais; and no, it doesn’t taste like this, so it’s a pure thought experiment).
