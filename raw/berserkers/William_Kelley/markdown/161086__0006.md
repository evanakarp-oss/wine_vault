Was really sorry to learn of this the other day. I remember one time visiting Bordeaux as a student, I was walking through the vineyards of Pomerol with some friends on Bastille Day. Denis, who we’d tasted with the week before, pulled up and asked what we were up to. We explained, and then he asked if we were thirsty. When we replied in the affirmative we all ended up back at l’Eglise-Clinet, drinking a bottle of his 2000 vintage. It was one of the very best moments in the whole two-week trip.
