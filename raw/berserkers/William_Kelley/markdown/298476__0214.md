There’s a lot of talk about this, but unless one is seeing château increase their tank capacity by 30+%, it’s tempting to suspect we are looking more at a marketing narrative in many cases than a reality. Stems take a lot of space in tanks, so you need a lot more tank space if you really want to include meaningful percentages of whole bunch. Estates such as Carmes Haut-Brion are actually equipped for this, but most are not.
