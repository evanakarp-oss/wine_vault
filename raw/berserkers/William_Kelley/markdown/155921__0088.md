> *Replying to :*  
> It is en primeur week in London. I’ve seen a few reports here and there and so far the reports appear to be fairly unenthusiastic. None of this will affect my buying - I try to buy the same things every year - but it does seem like a vintage where there is almost no consensus on the reds, other…

Well, any consensus about the vintage as a whole could only be misleading, as it is indeed very variable from domaine to domaine and even sometimes cuvée to cuvée.

That said, now that all the major commentators—except Allen—have published, there is some evident bandwidth between people who liked 18s a great deal and those who, while finding plenty of excellent wines, were a bit more ambivalent about the style of the vintage.
