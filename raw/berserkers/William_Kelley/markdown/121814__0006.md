Have had a few 2013 Ramonets now and am very impressed. Notably superior to their 2012s: more tight-knit, focussed and intense. Seems like their best vintage since 2010. The Bourgogne blanc is very creditable (though bizarrely bottled under little plastic ‘corks’, so something to drink up in the near term), the Boudriotte improved over three days open, with a bit more tension than that cru often has, and the Montrachet, which I had in Beaune a few weeks ago, is terrific!
