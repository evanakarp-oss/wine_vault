There are plenty of big houses who can draw on a broad palette of crus. The advantage Roederer has over many others is that they have invested seriously in farming better for over two decades now. The best 2014 vintage Champagnes were produced by good farmers. Of course, that’s true every year, but a challenging vintage throws it into relief.
