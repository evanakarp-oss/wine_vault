> What’s the style of Thomas wines? Would be my first time trying… There is always a focus on elegance in every wine I’ve had. 2014 & 2015 were a little more fruit-forward than say 2010-2013.

From barrel this summer, the 2016 showed very well. Even though it’s a hotter vintage (marginally) than the hot 2015, it showed a bit more freshness and restraint. It’s still an ampler, broader-scaled vintage but I wouldn’t hesitate to buy.
