> *Replying to :*  
> I was going to say that I was impressed by the airy clusters, but those are quite a few shot berries. Weather-related? Virused? I assume they’ll be eliminated at the sorting table.

Yes, often caused by cooler or wetter weather at flowering - anything that leads to poor fertilization. I left Burgundy a few weeks before flowering but I guess that’s the case here.

They won’t be eliminated though, it is often thought of as a positive for quality, especially in red… sometimes the small berries are riper than the rest, sometimes less ripe; but in either case they obviously increase the solid-to-juice ratio.
