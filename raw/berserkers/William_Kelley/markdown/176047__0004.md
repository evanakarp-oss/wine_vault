> *Replying to :*  
> Hi all I have been doing some research on Les Cailleret (the Puligny-Montrachet one) and am a bit confused by Clos Du Cailleret which I can’t seem to see on any maps. I am correct in saying that it sits inside Les Cailleret? It seems as though Dom. Jean Chartron had it as a Monopole until the 2017…

The Clos du Cailleret is indeed a Clos within Les Caillerets.

The Chartron family sold some vines within the Clos over the years: most recently to the Domaine des Lambrays in 1993, and to Pousse d’Or in 2004.

You can see their name on the old gates: [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/@46.9488723,4.7418557,3a,75y,313.48h,92.44t/data=!3m6!1e1!3m4!1sonQcAhDB8Xo_hKSgrVZzzA!2e0!7i16384!8i8192)

Bill Nanson has everything you might want to know here: [Puligny-Montrachet: Village Profile – Burgundy-Report](https://www.burgundy-report.com/burgundy-report-extra/03-2006/puligny-montrachet-village-profile/)
