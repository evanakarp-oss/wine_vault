When I was there last year Vincent told me it’s actually Pinot Gouges and Chardonnay. My understanding is that ampelographers will tell you that Pinot Gouges, a white mutant of Pinot Noir propagated by the Gouges family, is actually just a kind of Pinot Blanc, but it’s certainly a high quality selection which cannot be said for a lot of Pinot Blanc out there, so the distinction is worth making.

I can attest that the 1982 of this cuvée is cruising along beautifully.
