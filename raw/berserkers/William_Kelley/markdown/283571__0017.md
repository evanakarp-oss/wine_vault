> And would this maybe be why say, Lafleur renamed Acte to Les Perrieres de Lafleur?

They always intended to call it that, when (if) the wine had a sufficiently mature and individual expression to merit it! It derives from the limestone plateau of Fronsac, outside the AOC, and the vineyard (like many in Saint-Emilion and elsewhere) sits on top of big tunnels created in the quarrying of limestone to build the city of Bordeaux. So it isn’t a fanciful name!
