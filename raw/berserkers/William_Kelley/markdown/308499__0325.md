What is interesting is that even with complete crushing by foot, if you then do a static press without any crumbling/breaking the cake, you will have lower pH than whole bunch pressing and breaking the cake 3-4 times. The lowest I have managed to go using this technique is pH 3.04 at 13.6% natural in Meursault Narvaux in 2022. We were at pH 3.10 before precipitation during the winter.
