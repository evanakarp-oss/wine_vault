Looking back at my notes, it seems that I wrote that Hebrart told me he was planning to do the NV 1er Cru BdB as Extra-Brut when I last visited. I guess he decided to cover both bases, but I doubt a few grams of sugar makes much difference to what is a lovely wine. FWIW, I certainly didn’t think the EB needed any more sugar, but nor, I’m sure, would it harm it.
