Was wondering if someone would ask this! For Delys, there’s a foudre (number 8) and a tank, or “cuve” (number 9). But, it’s the same parcel, picked the same day, and the same vinification. And while the élevage is a bit different, the wines are essentially the same. So the differences are much smaller than those previous designated by Bouland’s system of numbering foudres (which, when he applied to other cuvées, I don’t think he ever did for Delys btw).
