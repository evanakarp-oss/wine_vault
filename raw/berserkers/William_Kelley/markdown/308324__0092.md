There is a difference between being a consumer advocate and “a man of the people”, whatever that means in this context. I derive immense joy from wine, but I am not really sure how anyone can represent pushing an expensive consumer good with proven negative health implications as an unmitigated social good. No one should be excluded from drinking wine by constructing artificial barriers of snobbery, language etc, but I am also not trying to convince people to drink alcohol. Moreover, the “people” to which Donald refers is in fact just a tiny subset of people like him.
