> *Replying to :*  
> Three or four visits is quite reasonable for both tasting and time. I can’t imagine doing 6 or 8, but then I don’t do it for a living. Of course if transit time is only a few minutes, or maybe a half hour, then that does make it possible to taste (and properly asses) more.

While it is physically possible to do six to eight, to me it is clear that the results of working like that are less reliable. Moreover, the last slot at the end of the day is unlikely to be allocated to high profile domaines, unless they specially request it of course; and so in a sense working like that reinforces existing perceived producer hierarchies. The issue is not so much available time as fatigue.
