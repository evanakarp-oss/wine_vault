My pleasure Bill.

The 1970 Pape Clement is a terrific wine, but quite high in acidity for Pape Clem. It really pops after three hours in the decanter.

The best surprise I ever had with Pape Clement was the '52, a generally despised vintage. But the '52 PC could pass for a '55 or even a '53. I remember opening the first bottle rather casually with low expectations, then having to call up everyone interested in wine in the neighborhood so they could have a taste, as it was too good not to share! That ended up being one of the most pleasure-giving cases of wine I ever purchased. I speculate other '52 Graves might be interesting too, but have never encountered any.

All best, William.
