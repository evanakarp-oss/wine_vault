They were developed by Michael Paetzold and even have their own instagram account: [https://www.instagram.com/wine.globe/](https://www.instagram.com/wine.globe/)

Some producers such as Arnaud Ente are using them 100%.

For red wine, based on what I have heard from producers using them, they require very complete settling before filling to avoid reduction.

They are very expensive and quite easy to break.
