I think the real question here is, is the American oak actually more obtrusive than it is in, say, Lopez de Heredia? I would speculate that it is not, and that your issues with the wine have to do with expectations and context rather than the wine itself. It’s perfectly natural to want Ridge Monte Bello to taste like claret, but there isn’t really any reason why it should.
