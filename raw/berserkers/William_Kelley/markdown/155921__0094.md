For sure. In instances where I have tasted a wine from barrel, sometimes multiple times, at the domaine, I have found the samples at London tastings quite unreliable by comparison. That’s no criticism of the merchants who do the best they can. And it’s not just the journey: one important Beaune négociant put on a big press tasting last year, with barrel samples that had been draw quite a while in advance, and by the time of the tasting, many were oxidized.
