The Domaine Leroy Aligoté (from vines in the commune of Puligny) is matured in tank, whereas the Domaine d’Auvenay Aligoté (from a cooler, stonier, sloping site in Auxey) is matured in new barrels. Interesting to see the difference you get from site + élevage with the same viticulture, sorting, pressing etc.
