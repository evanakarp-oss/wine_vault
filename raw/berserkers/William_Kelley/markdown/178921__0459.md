Exactly - 6% is fine for that sort of landowner. For a family where you have to pay an inheritance tax based on the market value of the land, and then, because of partitive inheritance, also buy out siblings, again at the market price, it is a very different manner.

The new proprietors may keep the former owners around for a generation or two (this is in fact already happening, as wealthy outsiders buy vineyards and give them in fermage to historic domaines). They will still work the land, but they will not own it. The era of the proprietor farming the vineyards has maybe 30 years’ life expectancy in my view.
