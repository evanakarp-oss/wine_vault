I’m a fan, and the new generation in the form of Robin Rapet seems promising.

1979 Corton-Charlemagne is a particular favorite of mine.

Note that the holdings in CC are in Pernand, so you should expect a finer-boned, less muscular, textural version of this grand cru than you get from Aloxe. That’s a feature, not a bug!
