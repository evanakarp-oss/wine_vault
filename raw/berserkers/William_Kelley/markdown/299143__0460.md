You will get no argument from me about the challenges of blind tasting.

To be clear, what I am criticizing is tasting blind and then publishing totally different scores *as if they were the results of a blind tasting* .

If, on top of that, a blind tasting raises a point of interrogation around a particular wine, such as in this case Pontet-Canet 2018, editing away the real results makes it more challenging for anyone else to criticize that wine, blind or not, because people like you, Juan, will say, “look, you’re a total outlier”.
