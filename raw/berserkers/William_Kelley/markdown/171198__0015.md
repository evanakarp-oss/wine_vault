> 2012 champagne seems okay but not incredible as far as i’m aware - compare 2008 cristal vs 2012, for example.

2012 is a really good vintage in Champagne! I actually tasted the 2012 and 2008 Cristal side by side last week, and having initially preferred the 2008 two years ago, I’m inclined to prefer the 2012 today. It’s a richer, more muscular vintage than 2008, with more complete maturity and more completely integrated acidity. The 2013 Cristal in many ways represents the best of both worlds (i.e. the improved viticulture that makes itself felt in 2012, combined with a more incisive, acid driven vintage) if you prefer racier, finer-boned vintages.

As for Dom Pérignon 2012, I didn’t taste it yet, but I would expect something quite powerful and dramatic more or less along the lines of their 2002. Let’s see!
