> Are there any “blue chip classic priced” early drinkers? I need some bottles that drink before 2045 The wa ui doest offer that filter

The quality of tannin in 2019 is such that a lot of wines are surprisingly polished and supple out of the gates (testament to how much progress has been made in managing extraction and élevage, as well as to the quality vintage). However, in this category I would look at things such as Haut Bages Libéral, La Lagune, du Tertre, Grandes Murailles and Grand Corbin Despagne.
