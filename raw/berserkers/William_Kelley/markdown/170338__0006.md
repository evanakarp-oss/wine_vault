> *Replying to :*  
> How was the vintage overall?

It is a very perfumed, supple, charming, elegant but ripe vintage, that is generally at its best today from cold cellars where it hasn’t moved for a long time. Perfectly conserved bottles are incredibly seductive and I have had a lot of great wines from this vintage. From before the 1956 frosts, which resulted in much replanting and a reconstitution of many Bordeaux vineyards (more productive selections, wider spacing for tractors etc etc), and also still the era before temperature control, with long élevage, multiple rackings and multiple egg white finings.
