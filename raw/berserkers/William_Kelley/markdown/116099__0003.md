I have encountered a lot of stewed, boderline oxidized bottles of the 1998 Vieux Telegraphe. The owner of a restaurant in London that I like told me he’d returned several cases for the same reason. So it may be the wine, not the bottle. A lot of the 1998 CdPs haven’t turned out that great. Beaucastel is a mess with 8g/L residual sugar.

The 2012 Cedric Bouchard wines are beautiful!

The Moreau Volnays are going from strength to strength, the oak being much better integrated in 2012 and 13.
