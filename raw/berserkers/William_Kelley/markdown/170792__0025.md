> However, my original point was more on how I’ve heard praise on 2011 from people who love fresh, cooler-vintage Bojo, whereas to me it has often come across as quite lush and solar.

Well this reflects to some extent a broader cultural shift in the wine world in the last decade, whereby in many cases the same consumers who didn’t buy e.g. 2007 Côte d’Or reds because they found them insufficiently ripe now eschew e.g. 2018 Côte d’Or reds on the grounds that they are too ripe.
