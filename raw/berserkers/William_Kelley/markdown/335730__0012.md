Historically, they have had very good Pinot genetics (like the Dugats, one of their ancestors worked in an important local nursery and selected good lineages, to the extent that locally some talk of “Pinot Magnien”). And indeed very good plots, including the only parcel of Cazetiers that runs from the top to the bottom of the climat. The famous 1955 Leroy Cazetiers came from them.
