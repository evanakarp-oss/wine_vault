This is the right answer! These sites were ancient quarries for stone. The parcel of Meursault Perrières belonging to Pierre and Anne Morey, for example, is where the shards of stone left over from quarrying were discarded. And here, in Nuits-Saint-Georges, you can see the only very recently retired quarry in Les Perrières: [Google Maps](https://www.google.fr/maps/@47.1275445,4.9397378,3a,75y,302.17h,80.9t/data=!3m7!1e1!3m5!1ss-OYLPj2X8-BW48ODY1nUg!2e0!6shttps:%2F%2Fstreetviewpixels-pa.googleapis.com%2Fv1%2Fthumbnail%3Fpanoid%3Ds-OYLPj2X8-BW48ODY1nUg%26cb_client%3Dmaps_sv.tactile.gps%26w%3D203%26h%3D100%26yaw%3D116.652794%26pitch%3D0%26thumbfov%3D100!7i13312!8i6656)
