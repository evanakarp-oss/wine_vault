Got numbers for my Friday and Saturday picks in Chambolle and Gevrey. Interestingly, very similar chemistry:

Chambolle Les Fouchères (29 Aug pick) - 13.3% potential, pH 3.31, 6 g/l tartaric 1.6 g/l malic Gevrey Aux Etelois (30 Aug pick) - 13.5% potential, pH 3.31, 6.1 g/l tartaric 1.8 g/l malic

These are both somewhat higher in pH that my Beaune picks this year of the 20th and 26th and I wonder if that is because of the little bit of rain we had. However, due to low malic at least the pHs will be comparatively stable. And, the flavors are “fresh”, which is more important than any analysis.

By contrast, in 2019 my Chambolle Fouchères was 13.6% potential, pH 3.20, 5.1 g/l tartaric and 3.2 g/l malic - so a quite a bit more malic acid than anything I have seen this year.
