I would venture that all the wines I rated very highly in 2022 have a strong personality.

I should clarify that a strong personality isn’t the same as an extreme style. Canon for example has a strong personality/identity, which is inextricable from its terroir (thin clay over limestone, a rather uniform exposition, on the plateau); but it is not an extreme style - in fact, it’s remarkably suave and harmonious. An estate such as Pape Clément has a more extreme style, but less of a personality/identity, as you can buy wines like that from a lot of different places.
