I think it’s a pity to generalize about an entire vintage like this. As I mentioned in another thread, Denis Bachelet’s Charmes-Chambertin is only 13.2% alcohol; most of Trapet’s are between 13 and 13.5%; the same is true of Dugat-Py and Claude Dugat; Charles Lachaux capped out at 13.2%. Just to give a few examples.
