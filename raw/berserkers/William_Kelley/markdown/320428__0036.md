Surprised I missed this in April, but I guess I was busy with Bordeaux en primeur…

The answer is 2020 for sure, though there are a few producers who did better in 2022 than in 2020 (e.g. Sauzet), I think for internal reasons rather than because of the vintage conditions per se. There are also some producers who turned out some really very good 2021s, that will appeal especially to those who look upon the white Burgundies of yesteryear with nostalgia: Lamy and J-C Bachelet are two examples that spring immediately to mind.
