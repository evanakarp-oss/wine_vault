Pensées isn’t really a second wine in the strict sense of the term either, coming from what was once an old watercourse that ran through the middle of the Lafleur vineyard. You can see it tracing through the property diagonally in aerial photos: [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/chateau+lafleur/@44.9315914,-0.1962618,568m/data=!3m1!1e3?entry=ttu) the soils are a bit more humid here and they are managed with cover crops for more of the year.
