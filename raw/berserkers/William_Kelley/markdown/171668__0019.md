Delys is a sous-climat of Corcelette, I think, in the way that Butteaux is a sous-climat of Montamains in Chablis for example.

As for Bouland’s parcel, it’s right behind his new winery and home (which are still under construction in the photo on google maps) here: [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/@46.1683579,4.6730662,3a,75y,232.42h,89.36t/data=!3m7!1e1!3m5!1sZHM7qtaBpfJi8SaHN8h5nA!2e0!6shttps:%2F%2Fstreetviewpixels-pa.googleapis.com%2Fv1%2Fthumbnail%3Fpanoid%3DZHM7qtaBpfJi8SaHN8h5nA%26cb_client%3Dmaps_sv.tactile.gps%26w%3D203%26h%3D100%26yaw%3D60.91035%26pitch%3D0%26thumbfov%3D100!7i13312!8i6656)
