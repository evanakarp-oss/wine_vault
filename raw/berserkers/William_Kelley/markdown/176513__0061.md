> Quite a few newer names come to mind from the 2020 report including Jean Marc Vincent Well, it could be that the wines of Anne-Marie and Jean-Marc Vincent have not been discussed much in publications such as the Wine Advocate, but the extraordinary quality of the wines has been discussed here for…

I’ve been writing about them since 2015, when I started reviewing Burgundy for *Decanter* , but it took a bit of time for people to listen. I did a big feature article and vertical on Jean-Marc last year, which I hope kicked things up a notch. Already, the dividends are being plowed back into the winery: JM is extending élevage longer, building new facilities to have some more space, and making more high density plantings. The reality is that the wines were underpriced for many years, given the work behind them and the quality in the bottle.
