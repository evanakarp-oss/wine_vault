Don’t want to get too deep, but I would say this:

- in the vineyard, you can make the wine better
- in the winery, beyond giving it a stylistic signature, you can only make it less bad

That’s to say, if you want to increase your inherent potential, you should focus on the vineyard. If you want to realize more of it, you should focus on the winery. Of course, it is advisable from a quality perspective to focus on both.
