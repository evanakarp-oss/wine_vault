I mean, it’s hard to say without tasting the same bottle, and I’ve only ever had very good experiences with Ultramarine (I finally got an allocation this year and am quite excited about it). A priori, a sort of asparagus-like aroma can indeed come from a combination of marginal/arrested maturity (i.e. ripeness) plus reduction; but trying to extrapolate from others’ tastings notes to what they were tasting is at best a fools’ errand, especially when the issue could be a defective bottle/cork rather than any kind of systematic one.
