I do take your point re. consistency across regions. Of course, there is also a somewhat philosophical dimension (what is comparable) and a practical dimension (different palates). But it is something we are looking at internally. If you want to discuss further I’d be happy to exchange some DMs.

As for the ranges, all I can say is that they are applied thoughtfully. You will note that in some cases the range is only two points, in others it increases to four. This isn’t about trying to “fudge” a judgement, it just reflects the reality of the exercise of tasting barrel samples. I venture that in this regard my production experience, such as it is, makes me especially aware of when samples may not permit as clear a read of the underlying wine as one might wish for.
