Too bad about your bottle of the Lynch! I opened one during harvest and it was one of the best Bordeaux I drank all year: still an infant, immensely concentrated, and so vibrant. For me, probably the best wine of the Médoc in 1989.

1995 Pontet Canet around the same period I found rather blocky and simple, but perfectly nice.
