I certainly think they will be approachable young (in instances where the grapes were tolerably ripe and extraction gentle), even for some of the more naturally powerful wines of the vintage such as Carmes and Pontet Canet. Obviously they won’t be showing tertiary characteristics, but you should have a lot of aromatic range and complexity given the profile of the wines.
