It really changes from year to year, with Guffens following the same general principles but adapting to the vintage. Premiers Jus des Hauts des Vignes 2022 includes the Croux, which wasn’t classified as premier cru because it’s not south-facing (I suspect as much as anything because the INAO didn’t want to set any precedents), as well as Guffens’ last harvest of La Roche. Reviews will be coming out before too long, and as ever Guffens’ viticulture and pressing approach has delivered wines with full ripeness but very low pH. For him, 2022 isn’t quite on the level of 2020, but it’s a very good vintage.
