The 1985 Bonneau du Martray Corton Charlemagne is excellent.

The first 1985 red Burgundy I ever tasted was a 1985 Clos de la Roche Jeremy opened on my first visit to Domaine Dujac—and to Burgundy!

The 1985 Bordeaux are really lovely. Arguably the best Léoville Barton and -Las Cases of the decade for example. Reports of their decline are premature.
