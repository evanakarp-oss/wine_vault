People sometimes say that CSJ would be better off all planted to red, and it is indeed a superb vineyard for Pinot Noir; but the northern part that touches Chaumées and Vergers, where Niellon’s plot is located, seems to do just fine in white.

I thought the 2017 CSJ white turned out especially well and bought some bottles for my cellar!
