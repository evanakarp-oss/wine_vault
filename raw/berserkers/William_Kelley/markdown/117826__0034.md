Cru Beaujolais (while some are creeping up in price producers like Bouland and Clos Roilette are making seriously age-worthy wines for a tiny tarrif) Muscadet (from a handful of producers) A few northern Rhone producers (e.g. Levet) Loire (pretty much everyone except Clos Rougeard and Joly’s wines) Topped-up Jura Chardonnay (Ganevat and Tissot still a lot cheaper than Cote d’Or alternatives) Chablis (at least in the UK allocation prices wines like Raveneau and Dauvissat are really cheap) Plenty of addresses in the Southern Rhone and Provence Lots of things in Piemonte Pepe and Valentini in Abruzzo Pretty much any top German wine

Those are some of the obvious choices…
