What is somewhat perplexing about all this is that Constellation doesn’t actually seem to have any aspirations to dilute the “brand value” To Kalon. All the branding materials of To Kalon Wine Company and the use of “To Kalon Vineyard” on the Mondavi labels leans into the notion of terroir and To Kalon being a special place—indeed, to quote their own materials, a “Grand Cru vineyard”. Yet Constellation are simultaneously opposing the name of the creek that runs through this same vineyard (and which indeed corresponds with the watercourse that actually created the alluvial fan that corresponds pretty precisely with the historical footprint of the To Kalon vineyard). Bizarre, and regrettable.
