> Just had it last week. I don’t see an issue opening it now. Way better than 2002 regular. Rep told me they sometimes do different dosage things with the different releases.

To me, the divergence between 2002 P1 (as we may call the original disgorgement for clarity) and 2002 P2 are not very pronounced when you have them side by side, which I have done a number of times. The differences, as far as we are told, are triage under cork and somewhat lower dosage for P2, and there is less than a decade separating P1 and P2 in the 2002 vintage.
