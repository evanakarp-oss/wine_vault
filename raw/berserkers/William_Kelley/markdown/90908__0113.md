> Burgaud’s 2009 Javernières is beginning to drink beautifully! Also surprised by this; usually takes more years to come 'round, in my experience.

Drank four or five in the last six months, they are showing beautifully but will hold for a decade without any problems. Bottles that have travelled in the USA and seen some warmth show some brett, FYI (downside to using used DRC barrels ) so hopefully you have kept yours cool.
