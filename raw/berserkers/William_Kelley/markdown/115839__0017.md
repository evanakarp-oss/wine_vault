I think we’re talking at crossed purposes. Rustic is a description of the style of the wine, not its tannins or evolution. In the same way that a Nuits St George Les Pruliers is more rustic than a Vosne Romanee Aux Reignots, or a Gruaud Larose is more rustic than a Pichon Lalande, or a 1982 Bordeaux is more rustic than a 1985, or Pegau is more rustic than Rayas, or whatever.
