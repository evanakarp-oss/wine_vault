Such sad news. I was really looking forward to seeing him again. I had lunch with him in London as a student and vividly remember it. He insisted I consume a Christmas pudding and a half-bottle of Sauternes, on the ground that while his doctor said he couldn’t, I should. He also recounted how his new doctor, in the presence of his wife, had once inquired how much he drank. Feeling compelled to answer honestly, he confessed to “perhaps a bottle a day, between lunch and dinner”. The doctor asked how much a bottle was, and when he said 750 ml, insisted that it was far too little: Anthony needed to be drinking a lot more water than that!
