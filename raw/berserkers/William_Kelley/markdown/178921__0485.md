Donald, no hard feelings, but I simply don’t agree. My predecessor at TWA only reviewed the wines of the Chalonnaise once, in 2014, and hasn’t done very much with them at his new address. If you consider one day spent in the Côte Chalonnaise in 2022 and a few samples tasted in London cutting edge coverage, then perhaps you haven’t yet mastered the TWA search engine. Forgive me if I sound indignant, but as the first to write about the likes of Maxime Cottenceau, Raphaël Corcia and to all intents and purposes Bruno Lorenzon in English, I do resent the notion that I am playing catch up. Since joining TWA I have reviewed 795 wines from the Côte Chalonnaise and made coverage a feature of my work, I’d love to know of anyone who has done more.
