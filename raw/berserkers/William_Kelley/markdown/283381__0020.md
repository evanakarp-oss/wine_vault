In short: taut but textural, with an incisive, chiseled profile structured by chalky extract.

Sébastian uses a Vaslin mechanical press, and presses long and hard, which extracts from the skins. The wines then spend a year in barrel and a year in tank, picking up texture from the lees, before they are bottled by hand.
