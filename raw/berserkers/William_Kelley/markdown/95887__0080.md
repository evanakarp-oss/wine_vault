Kind of you to say! I tasted some of the 2015s from bottle in the US, not at the domaine, so I don’t have too much context. I liked the 2015 Beaux Monts that you bought a lot, and it sounds as if Robert agreed with that assessment: I hope it lives up to the review when you try a bottle. The Echézeaux, when I tasted it, was also impressive; but with more puppy fat and mid-palate flesh than the Beaux Monts, so I can see why someone (i.e. Robert) might have liked it less. I didn’t taste the Grands Echezeaux. This estate has lots of old vine holdings in top appellations but also a reputation for being a little inconsistent. At some point I’d like to visit but there are so many domaines in Burgundy, as you know, and only so much time…
