> *Replying to :*  
> Another question to which you might know the answer. To whom is the reference to Auguste on the 2002 Lignier CdlR (and only on that CdlR, as far as I can see)? Cheers, Howard

Auguste is one of the late Romain Lignier’s sons. At that period, Romain Lignier (son of Hubert as you probably know) was making the Hubert Lignier wines.
