> *Replying to :*  
> does anyone have any updates on the frost damage and how its actually impacting things?

I think the assessments back in April/May were quite accurate: lots of damage to Chardonnay, especially the best sites on the slope; Pinot Noir in general not so impacted, though some areas hit hard (parts of Chambolle, northern Gevrey for example). Since then, there has been a bit of nasty hail, which impacted e.g. northern Gevrey. Flowering is underway now in very warm conditions, with storms coming apparently.
