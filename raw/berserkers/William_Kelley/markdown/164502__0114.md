Drank a 1998 last night, and I do prefer the 1996 and 2000 by an appreciable margin. Of course, bottle variation isn’t unheard with this estate, but the 1998 doesn’t have the same depth and is also a touch marked by chaptalization to my palate. Since you’re a Loire lover, Alfert, a touch of CF herbaceousness may be pulling your heartstrings, but I am not sure the 1998 is really the Médoc purist’s choice.
