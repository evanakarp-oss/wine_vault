That wasn’t really the point I was making. I was trying to highlight how native yeast winemaking is dependent on selecting, based on our preferences, only one or two of the benign microbes present among hundreds of undesirable selections that constitute the fruit’s native microflora. The notion that the identity of a vineyard resides in its grape skin microbiome is a little at odds with the fact that so much of it is capable of being detrimental to wine quality and indeed the expression of site, since flaws/faults are homogenizing.
