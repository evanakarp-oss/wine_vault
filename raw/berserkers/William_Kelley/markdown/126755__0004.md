The 2001 has begun to get tertiary and complex (and it’s been a year since I had it), whereas the 2006 is still pretty boisterous and exuberantly fruity; more powerful too, though the 01 is not a weed either.

Either way, I strongly recommend following the Reynaud opening protocol: pull the cork now, pour off a small glass, replace the cork and return to the cellar until dinner time.
