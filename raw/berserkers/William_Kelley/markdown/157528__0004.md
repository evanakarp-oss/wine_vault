> *Replying to :*  
> I bought a fair bit of Pattes Loup in the late noughties. Can’t remember exact vintage. They were lean and very tasty on release, but they did not last. Advanced quite rapidly. Drink them young, but gotta love '17 vintage Chablis.

Thomas started buying much better corks around 2012, and his '14s, for example, are evolving more slowly. Of course, he has also taken some quite unconventional (for Chablis) directions regarding very long élevage. His '14s, '15s and '17s have been especially strong.
