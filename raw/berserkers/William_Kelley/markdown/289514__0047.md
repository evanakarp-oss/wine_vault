2021 is going to be a great test! The best 2021s are really beautiful. Hyper perfumed, melting tannins, low alcohol, not much in the way of weight but very good persistence. The perfect antidote to the big years we have been having. Despite the trauma of the growing season I would actually sign up to make the vintage again, if I could, though I doubt it would win me many friends.
