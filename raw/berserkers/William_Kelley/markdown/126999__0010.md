2014 was a real success here: a vintage which synergized totally with the kind of wines Jacques likes to make. I tasted a few last month after working through the '15s and was really impressed.

And entirely agree with Jeremy that this is a strong contender for the best village Puligny to be found. Vicent Dureuil-Janthial’s Corvée des Vignes bottling is also up there for me in the last few vintages. Lamy’s Haute Densité Puligny ‘Les Tremblots’ might surpass them all however.
