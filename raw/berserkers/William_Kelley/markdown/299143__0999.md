The 1982 is pH 3.62, whereas the 2022 is pH 3.80, so there is indeed a big difference; yet today’s more reductive winemaking and élevage, and the wine’s tannic structure, mean that the high pH imparts more a sense of breadth and plenitude to the 2022 than it does any notion of ponderousness or lack of tension.
