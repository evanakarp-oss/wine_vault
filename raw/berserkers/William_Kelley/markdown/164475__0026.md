I assumed it was a trick question

In fact, I don’t think there is any relation, certainly not a close one, though I may be misremembering.

Jean-François Germain makes the Henri Germain wines, and his brother Eric has been brining a lot of Germain magic to the Vincent Girardin wines. It’s just hard to get people to put their preconceptions about that label aside and taste the wines for what they are. Eric is very well respected along the Côte for a reason!
