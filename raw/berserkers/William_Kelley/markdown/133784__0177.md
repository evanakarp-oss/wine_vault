> *Replying to :*  
> johngonzales: . . . having the To Kalon fruit . . . Enough already, and not meant as a criticism of John, who may not have read the rest of this thread, BUT THERE IS NO SUCH THING AS TOKALON FRUIT. ToKalon is a trademark that can be used by Constellation and it is not tied to a particular place. If…

While this is legally true at present, it is contested. After all, the Schrader-Beckstoffer-Mondavi lawsuit was settled out of court. And the outcome was that Beckstoffer and his clients were allowed to use the To Kalon name - hardly a victory for Mondavi or an endorsement of their trademark. So I would argue that the debate over whether To Kalon is a brand or a place is very far from being definitively settled in favor of the former. What would the outcome of another lawsuit be?
