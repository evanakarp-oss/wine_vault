> William, Great insights - thanks for sharing. Any idea of how often the wines are racked during elevage? I wonder if perhaps they are increasing that number to make the wines more ‘user friendly’ early? Just a thought . . . Cheers.

Mike writes:

“We have always racked every 6 months and the most current vintage gets racked after ML completion so three times the first year.  We clear out the entry hall after bottling by racking the current vintage back into a tunnel lane….”

He also adds that greater oxygen uptake from new barrels is probably helping to soften the vintage a bit.
