“Dry” being pH 3.0 with 5 grams per liter RS? The definition of “dry” for GGs is what, <10g/l?  It’s a different world of wine, and a different stylistic objective, too. But I agree that you can make a pneumatic press work pretty much like a basket press. The advantage and disadvantage of the pneumatic press is that it gives you more options, making it much easier to make mistakes.
