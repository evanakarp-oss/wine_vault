> *Replying to :*  
> Haven’t had them, but I’ve read and heard anecdotally that Clos des Lambrays is often a bit of a miss.

The old Clos des Lambrays wines of the 1910s, 1920s, 1930s and 1940s are about as good as Burgundy gets! Honestly, these debates often end up being rather arid, as anywhere on the Côte d’Or is quite an exciting place to produce wine, if the sites are farmed at the level that they deserve. Les Saint Georges will be valorized by great viticulture and winemaking, not by writing “Grand Cru” on the label.
