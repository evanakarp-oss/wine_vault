> Absolutely true. A young Ramey or Aubert, not to mention Rhys chardonnay is pretty damn good. Would be fun to do another Judgment of Paris today with some GC Burgs lined up against the best of California. Who is up to the challenge? Count me in, but it would be very easy to tell which one was the…

Side by side, they taste fatter, richer, more alcoholic. Analytically, a lot of GC white Burgundies have similar alcohols and see just as much new oak. But, side by side, my experience has been that it isn’t hard to tell which is which. I try to do this sort of thing from time to time as it “keeps one honest”, but it has been a few years, perhaps it’s time to do it again.
