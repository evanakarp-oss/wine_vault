Copper sulfate, hydroxide etc, as used by biodynamic growers, are indeed chemical

What you mean is that it wouldn’t have been a synthetic systemic product.

Being organic or biodynamic doesn’t mean that you don’t spray. It means you spray more often, using only a small range of contact products that need to be reapplied after around 20-25mm of rainfall. That can easily add up to 20 successive treatments and over 5kg of copper per hectare.

I think it’s important that people understand this.
