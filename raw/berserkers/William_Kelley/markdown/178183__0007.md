> Have you tried the 2019 of this yet? If so, I’d be interested to hear your thoughts. Thanks!

The 2019 is very good! Concentrated and chiseled. Best to wait as long as you can bear to open it, and drink '18 and '20 before at this address, as 2019 has the structure to age. It’s a blend of Narvaux, Grands Charrons and Vireuils, so pretty prime origins.
