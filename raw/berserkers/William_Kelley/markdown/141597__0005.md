> I had this wine at about age 4y … it contains also the declassified 1er crus. I wasn´t impressed then, actually quite disapointed … too green, too short, too devoid of ripe fruit, quite browning … If it is now (only) a “lovely drink” … I think that´s still not enough for the half fortune a bottle…

I think the hype generated by the various communal bottlings also containing the declassified higher appellations got a little carried away. In the end, the wines are very tasty, but one can see why Lalou chose to declassify. I was last served one of them blind a couple of years ago, and mortified the generous friend who’d brought it be calling it as a very nice Oregon Pinot Noir.
