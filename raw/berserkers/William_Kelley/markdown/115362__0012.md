For what it’s worth, I’ve enjoyed the G Rinaldi Brunate quite a bit. We had a bottle with an 8-hour decant or so back in June, which took on quite a dark and balsamic fruit profile after all that air. I could imagine it bearing some similarity to the 2000 in due course.

I also quite liked the Burlotto Cannubi, though the beautifully aromatic and singular Monvigliero is where I put my money.
