> Are there any prior vintages of CdC that the 2008 reminds you of?

Not obviously. In terms of depth and concentration it has a lot in common with the 2002, but it’s more chiseled and incisive and tightly wound. I would say that the only thing more exciting than drinking the wine today is thinking about how it will taste in a decade.
