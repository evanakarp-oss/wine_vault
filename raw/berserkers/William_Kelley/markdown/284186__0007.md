Kevin is right! It’s probably my all-time favorite vintage of this bottling, and I was a little surprised to see you found it wanting in complexity. Togni availed themselves of the “Table Wine” tag in labelling so I don’t think the ABV is listed on the label, but I would have thought it’s more in the region of 13-13.5% than 12.3%. I think I have one bottle left. It’s probably the only bottle in Beaune.
