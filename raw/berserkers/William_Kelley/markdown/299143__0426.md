Hi Juergen,

There’s a lot of talk of “best ever”, but those are not terms I have been bandying around, so please don’t hold me to account for other critics’ language; I have enough to keep track of defending my own! When I make a claim about where a wine can be situated in a château’s history, I attempt to give it some context. For example, when I talk about 2022 Canon, I observe that this is “an estate that is delivering its greatest run of vintages since the superb Post War series that preceded the frosts of 1956”.

Regarding the two examples you cite, however, I do believe that the 2022 Latour has the potential to match at least the 1982 - we will see. 2022 Haut-Brion I don’t see keeping company with '61 or '89.

All best,

William.
