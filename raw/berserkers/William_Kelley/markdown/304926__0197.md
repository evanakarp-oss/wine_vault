Don’t get ahead of yourself

There’s going to be a lot of commentary on the vintage in a matter of weeks. I ran into James Suckling in Saint-Émilion yesterday, and he will be publishing some thoughts on the Médoc in a few days’ time. In France, Quarin has already started writing about the vintage. Others will follow suite in short order.

So defer judgement until you have more data, would be my recommendation!
