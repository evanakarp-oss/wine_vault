Vincent is devoting increasing attention to his reds these days. The Pougets is typically a bit finer-boned than the heartier, more powerful Corton which is generally served after it and which comes from Les Meix and Les Chaumes (just above Aloxe). The 2015s were very successful and would be a good place to start. I don’t think either are quite in the same league as the Jadot Pougets, but that’s typically one of their best wines so the bar is set high.
