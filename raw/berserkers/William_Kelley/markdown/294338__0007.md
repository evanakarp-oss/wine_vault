They’re run independently by the very serious Audrey Braccini and are one of the jewels in Jadot’s crown these days for me. The last handful of vintages have been among the best of the Mâconnais, achieving tension and cut even in sites that ripen early, in very ripe vintages. The top bottlings are quite age worthy and do tend to shut down, but patience pays dividends.
