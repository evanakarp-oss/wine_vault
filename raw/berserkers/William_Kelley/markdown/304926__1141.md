The track record of LB starting out like that and becoming something compelling, like the 1989 and 1990 for example, is strong, and the consistency they achieve combined with considerable volume from vintage to vintage is impressive. The extraction and chunkiness isn’t accompanied by overripeness. Is it a somewhat old fashioned styled wine? Sure. But age 25 in a blind tasting of its peers it tends to do very well.
