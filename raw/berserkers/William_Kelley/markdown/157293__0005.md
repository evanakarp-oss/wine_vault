> *Replying to :*  
> Nice note. Soon this cuvée will get a big bigger when some vines that are in fermage revert to her. Just writing up my tasting of her 2018s, coincidentally! There was also a 2017 Cecile Tremblay Bourgogne Côte d’Or on the winelist! A new cuvée?

I can answer that! This used to be the Bourgogne cuvée “Croix Blanche”, which came from the lieu dit of that name in Vosne-Romanée (one of the superior regional spots in the commune). But Cécile then got some Bourgogne in the commune of Chambolle-Musigny, so the name didn’t make sense. Her partner Philippe Charlopin was also one of the cheerleaders for the new “Bourgogne Côte d’Or” AOC as I understand it.
