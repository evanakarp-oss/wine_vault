I think the Eternam system that Stas mentions they used is one whereby everything occurs in a nitrogen atmosphere - including topping up with a bottle of the same wine. This was recently done at Clos des Lambrays for all their library wines and it doesn’t appear to have fatigued the wines, even the very old ones, in the least. I am generally a bit skeptical about reconditioning but this system seems really serious and quite a few estates are using it.
