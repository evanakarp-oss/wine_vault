I’m just writing up my notes on the 2015 Bachelet-Monnot wines, including their Fussière; which, if I’m correct, comes from the same plot which produced this wine (Bernard Bachelet was their grand-father and their father turned over some vines to them when the sons set up Bachelet-Monnot).

The 15 is lovely, and the Clos de la Boutière bottling even a touch better. Very, very smart purchases in 2015.
