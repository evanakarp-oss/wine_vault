Where would that end?

The one time someone ever complained about a commentary in Burgundy was when I wrote something to the effect that, “this is an exceptional Bourgogne Blanc, but is it worth the three-digit price tag?”

The producer in question asked why I hadn’t written that about all the other Bourgogne Blanc and Rouge bottlings that command a three-digit price tag, such as Coche and Roumier. And I felt that he had a very valid point.

I think consumers are intelligent and informed enough to look at scores in the context of prices and make their own buying decisions that fit their criteria. Our job is to keep them maximally informed about the wines’ quality!
