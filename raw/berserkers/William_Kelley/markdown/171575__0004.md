2018 is maybe not the vintage to dip toes in communal appellation négociant wines, given the yields, but I wouldn’t write off the producer wholesale: anything labelled “récolte du Domaine”, especially the ex-Domaine Leflaive holdings, has been excellent for a while; to the extent that, rather than feeling that they’re capitalizing on the name, the results of a blind tasting might be in doubt.
