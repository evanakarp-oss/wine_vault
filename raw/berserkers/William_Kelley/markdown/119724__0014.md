Carlos, Bob:

Try the 2005 Pape Clement. It’s the classic example of a wine that was pumped up in the cellar and then deflated in bottle; the journey from rich and textural to obviously oaky, spirituous and diffuse took only ten years.

Since circa 2000 Pape Clement has become an entirely different wine, but it’s a great property.
