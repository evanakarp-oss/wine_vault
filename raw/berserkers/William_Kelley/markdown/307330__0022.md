The 1970 is very good, but one of the most delicate I have had. I had a few bottles and the first I regretted decanting. Of course the last was corked.

There is clearly a difference between lots / late release from the estate vs early release.

09 and 10 are the two vintages I really stocked up on, 48 bottles of each for the cellar, we opened one of each with the interns during harvest and while 10 probably has the edge I’m very happy to have both.
