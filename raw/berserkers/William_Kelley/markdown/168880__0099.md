Some interesting responses here!

I like everything my wife does, but my tastes also range more widely. We both love old Bordeaux, old (but very much alive) Champagne, old Rioja, mature Bruno Giacosa, Soldera, colheita ports etc. But she’s quite rotundone-sensitive and struggles with more marginally ripe expressions of Syrah, so for her the northern Rhône begins and ends at Cornas. While she enjoys old white Burgundy, it wouldn’t be her desert island wine. And so on. But, we me thanks to wine, and the first thing I said to her was, “are you interested in wine”. Full disclosure from the beginning, in other words.
