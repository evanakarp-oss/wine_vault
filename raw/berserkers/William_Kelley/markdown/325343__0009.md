I don’t think the tone comes over that way in French.

The trends regarding agrochemicals, clones and yields really started to bite in the 1970s, but they took time to do their work, and of course some producers bucked the trend on some or all counts, for varying amounts of time. Clones, for example, came in in 1971, so you didn’t see clonal vineyards producing wine until later in the decade…
