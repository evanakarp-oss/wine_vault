Well, I don’t think any of the wines on my list are “big bruisers”, either, they are just all quite serious, intensely flavored wines that are capable of being an event as well as a beverage. Much as I like things like Pepière Clos des Briords or Aviet’s Arbois reds, I’m not going to trade out Coche and Mugneret-Gibourg in their favor. But I’m sure happy to have them in my real cellar.
