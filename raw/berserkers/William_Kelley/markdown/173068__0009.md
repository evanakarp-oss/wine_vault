I actually already visited, Herwig! And I agree with you. Vineyards are all organic certified (they were rented to someone who worked organically and Benoit continued). Winemaking is simple but attentive: destemmed, classic macerations in concrete tanks, basket press, 18 months in barrel. 2019 is the first real vintage (Benoit made a few barrels for the preceding decade which he mainly sold at wine fairs), and a very strong start as you say.
