> William, but do you think almost 1.000 bucks is justified for a bottle of DRC GE or any wine if you will? At least if you compare this tariff to other fine Burgundy?

All I can say is that, if spending the money was simply not an issue for me, I would spend it quite happily on the 13 GE but refrain from spending it on the 2014 Echezeaux (which I found a bit disappointing - would rather have Cécile Trembaly’s, the Mugneret sisters’, or Gilbert Felettig’s, for example). That is ultimately an emotional response, certainly, but I am not sure purchasing fine wine is really susceptible to a rationalist cost-benefit analysis.
