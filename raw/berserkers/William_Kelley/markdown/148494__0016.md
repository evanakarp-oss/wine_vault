Not liking the wine is fine, but since it fermented in a tank with minimal punching down and pumping over and then matured in barrel without racking until bottling, it is hard to see how it could be described as “over worked”.

But I agree that the pricing of Bourgogne from all the top domaines of the Côte de Nuits on the marketplace today is a bit hard to justify—especially vis a vis the best wines of the Côte Chalonnaise.
