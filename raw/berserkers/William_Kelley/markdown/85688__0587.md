Just finishing an 89 Grand Corbin Despagne tonight with pot au feu… a really nice wine, from sand over blue clay on the Pomerol border. The excesses of the 90s/00s passed them by, but now they have brought down yields, refocussed on farming, and are producing terrific stuff. I bought two cases of the 2019 and am very happy to have them.
