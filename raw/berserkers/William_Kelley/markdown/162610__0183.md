> I would like to see a show of hands who besides Willliam knows what the word empyreumatic means, much less has used it to describe a wine!

I think this term entered the Anglophone wine vocabulary, to the extent that it has, via the English translation of Émile Peynaud’s Le Goût du Vin, you’ll find it in some of RP’s early books as well as elsewhere. But, I don’t really use it any more precisely because, quite reasonably, no one knows what it means…
