> *Replying to :*  
> I love the 1990 Cuvée DD. Any thoughts/opinions on their burgundies?

Terrific! They have 1er Cru Chouacheux and the lieu-dit Pierres Blanches which is on the slope above Grèves and commonly planted to white, but the same geology and altitude pretty much as Corton Clos du Roi.

But not much is made, so not easy to find, and 2001 is the latest release. In 2000 the PB is deeper and more complete, as Chouacheux was hailed, but in 2001 the Chouacheux is somewhat richer and more gourmand than the more ethereal PB, so the difference is more style than quality.
