> Interesting story: my colleague also managed to set up an appointment at Rayas (don’t ask me how) around noon today - he called yesterday to confirm, but of course Mr Reynaud had forgotten about it. But it was ok, and they can even stay for lunch, but… need to bring the lunch themselves for them…

Haha that works! The first time I went, with a few friends from my student tasting group, we arrived early and waited outside. At the hour of our appointment, an upstairs window opened and a head with a flat cap on briefly looked out. 15 minutes later, it looked out again, and then Reynaud must have decided we had passed the first test and came down to open the door. His line was “ah, les débutants d’Oxford sont arrivés”.
