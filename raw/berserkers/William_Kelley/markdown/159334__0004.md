Bachelet’s 2017s turned out brilliantly and will offer broad drinking windows, warmly recommended. The Cathiard 2017s are similarly exquisite and in the context of contemporary pricing for that domaine’s wines, why not. And the Hudelot-Noëllat 2016s are great, adapted to extended cellaring if you have the patience for it.
