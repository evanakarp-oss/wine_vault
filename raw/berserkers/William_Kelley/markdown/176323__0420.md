> Finally the pendulum has swung. The age of “over extracted” is finally over… and I am glad

Bordeaux has changed a lot in the last decade, and today, what I like to call the “shock and awe” style of winemaking is really limited to a few holdouts who remain attached to the style of the early 2000s. Frankly, I suspect that in many instances these estates’ winemaking teams would like to pull back too, but that it’s the proprietors who are averse to change. Of course, the humbling takeaway is how quickly one goes from being a revolutionary to being a reactionary in the wine world.
