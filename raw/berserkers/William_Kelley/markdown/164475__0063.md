> *Replying to :*  
> I just had Drouhin’s 2019 “Côte de Beaune” as opposed to the bottling that includes villages. The front label has the same script used on Mouches. Very solid wine at its price point.

Yes, the 2019 turned out especially well. IIRC it also contains declassified young vine 1er cru fruit.

The AOC “Côte de Beaune” itself is very interesting, as there are some top sites such as Pierres Blanches that are not at all know. Sadly, as a result, the viticulture is not always on the level that it would need to be to deliver greatness, but the soils and exposition are immensely promising.
