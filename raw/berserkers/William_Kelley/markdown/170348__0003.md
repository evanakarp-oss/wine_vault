> *Replying to :*  
> Any recommendations for a beaujolais made with no carbonic maceration?

The question has been somewhat confused by the fact that in the Beaujolais folks have for some time taken to calling traditional whole bunch vinification with crushing “semi-carbonic maceration”.

In addition to Bouland, I would recommend Louis-Claude Desvignes, Château Thivin, Jean-Paul Brun, Chermette, Jean-Marc Burgaud - and plenty of others.
