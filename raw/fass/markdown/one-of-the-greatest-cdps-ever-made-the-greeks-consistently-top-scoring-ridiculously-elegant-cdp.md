#### One of the Greatest CDPs Ever Made

Back in the day, I used to drink a lot of CDP. It was largely affordable and generally interesting and quite good. Then Parker and global warming took over and most of it turned into overpriced, overly alcoholic jammy swill. So I moved on (mostly to the wines of the Northern Rhone).

But there are still a small number of excellent wines made in CDP and none better than today’s offer. I have a small allocation but anyone who wants to time travel a bit and enjoy the great CDP of yesteryear should give this offer a read.

**The Greek** We all know the Greek, who runs the top wine store in the Rhone and who has been the first retailer to sell pretty much every top winery in the region. We call him the King of the Rhone. He knows all of the best plots in the entire region and makes a series of stunning wines. This CDP is his magnum opus form the South.

**The Wine**

This is perhaps the hardest to get and I’m thrilled to sell the **2021 Compagnie L’Hermitage Chateauneuf-du-Pape “lieu dit Pignan”** for as little as **$89.99** bottle on a 3-pack. This is 100% old vine Grenache (80-100 years) and is one of the greatest Chateauneuf-du-Papes I have ever tasted. In 2021, George says it is better than Rayas and the best pure Grenache made in all of the Rhone. The 2019 is 99 points Jeb Dunnuck. It is such a **deep, stoney and extraordinarily complex wine** . My favorite CDP year in and year out.

**Nose** is **just gorgeous herbs. So complex and so detailed.** **Gorgeous plum and berry fruit** . So **intense** yet **refined** and delicate. Heavenly nose. 9.8. Just stunning. The nose is pungent and aromatic with a **raspberry** **coulis, complex spice** and so much **stoney minerality** . Really a wine that you can sit with over hours and just watch (smell) it evolve). Wow – what a nose. Truly epic and wild yet refined at the same time.

On the **palate** , the **fruit and freshness are once in a generation** type stuff. Deep, **palate staining fruit** and **astonishing balance.** It goes on for minutes and after proper aeration a perfume like you cannot believe develops. There is also **mind-boggling inner mouth perfume** . Darker fruits than more red. Palate is **juicy, complex** and so, so, so **stoney** .  And this wine is so stoney, which I will repeat often. I can only imagine the source of the vineyard and grapes given the name of the wine.  Amazing sweetness, structure and tannins. Long. Elegant. Wonderful.This is a CDP in a pure style with astonishing depth and layers of fruit allpresented on slabs of rock.

For my money, this is as good as many of the more high priced deluxe cuvees out there.
