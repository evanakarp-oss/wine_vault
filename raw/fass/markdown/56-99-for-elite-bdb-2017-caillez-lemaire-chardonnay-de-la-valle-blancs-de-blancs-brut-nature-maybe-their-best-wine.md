Virginie was “hoping” I would like it when she wrote she was sending me a sample. I wish I could be even as half as humble as she is!

Wow. That palate has flavor! Flava in my ear as the acid gets all the way up to my ear. Amazing sap and power. Rich and energetic and gets into every nook and cranny of the palate. So, so salty this is brilliant like a bubbling Puligny Montrachet. Iodine coming out now. There is such fine mousse and finesse . Wow this is so so pure. A perfect representation of chardonnay of the Valle de Marne. So sappy. Concentrated and so pure. Almost painfully intense. Wow, huge spice on the finish. So fresh and precise and wow is this long. What palate presence, and the palate develops a mineral sweetness that is stunning. Wow. This is sick. The palate presence of great BDB cannot be denied. A masterpiece. This has 10-12 years of age ahead of it. There is top flight finesse and elegance and purity you would need to pay $100+ for in the 3-tier system.
