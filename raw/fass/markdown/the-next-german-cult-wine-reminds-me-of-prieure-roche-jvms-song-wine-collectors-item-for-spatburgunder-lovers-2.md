**As I look at the different stylistic approaches among our producers who make single vineyard, Premier Cru / Grand Cru Style wines, I can think of 4 broad groups.** 1.Buy And Hold . Christoph Walter is                    Fassproducers, Furst is another.                  These producers have elite terroir              and make these wines to age with              terrific structure. Typically you                      want   to wait 10 years for them                  but  when you do open them, they              are incredible, dense yet                              balanced masterpieces. 2.Whimsical Magic. The poster child for this style is obviously Enderle & Moll. Their allocated single soil              wines are works of utter random brilliance. Almost impossible to describe and as unique Pinot                    Noirs as exist on earth. Marbelous could fit in here as well. 3 . Teutonic Marching Band . By this description I mean that the winemaker takes the terroir and uses                  elite level technique to produce a wine of precision and density that can be drunk young.                            Ziereisen is obviously the leader of this style with his Jaspis wines. Thorle is right there behind                  Hanspeter Ziereisen. Also our new producer Dr. Wehrheim is of that group. 4 . Burgundy in Germany . This style is what it sounds like. German Pinot Noirs that are stylistically                       similar  to Burgundy. Obviously, these are not carbon copies because the terroir is different but                 the idea is that the wines are elegance and complexity driven. Henrik Mobitz was our original                     leader here and now, today’s winemaker, Jürgen Von der Mark, is our iconic producer in this                     style.

Jürgen’s Grand Cru wines are simply brilliant expressions of Pinot Noir. They are light to medium bodied and have just unreal flavor intensity. The purity and the clarity of the fruit is top notch. They are wispy and sneakily dense plus the Grand Cru wines have that trademark JVDM (Jürgen Von der Mark) for short. These are genius wines of the highest degree and made lovingly in small batches with the knowledge and experience of a master of wine.

**The Wine** Today’s wine is the 2022 Jürgen Von der Mark “blah blah ” Pinot Noir for $55.99 a bottle on a 4-pack. This is VERY Limited.

Such a deep, complex and aromatic nose. Deep mid season cherry and overwhelming spice . Such stunning spice. Singular. Super earthy and mineral. Like a MSD 1er Cru or SLB Dominode. But so elegant and precise. A nose one can sniff and sniff. 9.7 nose. Genius. Leaves. Sweet leaves and sweet spices. Baking spices and such vivid cherry fruit. Sick nose . Incredible. The next cult German Pinot type of nose . It’s so obvious. It’s got intense black cherries , mid season best of bunch red cherries and some cooling blueberry that echoes Cecile Tremblay. It’s dark but not too dark. Loads of spice and earth . But it is so so so refined. Delicate yet intense nose. Superb. Beguiling. Huge minerality . Smells like red rocks. Nimble. You sniff for 10 minutes before you sip as it is so good and keeps changing. After 10 minutes there are just massive complex aromas.

On day 2, deep and expansive. Prieure Roche is what it reminds me of today! Finally! Been trying to figure out for days now. Deep cherry, blueberry, earth and mint. So precise and so much clarity. Cranberry, cherry and so clean. Wow.

Palate has insane inner mouth perfume and massive elegance. Just an incredible palate. Mega juicy and so complex. So pure and clean. Lithe and elegant but intensely flavored with bursting mid season red and black cherries . Incredible energy and sweetness of fruit and man it is so so elegant. Pure as the driven snow. This is mega elite . The intensity combined with delicacy is incredible. Masterful balance and top of the line purity. Delicate and powdery tannins with perfect ethereal texture. Insanely long finish. This is as transparent and beguilingly fruity as it gets. OMG the purity, finesse and balance here are just divine as is the sweetness. Such sweet cherry fruit and dense yet nimble. This is so stunning and so insane. Balance is perfect. This really is special. Amazing length and just as pure as pure can be. So complex, tangy sweet and fresh. Just spellbound by this.

On Day 2 this was better. Juicy, so clean. Amazing mouthfeel, purity and balance. Best mid season cherries ever. Juicy and so clean. Delicate, haunting and complex.
