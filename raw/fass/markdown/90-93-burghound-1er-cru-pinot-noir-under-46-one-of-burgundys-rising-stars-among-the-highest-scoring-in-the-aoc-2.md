The 2022 Francois Buffet Pernand-Vergelesses “Les Vergelesses” is the best Pernand I’ve ever had and I’ve had a lot. As soon as I opened it and stuck my nose in the glass I was floored. It had that unmistakable depth and purity that one gets only in super special wines. This was and is a benchmark in my wine career. I only had a small allocation until I tasted it. It is a privilege and honor to be able to sell a wine this profound for such a fair price. I recently had a new customer start with us and I asked why he is shopping with us and he said that you are, according to my friend one of the last if not the last places in America to sell elite Burgundy at a fair price. I think that’s one of the greatest compliments I have ever had in all my years in the wine business.

Gorgeous, deep, vivid and focused nose of mid season cherries, dark cherries and black cherries . So well defined, vivid and clear. This really captures the 2020 fruit so perfectly. Nose also has terrific mineral tones and earth tones. What makes this the best Pernand i’ve ever had is the absolute clarity and cleanliness of the aromas . They are so well defined and just so vivid. This is only something one gets in much more elite appellations like Vosne Romanee or Chambolle Musigny.

On day 2, nose is dusty and earthy. Super sappy / super cherry, damson and violets. Palate is mega juicy and super pure with crazy depth. Such fine tannins. Perfect balance. Masterful. Terrific power and depth while remaining elegant. Stunning.

Terrific palate. Incredible inner mouth aromas. Juicy and full of freshness, sour cherries, sweet red cherries and tons of acid. Again on the palate as is reflected on the nose is the clarity, vividness and depth of fruit. You can only get this from the most perfectly ripened fruit and insane attention in the vineyard to get that fruit. Crunchy palate with great cut and precise like the finest diamond in the world. Insane energy and it’s so so linear. Man, this is well made and so sleek. The complexity is incredible. Earth, mineral and so much fruit and sap. Gorgeous purity and unending length with loads of cherry flower, cherry compote and wonderful echo of high quality limestone on the finish. The piercing quality of the ridiculously intense sweet fruit combined with the sweetness (yes more sweetness) and freshness is just remarkable. Amazing texture and length. This is Grand Cru in every way shape or form. Remarkable value here. Stunning purity and length . Lovely licorice lines the finish. A Burg lover’s dream. Sensational wine and wonderful to see what Buffet does outside of Volnay and Pommard. Awesome mineral wash on the finish full of freshness and acid. How is this a 9.5? I gave this 95 points which I’ve never given a Pernand that much. But it is. Stunning wine and classical structure that invites a decade plus of aging. Will this go to a 96 points in the future?
