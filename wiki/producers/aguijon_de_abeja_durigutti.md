# Aguijón de Abeja (Durigutti)

Winemaker: **Héctor & Pablo Durigutti**. Multi-region: Patagonia / Salta / Catamarca / San Juan (Patagonia). Category: **Emerging**.

## Approach

Durigutti brothers' multi-regional organic and natural wine project ranging across Argentina's most distinctive terroirs — from Patagonia to Catamarca to San Juan. Certified organic and natural across the range. Showcases how Argentina's diverse climates and elevations express the same varieties in radically different ways.

## Grapes

Malbec, Cabernet Sauvignon, Bonarda, Cabernet Franc, Chardonnay/Semillon

## Key Wines

- Malbec (Patagonia)
- Cabernet Sauvignon (Salta)
- Bonarda (San Juan)
- Malbec Reserva (Patagonia)
