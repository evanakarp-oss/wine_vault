# Finca Suarez

Winemaker: **Juanfa Suárez**. Paraje Altamira (Mendoza). Category: **Radical Natural**.

## Approach

Clay tinajas, zero sulfites, native yeasts, unfiltered. Considered one of the pioneers of natural wine in Argentina. Extreme authenticity, vibrant acidity, and an artistic sensibility felt in every bottle.

## Grapes

Semillon, Malbec, Criolla

## Key Wines

- El Gavilán
- El Risueño
