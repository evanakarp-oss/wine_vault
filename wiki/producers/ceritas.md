# Ceritas

John and Phoebe Raytek's Sonoma Coast label — site-driven, early-picked Chardonnay and Pinot Noir from cool coastal parcels, made in a restrained, mineral register. The cellar also holds their rare Peter Martin Ray Cabernet (Santa Cruz Mountains) — squarely the tension/sense-of-place axis.
