# Villa Calicantus

Smallest Bardolino winery (1.2 hectares), located in Calmasino village (eastern Lake Garda shores), on one of Bardolino Classico's highest hills surrounded by olive trees and small woods. Owner and winemaker Daniele Delaini's first vintage 2011; family history extends to 1860s with his great-grandfather founding winery. Biodynamic producer cultivating Corvina, Rondinella, Molinara (Bardolino's classic red varieties). Produces red, white, rosé, sparkling, sweet wines, and spirits.

## Notes

**Historic small-scale:** Great-grandfather's 1860s founding; Delaini's contemporary biodynamic stewardship spans 150+ years. Lake Garda moderation of continental climate. 1.2 hectare constraint creates scarcity-driven positioning. Highest-hill location in Bardolino Classico zone offers elevation advantage. Tri-varietal Bardolino blend reflects regional tradition.
