# Dario Princic

Oslavia natural winemaker, a close friend of Stanko Radikon and part of the same
macerated-white circle. Works Ribolla, Pinot Grigio (the coppery **Sivi**),
Chardonnay, Sauvignon and the **Trebez** blend with weeks of skin contact, native
ferments and minimal sulfur. Already in the cellar (2020 Bianco Trebez); page created
during the 2026-07 Friuli pass ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).

**Disambiguation:** three Princic estates matter in Collio — *Dario* (this page,
Oslavia natural school), [[doro_princic|Doro Princic]] (Pradis, classic
stainless-steel whites), and Damian Princic of [[colle_duga|Colle Duga]] (Zegla).
Unrelated styles; don't cross-match retailer offers on surname alone.
