# Domaine Leroy

Lalou Bize-Leroy's domaine, established 1988 around the former Domaine Charles
Noëllat in Vosne-Romanée (with Domaine d'Auvenay as her personal estate).
Biodynamic from the outset, with microscopic yields and an uncompromising
selection — among the most sought-after and expensive wines in Burgundy. Grand
cru holdings include Musigny, Chambertin, Clos de Vougeot, Richebourg,
Romanée-Saint-Vivant and Latricières-Chambertin, alongside village and premier
cru parcels. Imported to the US via Wine Source.
