# Valentini

Legendary, fiercely traditional Abruzzo estate — for many the benchmark
producer of the region and maker of one of Italy's greatest white wines, the
**Trebbiano d'Abruzzo**. The family declassifies aggressively, bottling only a
tiny fraction of production in the best years, which makes the wines scarce and
long-lived; aged Trebbiano develops extraordinary depth and ages for decades.
The Cerasuolo d'Abruzzo (rosato) and Montepulciano d'Abruzzo are similarly
cult-status. **Flagged as a going-forward focus / watchlist target** (Evan,
2026-06-22) — aged white in Evan's wheelhouse, primarily sourced at auction and
via private collections rather than standing retail.
