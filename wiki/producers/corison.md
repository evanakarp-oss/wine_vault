# Corison

_Cathy Corison's restrained St. Helena Cabernet — picked early for freshness and made in a mid-weight, transparent style that ages for decades. The estate Kronos Vineyard (own-rooted old vines on the St. Helena bench) is the flagship; the Napa Valley bottling is the value entry._

## Notes

Track-1 reference producer (CLAUDE.md): elegance and site-transparency over power. Corison bet her career on picking earlier and lower-alcohol than 1990s Napa fashion, and the wines have proven the case — 25-year-old Corison is still fresh. Kronos is organically farmed, own-rooted, low-yielding. A benchmark for the 'sense of place + tension' axis the cellar is built around.
