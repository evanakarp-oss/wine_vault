# G. Calabrese

Small-scale winery in Reggio di Calabria, the southernmost province of mainland Italy, working with native Calabrian varieties. G. Calabrese tends approximately 10-15 hectares of vineyard using sustainable viticulture methods adapted to Calabria's challenging Mediterranean terrain. The producer focuses on Gaglioppo as the primary variety, while maintaining traditional cultivation practices reflective of Calabrian heritage viticulture.

## Notes

G. Calabrese represents the family winemaking tradition in Reggio di Calabria, one of Italy's most historically marginalized wine regions. Located in Calabria's toe, near the Strait of Messina, the area benefits from unique microclimatic conditions influenced by maritime and mountainous terrain. Gaglioppo wines from G. Calabrese showcase the variety's depth and structural potential, displaying ripe red fruit, herbal spice, and mineral undertones characteristic of quality Calabrian reds. The producer's commitment to sustainable farming and varietal authenticity reflects the gradual transformation of Calabrian viticulture toward quality-focused production. As part of an emerging producer network, G. Calabrese contributes to changing perceptions of Calabria from a historical bulk-wine region to a source of distinctive, terroir-driven Mediterranean wines worthy of serious consideration.
