# Pierre & Rodolphe Gauthier

Bourgueil-based organic producer with 8 bottles in cellar. Gauthier focuses on traditional Bourgueil Cab Franc from clay/sand terroirs. Organic viticulture and natural winemaking produce distinctive, mineral-driven wines built for extended aging.

## Notes

Grower-focused producer emphasizing site expression and minimal intervention. Gauthier's wines show the characteristic Bourgueil elegance—acidity-driven, mineral-focused, food-pairing oriented. Built for 10-15 year aging. Solid quality producer at this portfolio level.
