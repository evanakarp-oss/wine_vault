# La Biancara

Legendary natural wine producer founded 1988 when Angiolino Maule left pizzeria business for volcanic hillside vines in Gambellara, Veneto. Four decades refining minimal-intervention philosophy: no herbicides, pesticides, chemical fertilizers; limited copper/sulfur; hand harvesting; spontaneous fermentation; zero micro-oxygenation. Biodynamic certified; 18 hectares across Gambellara and Colli Berici. Founded VinNatur association 2006 (130+ small European producers) funding soil-health research, natural viticulture science, low-sulfur winemaking.

## Notes

**Movement founder:** Angiolino's influence on Italian natural-wine generation profound; VinNatur represents formalized commitment to terroir and soil health. Zero-sulfur Garganega defines modern white natural wine. Pizzeria-to-vineyard transition story exemplifies passion-driven viticulture. Volcanic soils in Gambellara create distinctive minerality. Grenache (Tai Rosso) and Merlot complement regional Garganega focus.
