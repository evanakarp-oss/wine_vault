# Ronco delle Betulle

Pioneering organic producer in Rosazzo (Colli Orientali del Friuli), established 1967 by Giovanbattista Adami. Now managed by Ivana Adami and son Simone, farming 12 certified-organic hectares focused on native Friuli grape varieties—Friulano, Ribolla Gialla, Refosco, and the hyperlocal Pignolo rarity—producing ~70,000 bottles/year of regionally distinctive wines.

## History & Philosophy

Ronco delle Betulle was founded in 1967 by Giovanbattista Adami on the eastern hills of Friuli, in the Rosazzo subregion. Ivana Adami assumed stewardship in the 1990s, modernizing the cellar while respecting traditional winemaking styles. She now works alongside her son Simone, representing the estate's evolution into a fully modern, quality-focused producer.

**Farming Philosophy**: The estate's 12 hectares have been **certified organic since the 2019 vintage**. Ivana emphasizes the **preservation of authentic character** in fine wines and the **avoidance of artificial manipulation**—a philosophy reflected in restrained, terroir-focused winemaking.

## Vineyard & Terroir

Located on the eastern hills of Friuli in Rosazzo, the vineyard benefits from a distinctive terroir shaped by the region's marl and sandstone geology. The estate focuses on **native Friuli grape varieties**, eschewing international cultivars in favor of regional heritage.

## Key Wines & Varieties

- **Friulano** (white)
- **Ribolla Gialla** (white)
- **Refosco dal Peduncolo Rosso** (red)
- **Pignolo** (red) — hyperlocal Friuli rarity, increasingly difficult to find
- **Villa Oleis Rosso** (red blend)
- **Narciso** (Rosazzo blend)

**H. Mercer Wine & Spirit Imports** distributes Ronco delle Betulle wines in the USA.
