# La Psigula

Tiny new-generation Alto Piemonte estate in Curino, in the Biellese hills — founded by young winemaker Giacomo Foglia and his wife Claudia at the foot of an old tower (hence the 'Vigna Torre' cru). Bramaterra DOC: the volcanic-porphyry Spanna/Croatina/Vespolina blend south of Gattinara. Bramaterra $46, Riserva Vigna Torre $74 — under-radar entry to the appellation alongside Tenuta Sella and Antoniolo.
