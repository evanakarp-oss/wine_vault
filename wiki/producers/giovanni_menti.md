# Giovanni Menti

Fourth-generation winery (7.5 hectares, Gambellara, Vicenza) with roots in late-19th-century migration from Agno valley "Menti di Sopra" to Lessinia foothills. Current operator Stefano Menti focuses on biodynamic agriculture; converted entire vineyard to organic 2011. Old vines, volcanic soils. Main focus Garganega white grape; also cultivates Durella. Produces natural whites, pet-nats, traditional sparkling wines. Four generations of family winemaking in Gambellara village.
