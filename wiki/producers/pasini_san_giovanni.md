# Pasini San Giovanni

Third-generation family winery (founded 1958 by Andrea Pasini) on Lake Garda's Brescia shore, operating 36 hectares across Valtènesi and Lugana appellations. Certified organic since 2016, fully solar-powered carbon-neutral operation. Pioneered single-varietal Valtènesi rosé using indigenous Groppello Gentile (2014). Specializes in Lugana (Verdicchio-based whites), Valtènesi reds, and Metodo Classico sparkling from heritage varieties.

## Notes

**Terroir:** Lake Garda moderates continental climate; microclimate allows ripening of delicate Groppello and Turbiana. Pioneering producer: first winery to release single-variety Valtènesi Rosé. Biodiversity emphasis in vineyard management; indigenous-varietal focus reflects regional identity. Carbon-neutral via renewables; represents sustainable-farming frontier in Lake Garda region.
