# Domaine des Roches Neuves

Thierry Germain's biodynamic Saumur-Champigny estate — one of the Loire's most influential producers of the modern era. The FASS book is deep: Franc de Pied (own-rooted Cabernet Franc), Clos de l'Échelier (single-vineyard), Les Mémoires (old-vine selection), Clos Romans (white), Marginale, plus Clos d'Échalier Blanc. Stylistically silky, perfumed, low SO2; Roches Neuves is the reference for what biodynamic Cabernet Franc can be at this latitude.
