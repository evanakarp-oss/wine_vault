# Walter Massa

Colli Tortonesi-based producer in Piemonte. Walter Massa is known for distinctive Cortese and other white wines from the Colli Tortonesi region.

## Terroir & Appellation

**Colli Tortonesi, Piemonte** — Tortonese Hills with limestone soils; producing distinctive white wines (Cortese) and light reds.
