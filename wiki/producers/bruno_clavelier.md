# Bruno Clavelier

Vosne-based domaine, ~6 ha, run by Bruno Clavelier from inherited family
vines (his grandfather was a vigneron in Vosne; Bruno took over in the
late 1980s after a sports-medicine career). **Biodynamic since 2002.**
Old vines throughout (most parcels are 50+ years).

## Holdings

A scattered but carefully chosen footprint along the Côte:

- **Vosne-Romanée 1er Cru** Les Beaux Monts, La Combe Brûlée, Aux Brûlées
- **Vosne-Romanée Villages** Les Hauts Maizières, La Montagne (old-vine
  parcels just outside the 1er Cru tier)
- **Chambolle-Musigny 1er Cru** La Combe d'Orveau (Vieilles Vignes)
- **Nuits-Saint-Georges 1er Cru** Aux Cras
- **Corton Grand Cru** Le Rognet
- Bourgogne and Bourgogne-Aligoté from Vosne sources

## Why this domaine matters

Clavelier is the **Vosne-villages-from-old-vines** angle on the study view.
Hauts Maizières and La Montagne sit right at the Vosne-Flagey hinge, on
slope continuous with Beaumonts, and the old-vine fruit + biodynamics push
the wines well above the village-level price point. The Combe d'Orveau
Chambolle parcel is also worth noting — it abuts Musigny on the slope and
is a textbook example of "Chambolle 1er adjacent to grand cru dirt."
