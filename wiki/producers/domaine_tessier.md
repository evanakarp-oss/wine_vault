# Domaine Arnaud Tessier

Meursault domaine that Arnaud Tessier built after his family stopped selling grapes to the négoce, bottling its own wines from 2007 and taking the reins at 21 when his father died in 2009. Roughly 7.5 ha across some of Meursault's best lieux-dits, farmed organically though uncertified.
