# Matías Michelini (projects)

Winemaker: **Matías Michelini**. Gualtallary / San Pablo (Mendoza). Category: **Low Intervention**.

## Approach

Pioneer of the Argentine new wave. Native yeasts, concrete eggs, low alcohol, minimalist approach. International reference point for Gualtallary. #10 James Suckling Top 100 Argentina 2025 for Agua de Roca Sauvignon Blanc.

## Grapes

Sauvignon Blanc, Malbec, Chardonnay, Grenache

- Caos Chardonnay
- GarnaCHE
