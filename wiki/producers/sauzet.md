# Domaine Étienne Sauzet

_A blue-chip Puligny-Montrachet domaine — premiers crus Les Combettes, Champ Canet, Les Perrières, Les Champs Gain and grand crus Bâtard, Bienvenues-Bâtard, Chevalier and a sliver of Montrachet — farmed biodynamically since around 2010 and made in a pure, precise, ageworthy style._

## Notes

Classic Puligny address that shifted to biodynamics under Benoît Riffault and Emilie Boudot, gaining freshness and site-transparency. The Champ Canet '20 6-pack ($116) and Combettes ($133–150) in the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] are the fair-value entries to the range.
