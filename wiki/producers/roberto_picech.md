# Roberto Picèch

Small family estate on the Pradis hill (Cormons), ~7 ha. Roberto (with wife Alessia,
label "Jelka" honors his mother) makes textured, patient Collio whites — Friulano,
Malvasia, Pinot Bianco, the **Athena** blend and a Collio Bianco Jelka — plus the
Rosso Ruben riserva (Merlot/Cab). Wines see extended lees time and release late for
the zone. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]), which carries an unusually deep
Picèch magnum vertical.
