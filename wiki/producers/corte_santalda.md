# Corte Sant'Alda

Highly regarded biodynamic estate in Valpolicella foothills (Mezzane area, ~30km northeast Verona) operated by Marinella Camerani, named after her daughter Alda. Mid-1980s courageous transition to organic viticulture. 15 hectares certified organic in Lessini Mountain foothills. Produces six reds: Valpolicella, Valpolicella Superiore, Valpolicella Ripasso Superiore, Recioto, and two Amarones, plus white Soave. Emerged from Valpolicella's backwaters to regional prominence; celebrated for extraordinary balance, concentration, complexity.

## Notes

**Regional pioneer:** Marinella's organic transition (mid-1980s) predates modern natural-wine movement. Amarone specialization combined with organic farming creates premium positioning. Lessini foothills terroir provides altitude and soil complexity. Ripasso and two Amarone offerings reflect depth of production ambition. Soave entry demonstrates cross-regional diversification.
