# Le Due Terre

Flavio Basilicata and Silvana Forte's micro-estate (~5 ha) at Prepotto — one of the
quiet greats of the Colli Orientali. **Sacrisassi Bianco** (Friulano/Ribolla) and
**Sacrisassi Rosso** (Schioppettino/Refosco), plus a varietal Schioppettino, Merlot
and Pinot Nero: low yields, old-vine concentration, gentle old-wood élevage.
Already owned in the cellar; the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list carries them too
("The Two Lands" — [[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
