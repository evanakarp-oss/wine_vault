# Champagne Guiborat

Damery-based family grower recently revitalized by Richard and Karine Foquet. Brad Baker, The Champagne Warrior, wrote in June 2014: "Richard and Karine Foquet have injected a giant dose of awesome into the family's wares ... I find [the wines] impossible not to love ... the prices are ridiculous for the quality and I have a feeling this will change soon."

## Notes

Emerging producer with compelling quality-to-price ratio. Minimal intervention approach and organic/sustainable practices. Damery location (Marne Valley) provides lighter, more elegant fruit profile. Excellent entry point for grower Champagne collectors seeking value and future upside.
