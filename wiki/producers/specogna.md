# Specogna

Third-generation estate at Corno di Rosazzo founded by Leonardo Specogna (1963), now
run by grandsons Cristian and Michele. Bridges classic and ambitious: skin-contact
**Pinot Grigio Riserva** (ramato style), the **Identità** white blend, Friulano,
Ribolla, Sauvignon, plus Schioppettino and Pignolo on ponca hillsides. Surfaced for
the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
