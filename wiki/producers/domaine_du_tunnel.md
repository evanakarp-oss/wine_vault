# Domaine du Tunnel

Stéphane Robert founded Domaine du Tunnel in 1996 from a rented one-hectare Cornas parcel; the domaine now spans Cornas, Saint-Péray (both still and roussanne-driven Pur Blanc / Prestige), Saint-Joseph, and a tiny Condrieu. Vin Noir is the flagship Cornas — old-vine Syrah from Champelrose, lifted, structured. Saint-Péray Pur Blanc is the white anchor: 100% Roussanne, old vines, mineral and dense.
