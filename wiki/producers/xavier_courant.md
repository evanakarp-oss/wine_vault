# Xavier Courant – Domaine L'Oubliée

Bourgueil-based biodynamic producer with 8 bottles in cellar. Courant focuses on small-scale, natural winemaking emphasizing old-vine Cab Franc and distinctive site expression. L'Oubliée produces wines built for extended aging with minimal intervention philosophy.
