# Mickael Bourg

Vincent Paris alum (worked with Allemand before that) — Mickael's flagship is the Cornas Les P'tits Bout ($66–$72), with a Saint-Péray and an entry-level VDF La Démarcante (no AOC, native fermentation, no SO2 additions at bottling). Among the younger generation pushing Cornas in a more lifted, elegant direction.
