# Domaine Hubert Lamy

_Olivier Lamy has turned Saint-Aubin into a grand-cru-quality address through obsessive viticulture — most famously the ultra-high-density plantings (up to 30,000 vines/ha) in Clos du Meix and Derrière chez Édouard. Precise, mineral, low-oxygen whites that punch far above their appellation._

## Notes

One of the great value stories in white Burgundy: St-Aubin premiers crus (En Remilly, Clos de la Chatenière, Les Frionnes, Derrière chez Édouard) that rival Puligny at a fraction of the price, plus a tiny Criots-Bâtard. The NEW Lamy block in the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] — St-Aubin En Remilly and Puligny Tremblots VV cases at $110–133/btl — is grand-cru-quality Chardonnay from a terroir grower.
