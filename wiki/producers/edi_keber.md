# Edi Keber

The great radical-classicist of Zegla (Cormons): around 2010 Edi and son Kristian
reduced the estate to essentially **one wine** — Collio Bianco, a Friulano-led blend
with Ribolla and Malvasia — arguing that *Collio* itself, not the varietal, is the
brand. Concrete and used-oak élevage, built to age a decade. A tiny amount of Merlot
persists in some vintages. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
