# Colle Duga

Damian Princic's small family estate on the Zegla hill outside Cormons (~7 ha) —
insider's Collio: Friulano, Sauvignon, Chardonnay, a Collio Bianco and a serious
Merlot, farmed and made by one family with quiet precision. Regularly one of the
best-value Tre Bicchieri-tier names in the zone. Surfaced for the vault via the
2026-07 Friuli enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]),
garbled there as "Duga Hill". Not to be confused with the other Princics — see
[[dario_princic|Dario Princic]] for the three-way disambiguation.
