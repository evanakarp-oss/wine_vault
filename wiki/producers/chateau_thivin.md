# Château Thivin

The reference estate of Côte-de-Brouilly — Geoffray family, Mont Brouilly slope. FASS book is wide: Cuvée Zaccharie (top cuvée), Cuvée Godefroy, La Chapelle (old vines), Les Griottes de Brulhié. Granite-and-blue-stone (cornieule) terroir gives the Thivin Gamays their characteristic mineral grip. Classical rather than natural, but a Beaujolais icon by any measure.
