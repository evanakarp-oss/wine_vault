# Domaine du Collier

Faye d'Anjou estate producing distinctive whites and reds under the direction of Antoine Foucault (son of Charlie Foucault, Clos Rougeard founder). Le Rouge & Le Blanc described the 2010 Charpentrie blanc as "the 2nd best wine of their tasting just behind the Clos Rougeard," noting "The majority of tasters delighted in the power, mineral and aerian character of this great chenin."

## Notes

Organic viticulture in Faye d'Anjou, with access to prime Savennières parcels. Antoine Foucault brings Clos Rougeard pedigree to a smaller, focused operation. Dry Chenins emphasize mineral tension and food pairing over fruit. Reds (primarily Cabernet Franc) show similar site-driven philosophy. Emerging producer with significant upside.
