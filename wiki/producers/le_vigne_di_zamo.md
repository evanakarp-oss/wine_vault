# Le Vigne di Zamò

Substantial Rosazzo-area estate (Zamò family since 1924, with vineyards on the
Rosazzo abbey hill and Buttrio). Known for old-vine Friulano (**Vigne Cinquant'anni**
line), **Pinot Bianco Tullio Zamò**, Malvasia del Leon, the Ronco delle Acacie white
blend, and serious native reds — **Pignolo** especially, plus Ronco dei Roseti.
Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]) with the second-deepest bench
(17 line items).
