# Benjamin Zidarich

Pioneering biodynamic-natural producer building estate from 1998 (founded 1988) in Prepotto near Duino Aurisina, north Trieste, on 8 hectares in rocky Carso region bordering Slovenia. Extremely natural viticulture: zero SO2 throughout vinification, lunar-calendar bottling on full moons. Cultivates primarily Vitovska (local classic), plus Malvasia, Sauvignon, Teran (red), Merlot. ~28,000 bottles annually. Iron-rich red loamy topsoil over pure limestone (280m asl, west-facing, Gulf of Trieste views). Recognized for pioneering amber/orange wines.

## Notes

**Radical naturalism:** Zero SO2 production rare even in natural-wine movement. Lunar-calendar viticulture reflects full biodynamic commitment. Vitovska—territorial variety rarely found outside Carso—expresses limestone minerality in orange-wine style. Iron-rich terroir creates distinctive soil profile. Beachfront Carso location (overlooking Gulf of Trieste) creates microclimate with Mediterranean modulation.
