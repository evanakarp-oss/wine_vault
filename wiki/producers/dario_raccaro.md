# Dario Raccaro

Tiny Cormons cellar (~4 ha) revered for two whites: **Malvasia** (from 80+-year-old
vines in town) and **Friulano Vigna del Rolat** — both benchmarks of unoaked,
old-vine Collio intensity. Small Pinot Grigio and Merlot too. As artisan as Collio
gets without joining the macerated school. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07
Friuli enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
