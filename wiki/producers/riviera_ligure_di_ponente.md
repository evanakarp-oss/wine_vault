# Riviera Ligure di Ponente

Producer from the Ponente (western) Liguria coast. Riviera Ligure di Ponente is known for Rossese and other Mediterranean coastal wine varieties reflecting the region's distinctive terroir.

## Terroir & Appellation

**Ponente, Liguria** — Western Ligurian coast with Mediterranean influence; limestone soils producing distinctive red wines (Rossese) and mineral-driven whites.
