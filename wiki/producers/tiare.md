# Tiare

Roberto Snidarcig's small estate in Dolegna del Collio ("tiare" is Friulian for
*earth/soil*). Sauvignon specialist — his Collio Sauvignon has won the Concours
Mondial du Sauvignon's top prize more than once — plus Friulano, Ribolla, Pinot
Grigio and a Pinot Nero. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
