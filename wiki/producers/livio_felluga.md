# Livio Felluga

The patriarch estate of the Colli Orientali — the famous antique-map label. Livio
Felluga (d. 2016 at 102) rebuilt hillside viticulture at Rosazzo after WWII; the
house remains family-run at real scale (~160 ha). **Terre Alte** (Friulano/Pinot
Bianco/Sauvignon) and **Abbazia di Rosazzo** (Rosazzo DOCG) are the flagship whites;
**Picolit**, the Sossò Merlot-Refosco-Pignolo, and Pignolo riserva lead the top end.
Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]) — the list's single deepest
producer (14 line items).

restaurant-tier. Between the famous houses, prefer this over Jermann for structure.
