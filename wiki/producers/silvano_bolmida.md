# Silvano Bolmida

Small family estate (~7.4 ha) in the historic Bussia cru of Monforte d'Alba,
between roughly 420–470 m on limestone-clay soils. Founded by Silvano Bolmida,
who trained at the Alba wine school; farming is organic/sustainable. The
winemaking is built for power and structure: long, slow fermentations with
extended skin macerations of 60–120 days, aging in a mix of small barrels and
large casks, then a long settling in stainless before bottling. Flagships are
Barolo Bussia, Barolo Vigne dei Fantini and the Barolo Bussia Riserva — exactly
the "more powerful" register JP pointed to.
