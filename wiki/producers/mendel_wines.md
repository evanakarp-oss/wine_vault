# Mendel Wines

Winemaker: **Roberto de la Mota**. Perdriel, Luján de Cuyo + Paraje Altamira (Mendoza). Category: **Artisan / Terroir-Driven**.

## Approach

Roberto de la Mota — widely considered the 'Godfather' of modern Argentine wine — founded Mendel in 2002 with the Sielecki family around historic 1928 estate vines in Perdriel. Son of legendary winemaker Raúl de la Mota; trained under Émile Peynaud in Montpellier; architect of the Terrazas de los Andes and Cheval des Andes programs. At Mendel: minimal intervention, no filtering, old vines only. Revival of ungrafted 70–80 year-old Semillon and Chenin Blanc — the Petit Verdot for Unus blend was massal selection from Château Margaux vines brought to Argentina in 1994. Tim Atkin Winemaking Legend 2024.
