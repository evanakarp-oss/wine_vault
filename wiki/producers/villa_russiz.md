# Villa Russiz

Historic Capriva estate founded 1869 by the French Count Théodore de La Tour, who
brought French vine cuttings to Collio — one of the region's founding documents for
Sauvignon and Merlot. Run as a charitable foundation. Flagships: **Sauvignon de La
Tour** and the Merlot **Graf de La Tour**, both age-worthy; plus classic Collio
Friulano, Pinot Bianco, Ribolla. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli
enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
