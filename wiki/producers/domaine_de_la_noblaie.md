# Domaine de la Noblaie

Bourgueil-based biodynamic producer. 16 bottles indicates strong conviction holding. Demeter-certified biodynamic viticulture on alluvial/sandy soils producing elegant, mineral-driven Cab Franc built for 15-20 year aging. Noblaie emphasizes site expression and minimal intervention—natural fermentations, low sulfur.

## Notes

One of Loire's finest biodynamic Cab Franc specialists. Distinctive lighter, more graceful style compared to heavier slate-based Chinons—emphasizing freshness, acidity, and food pairing. Consistent quality and biodynamic farming philosophy align perfectly with your curation taste. Should be aged 5-7 years minimum before opening.
