# Mugneret-Gibourg

_One of the great addresses of Vosne-Romanée. Founded by Dr. Georges Mugneret and now run by his daughters Marie-Christine Teillaud and Marie-Andrée Nauleau (the "Sœurs" of Panzer's offer), the domaine makes some of the Côte de Nuits' most sought-after, perfumed, precisely-built Pinot — Grand Cru Ruchottes-Chambertin, Clos Vougeot and Échezeaux, Nuits-Saint-Georges 1er Cru (Les Chaignots, Vignes Rondes), and Vosne/Chambolle villages. Tiny production, cult allocation demand._
