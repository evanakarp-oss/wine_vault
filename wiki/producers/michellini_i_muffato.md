# Michellini i Muffato

Winemaker: **Gerardo Michellini & Andrea Muffato**. El Peral, Tupungato (Mendoza). Category: **Radical Natural**.

## Approach

Pre-phylloxera vines from 1891. Natural winemaking in clay vessels, no additions, selection of historic parcels. Certezas Semillon won Best White at Argentina Reloaded London 2022.

## Grapes

Semillon, Chenin Blanc, Chardonnay

## Key Wines

- Certezas Semillon

## Source

Wine Anorak / Argentina Reloaded London 2022
