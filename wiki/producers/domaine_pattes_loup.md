# Domaine Pattes Loup

Chablis-based young domaine under the direction of Thomas Pico. Antonio Galloni commented that it is "arguably the single most exciting young domaine in Chablis today ... these are some of the most ground-breaking, intensely captivating wines being made in Chablis today. Readers should do whatever they can to taste them."

## Notes

Small-scale organic viticulture in Chablis Grand Cru. Pico represents the new generation of rigorous, terroir-focused Chablis producers. Minimal intervention approach—natural fermentations, extended aging—produces mineral-driven, structured Chardonnays built for 15-20 year evolution. Significant critical acclaim and limited production create strong demand. One of Burgundy's most exciting emerging producers.
