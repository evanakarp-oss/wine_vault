# Livon

Mid-size family house at Dolegnano (founded 1964) spanning Collio and Colli
Orientali, with the **Villa Chiopris** entry-line (incl. Prosecco), sparkling Ribolla,
and top bottlings like Braide Alte. Competent commercial tier with some serious
single-vineyard wines (Pinot Bianco Cavezzo). Also owns estates in Chianti/Montalcino
and Umbria. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
