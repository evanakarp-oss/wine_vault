# Eugenio Collavini

Large family house at Corno di Rosazzo (founded 1896), best known for popularizing
**sparkling Ribolla Gialla** ("Il Grigio" / Ribolla Brut) and the Broy Collio Bianco.
The "Dal Giardino Oltre" line on the 2026-07 list is a newer premium series.
Commercial scale with solid technical quality. Surfaced for the vault via the
2026-07 Friuli enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
