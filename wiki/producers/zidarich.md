# Zidarich

Benjamin Zidarich hand-dug his cellar five stories into the karst at Prepotto
(Duino) and ferments **Vitovska** in carved stone vats (*kamen* = stone) with skin
contact — the definitive expressionist of Carso limestone. Vitovska, Vitovska
Kamen, Malvasia, Prulke blend; Terrano in red. Poured in CSW's 2026 Friuli offer.
Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
