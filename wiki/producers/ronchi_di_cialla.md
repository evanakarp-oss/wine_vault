# Ronchi di Cialla

The Rapuzzi family's estate in the cool Cialla valley (Prepotto) — the producer that
**saved Schioppettino from extinction** in the 1970s (Dina and Paolo Rapuzzi won the
first Risit d'Aur for it) and re-established Pignolo, Verduzzo and Picolit. Cialla
is effectively their monopole sub-zone: limestone, cool air, and a house habit of
holding back stock for late release. Already the cellar's deepest Friuli producer;
CSW featured them in the 2026 Friuli offer.
