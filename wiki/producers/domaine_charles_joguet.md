# Domaine Charles Joguet

Legendary Chinon producer established 1957. Organic viticulture focusing on old-vine Cabernet Franc from distinctive terroirs (Clos de la Dioterie, Clos de la Cure, Varennes du Grand Clos). Joguet's philosophy emphasizes minimal intervention and natural expression of slate/clay soils.

## Notes

17 bottles in cellar indicates this is a cornerstone Loire holding. Charles Joguet remains one of France's greatest Cab Franc specialists—wines built for 20-30 year aging. Distinctive mineral, herbaceous profile with firm tannin structure. Each vineyard parcel bottled separately to show terroir differences. Clos de la Dioterie represents the flagship (deep slate soils, intense structure). Wine Advocate and Vinous consistently rate top cuvées 90+. Essential Loire producer; should be a benchmark for Cab Franc understanding.
