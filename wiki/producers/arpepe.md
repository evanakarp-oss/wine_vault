# Arpepe

Fifth-generation family estate in Sondrio, Valtellina, founded in the 19th century as Pelizzatti and reestablished as Arpepe in the 1980s. Operates 15 hectares across three sub-zones: Sassella (9.5 ha), Grumello (4.5 ha), and Inferno (1 ha), planted exclusively with Chiavennasca (Nebbiolo). Run by siblings Isabella, Emanuele, and Guido Pelizzatti Perego.

## Notes

**Historic producer:** Name conflates founder Arturo Pelizzatti Perego's initials (ArPePe). Valtellina's steep alpine terraces and continental climate favor age-worthy, mineral Nebbiolo. Arpepe's focus on single-vineyard expression from each cru reflects traditional Valtellina philosophy. High-altitude viticulture (350–700m) in glacial valley terrain.
