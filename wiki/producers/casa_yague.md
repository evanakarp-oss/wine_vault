# Casa Yagüe

Winemaker: **Patricia Ferrari & Marcelo Yagüe**. Valle de Uco (Mendoza). Category: **Artisan / Terroir-Driven**.

## Approach

Patricia Ferrari and Marcelo Yagüe's family project, balancing Uco Valley terroir expression with minimal intervention. Small production, emphasis on freshness and drinkability over extraction. Featured at Argentina Reloaded Buenos Aires 2025.

## Grapes

Malbec, Pinot Noir, Chardonnay, Semillon

## Key Wines

- Casa Yagüe Malbec
- Casa Yagüe Pinot Noir
- Casa Yagüe Chardonnay
