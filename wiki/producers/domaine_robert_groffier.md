# Domaine Robert Groffier Père & Fils

Cellared in Morey-Saint-Denis but famous for Chambolle, the Groffier family owns one of the largest holdings in the great 1er cru Les Amoureuses, alongside parcels in Bonnes-Mares and Chambertin-Clos de Bèze. Perfumed, silky reds with a devoted following.
