# Cantina Filippi

Exciting family-run producer in Soave's highest-altitude sites (320–400m), led by Filippo Filippi pursuing terroir-driven natural winemaking. Organic certified; emphasizes local varieties Garganega and Trebbiano di Soave with biodiversity respect and natural soil balance. Three single-vineyard Soave crus: Castelcerino (400m elevation), Monteseroni, Vigne della Brà. Cool microclimate and volcanic soils create distinctive freshness, minerality, tension. Located in Castelcerino area, highest Soave municipality section.

## Notes

**Altitude advantage:** High-elevation Soave sites (320–400m) create cool-climate conditions unusual for region. Volcanic soils and altitude combine for tension, freshness, mineral expression. Garganega-Trebbiano focus reflects Soave tradition; terroir-driven approach distinguishes from generic production. Castelcerino cru designation emphasizes geographic specificity.
