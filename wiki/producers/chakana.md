# Chakana

Winemaker: **Juan Pablo Michelini (consultant)**. Agrelo / Paraje Altamira (Mendoza). Category: **Certified Organic + Natural**.

## Approach

Andean reciprocity philosophy. Agroecological practices, transition to organic/biodynamic since 2012. Vientre Malbec: zero sulfites, no acidity correction, zero additives. 'Zero makeup. A high-risk, super transparent wine that speaks of Paraje Altamira at its purest.'

## Grapes

Malbec, Cab Franc, Bonarda

## Key Wines

- Vientre Malbec
- Ayni
- Estate Selection Altamira
