# Robert Chevillon

_The reference address for traditional Nuits-Saint-Georges. Brothers Bertrand and Denis Chevillon farm a deep bench of old-vine premiers crus — Les Saint-Georges, Les Vaucrains, Les Cailles, Les Chaignots, Roncières, Pruliers, Perrières — in a structured, unshowy, terroir-first style built for the cellar._

## Notes

Classic grower Nuits: firm, earthy, ageworthy Pinot from vines up to 80+ years, with a clear cru-to-cru hierarchy (Vaucrains and Les Saint-Georges the most structured, Cailles/Chaignots more perfumed). The NEW Chevillon consignment in the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] — Chaignots '14 8-pack at $50/btl, Roncières '05, Vaucrains '11, St-Georges — is terroir Nuits at fair grower prices.
