# J.J. Prüm

The reference estate of off-dry Mosel Riesling — Manfred Prüm's house in Wehlen. Wehlener Sonnenuhr Kabinett and Graacher Himmelreich Auslese are the FASS book picks. The house style — sulfur-prominent in youth, mind-bendingly long-aging, slate-driven — is one of the wine world's stylistic anchors.
