# Doro Princic

Benchmark *classic* (non-macerated) Collio whites from Pradis di Cormons. Named for
founder Isidoro "Doro" Princic; long run by son Alessandro (Sandro), now with his son
Carlo. Stainless-steel vinification, full malo, bottled unfined — Pinot Bianco and
Malvasia are the famous ones, plus Friulano, Sauvignon and a Merlot. Kermit Lynch
imports to the US. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]); also poured in CSW's 2026 Friuli
offer.
