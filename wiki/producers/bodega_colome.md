# Bodega Colomé

Winemaker: **Thibaut Delmotte**. Calchaquí Valley, Molinos (2,300m) + Altura Máxima (3,111m) (Salta). Category: **Artisan / Terroir-Driven**.

## Approach

Founded 1831 — Argentina's oldest winery, owned by the Hess family since 2001. French winemaker Thibaut Delmotte has been in charge since 2005, transforming it into a consistent Tim Atkin First Growth (2020–2024). Vineyards range from La Brava (1,750m in Cafayate) to Altura Máxima at 3,111m — one of the world's highest. All farmed with biodynamic and organic principles (not certified due to ant nest treatment). Minimal intervention: Auténtico aged in tank and concrete egg, zero oak. #31 James Suckling Top 100 Argentina 2025.

## Grapes

Malbec, Torrontés, Tannat, Bonarda, Cab Sauvignon, Syrah, Petit Verdot
