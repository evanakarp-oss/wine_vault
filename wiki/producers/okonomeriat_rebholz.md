# Ökonomierat Rebholz

Hansjörg Rebholz — the modern voice of Südpfalz, biodynamic, no malolactic, long-aging Rieslings and serious Weiss- and Spätburgunders. Kastanienbusch GG at FASS ($85) is the headline; same hill as Dr. Wehrheim and Sven Klundt.
