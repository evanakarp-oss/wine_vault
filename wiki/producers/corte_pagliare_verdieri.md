# Corte Pagliare Verdieri

Historic winery in Sabbioneta, Mantua province, producing traditional Lambrusco Mantovano from the local Viadanese Sabbionetano clone. Ferments in bottle (ancestral method) without added yeasts, preserving varietal character. Wines display black fruit aromas with dry, earthy palate notes of ripe strawberry and raspberry, with characteristic funkiness from natural fermentation.

## Notes

**Historic town:** Sabbioneta built by Gonzaga family in Renaissance (UNESCO site). Fermentation occurs in bottle without temperature control or inoculated yeasts, honoring ancestral sparkling-wine tradition. Dry, mineral profile distinguishes regional Lambrusco from sweeter commercial versions. Bone-dry style with natural spice and ripe fruit reflects quality-oriented viticulture.
