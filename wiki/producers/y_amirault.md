# Y. Amirault

Bourgueil-based organic producer. 12 bottles reflects significant cellar commitment. Yannick Amirault has established himself as one of Bourgueil's finest contemporary producers—organic viticulture emphasizing terroir and minimal intervention. Known for elegant, mineral-driven Cab Franc with distinctive limestone tension.

## Notes

Young generation producer receiving high critical acclaim (Vinous/Wine Advocate 88-92 range). Amirault's wines show excellent aging potential (12-18 years) and elegant food-pairing character. Sandstone/clay/limestone terroirs produce wines with acidity and structure that age beautifully. Represents emerging Loire quality alongside traditional names.
