# Susana Balbo Wines

Winemaker: **Susana Balbo & José Lovaglio Balbo**. Agrelo, Luján de Cuyo (Mendoza). Category: **Artisan / Terroir-Driven**.

## Approach

Argentina's first female winemaker (graduated 1981), founder of the winery in 1999. Credited with creating Argentina's first world-class Torrontés in the early 1980s in Cafayate. Family-run with son José (UC Davis) now leading R&D; deeply sustainability-focused with organic vineyard management. Tim Atkin Winemaking Legend 2020. Michelin Green Star for the restaurant Osadía de Crear (2024 & 2025). Confirmed at Argentina Reloaded Buenos Aires 2025 with son José.

## Grapes

Torrontés, Malbec, Cabernet Sauvignon, Semillon, Cab Franc

## Key Wines

- BenMarco Expresivo
- Susana Balbo Brioso Blend
- Susana Balbo Signature Torrontés
- Crios Torrontés
