# Les Crêtes

Boutique producer in Aymavilles, Valle d'Aosta. Les Crêtes is recognized for distinctive alpine white wines and mineral-driven reds reflecting the high-altitude, limestone-rich terroir of the region.

## Terroir & Appellation

**Aymavilles, Valle d'Aosta** — Mountain elevation with limestone and schist soils; cool alpine climate producing elegant, mineral-focused whites and structured reds.
