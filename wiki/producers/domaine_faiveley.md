# Domaine Faiveley

Large domaine/négociant with extensive holdings across both Côte de Nuits Grand Crus (Latricières, Mazis, Chambolle Bonnes Mares, Clos de Vougeot, Échezeaux) and Côte Chalonnaise. **Mercurey is the heart of the estate** — multiple 1er Crus including Clos du Roy, Clos des Myglands (monopole), Clos Rochette, La Framboisière.
