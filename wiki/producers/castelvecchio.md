# Castelvecchio

The largest estate of the Isonzo-side Carso (Sagrado, ~35 ha around a historic
villa) — unusually red-leaning for the plateau: Terrano, Refosco, Cabernet
Sauvignon, plus Malvasia and Vitovska in white. Sturdier, more conventional idiom
than the Kante/Zidarich school. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli
enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
