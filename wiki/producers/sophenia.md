# Sophenia

Winemaker: **Matías Garces Silva**. Gualtallary, Tupungato (Mendoza). Category: **Artisan / Terroir-Driven**.

## Approach

Estate winery in Gualtallary with 200ha+ under vine at 1,200–1,500m. Sophenia was one of the early serious investors in high-altitude Gualtallary and has since developed a single-vineyard program. Synthesis is the premium tier, with estate Malbec and Cab Franc selected from the best parcels. Featured at Argentina Reloaded Rio de Janeiro 2024.

## Grapes

Malbec, Cabernet Franc, Cabernet Sauvignon, Chardonnay

## Key Wines

- Sophenia Synthesis Malbec
- Sophenia Synthesis Cab Franc
- Sophenia Altosur Malbec
