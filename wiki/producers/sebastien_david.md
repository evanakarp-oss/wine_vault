# Sébastien David

Chinon-based organic producer with 8 bottles in cellar. Emerging producer focusing on distinctive, mineral-driven Cab Franc from clay/slate terroirs. David's approach emphasizes natural fermentations and minimal intervention.

## Notes

Young generation Chinon producer gaining reputation for quality and site expression. Wines show elegant structure and mineral character—limestone-driven rather than power-focused. Built for 12-15 year aging. Emerging producer with significant potential; good value at current market positioning.
