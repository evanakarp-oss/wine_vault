# Paolo Caccese

Tiny (~6 ha) idiosyncratic estate on Pradis hill, Cormons — a lawyer-turned-vigneron
cult favorite locally, farming an old-fashioned mixed set: Friulano, Malvasia, Pinot
Bianco, Ribolla, plus rarities like Müller-Thurgau and Traminer. Old-school unoaked
whites, modest prices, near-zero export profile. Surfaced for the vault via the
2026-07 Friuli enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
