# Thörle

Saulheim brothers Christoph and Johannes Thörle — one of the deeper Rheinhessen books at any retailer, spanning GG Riesling (Hölle, Schlossberg, Probstey), GG Spätburgunder (Hölle, Probstey), GG Silvaner (Probstey), Saulheim Spätburgunder Kalkstein at the entry tier, plus a traditional-method Blanc de Blancs Brut. Many spelling variants in the raw data — Holle / Probstey / Saulheimer / Schlossberg are vineyard names that got parsed as producer.
