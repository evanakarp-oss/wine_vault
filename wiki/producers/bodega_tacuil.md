# Bodega Tacuil

Winemaker: **Raúl Yeyé Dávalos**. Calchaquí Valley, Salta (Salta). Category: **Low Intervention**.

## Approach

One of the most storied family estates in Salta's upper Calchaquí Valley — the Dávalos family has farmed these vineyards for generations at altitude. Raúl 'Yeyé' Dávalos represents the custodian generation of a singular terroir. Bodega Colomé's 'Auténtico' is made as a homage to Raúl Dávalos Senior, who was famous for hating oak-aged wines. Old-vine, low-intervention, mountain-altitude wines. Featured at Argentina Reloaded Buenos Aires 2025.

## Grapes

Malbec, Cabernet Sauvignon, Torrontés, Tannat
