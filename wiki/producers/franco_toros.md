# Franco Toros

Polished benchmark grower on the Novali hill above Cormons. His **Pinot Bianco** is
routinely cited as the best in Italy (a perennial Tre Bicchieri), alongside Friulano,
Chardonnay, Pinot Grigio and a dense, oak-raised **Merlot Selezione**. Style is
precise and concentrated rather than rustic — classic-modern Collio at grower scale.
Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
