# Jean-Maurice Raffault

Chinon producer with 11 bottles indicating solid portfolio presence. Established reputation for elegant, structured Cab Franc from diverse terroirs. Raffault's approach balances traditional Chinon character (mineral, herbaceous) with ripe fruit and approachable aging curve.

## Notes

Mid-tier Loire producer (between commercial and avant-garde grower scale). Known for consistent quality across range—Les Picasses, Cabernet Franc are workhorse bottlings; Peuilly offers more structure and aging potential. Wines typically approachable at 5-7 years, improve through 15-20 year window. Reliable value producer.
