# Huichaira Vineyards

Winemaker: **Ale Sejanovich & Jeff Mausbach**. Quebrada de Humahuaca, Jujuy (Jujuy). Category: **Low Intervention**.

## Approach

Ale Sejanovich and Jeff Mausbach source from small growers in the extreme-altitude gorge of Humahuaca in Jujuy (2,500–3,000m+). The soils are arid and stony, yields tiny. Jujuy's high UV, dramatic diurnal swings, and ancient alluvial soils produce co-fermented reds of unusual linearity: deep color, low pH, high natural acidity. Sejanovich quoted in James Suckling 2025: the altitude 'helps produce deep-colored wines with structure, low pH, and high natural acidity, creating uniquely balanced profiles.' The Cielo Arriba is a Malbec/Cab Franc/Syrah co-fermentation.

## Grapes

Malbec, Cabernet Franc, Syrah, Criolla Chica
