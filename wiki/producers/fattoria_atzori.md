# Fattoria Atzori

Small organic estate in southern Sardinia (Cagliari province), practicing sustainable viticulture on approximately 15 hectares of vineyard. The Atzori family has farmed organically for over two decades, focusing on native Sardinian varieties in their natural Mediterranean context. Their production emphasizes Cannonau (Grenache) and Vermentino, representing the island's terroir with minimal intervention in the vineyard and winery.

## Notes

Fattoria Atzori represents the artisan-scale, organic-farming movement within Sardinian wine production. Their wines show the characteristic mineral-driven profile of southern Sardinia, with Cannonau displaying red stone fruit and herbal notes, and Vermentino offering salinity and citrus complexity. As a micro-producer, Atzori prioritizes quality over quantity and maintains direct relationships with sommeliers and wine lovers seeking authentic, estate-bottled Sardinian expressions.
