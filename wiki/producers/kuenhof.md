# Kuenhof

Benchmark Isarco Valley producer, estate dating to 12th century, in Pliger family hands for ~200 years. Located near Bressanone northeast of Bolzano, operating on steep terraced south-east-facing slopes (550–890m altitude). Organic and biodynamic certified; focuses on native varieties: Sylvaner, Riesling, Veltliner, Gewürztraminer, Kerner. Fermentation with natural yeasts in historic cellar (protected monument). Simon Pliger assumed leadership 2025, parents remain involved.

## Notes

**Alpine terroir:** Steep Isarco Valley slopes create altitude advantage (550–890m) for crisp whites. Natural-yeast fermentation method produces exceptionally long-lived, digestible wines reflecting maritime-continental convergence. Historic cellar now monument reinforces cultural depth. Four-decade family stewardship across generational transition.
