# El Bayeh

Winemaker: **Daniel Manzur + Matías Michelini**. Quebrada de Humahuaca (Jujuy). Category: **Radical Natural**.

## Approach

Rescuing and reviving criolla varieties from century-old pergola-trained vines irrigated by traditional acequias. Whole cluster, native yeasts, low alcohol, zero sulfites. Named best discovery of 2024 by multiple Argentine sommeliers. #40 James Suckling Top 100 Argentina 2025.

## Grapes

Criolla Chica, Criolla Grande, Moscatel Rosado

## Key Wines

- Viñas Elegidas Don Pilar
- Pequeños Parceleros de la Quebrada Maimará
- Pequeños Parceleros Tilcara
