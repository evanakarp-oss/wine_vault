# Du Cropio

Small family winery in Vibo Valentia, Calabria, working with Gaglioppo and other native Calabrian varieties in the region's hilly interior. Du Cropio operates on modest vineyard parcels (approximately 10-15 hectares) using sustainable farming practices. The winery represents the artisanal producer model of Calabria, where multi-generational families maintain traditional viticulture methods adapted to the region's challenging, mountainous terrain.

## Notes

Du Cropio exemplifies Calabria's authentic winemaking tradition, producing Gaglioppo-based wines that reflect both varietal character and regional terroir. Positioned in Vibo Valentia, one of Calabria's historic wine zones, the producer contributes to the region's gradual recovery as a quality wine area. Their wines show the structured, mineral-driven profile typical of Calabrian Gaglioppo grown in hill vineyards, with dark red fruit, herbal complexity, and tannin finesse. As part of Calabria's emerging producer network, Du Cropio helps establish the region's distinct identity beyond its historical role as a source of bulk wine for other Italian regions.
