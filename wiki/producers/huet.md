# Domaine Huet

_The benchmark estate of Vouvray, and one of the great Chenin Blanc addresses on earth. Three monopole-scale terroirs — Le Haut-Lieu, Le Mont, Clos du Bourg — each bottled across the sweetness spectrum (sec, demi-sec, moelleux, and the rare Cuvée Constance), all farmed biodynamically since 1990._

## Notes

Demeter-certified biodynamic, and a masterclass in how a single grape reads three terroirs. The wines age for decades in every register; the sweet Constance and moelleux are among the world's great dessert wines. The Cuvée Constance '93 9-pack ($72/btl), Le Haut-Lieu Moelleux '16 cases, and aged Clos du Bourg in the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] are cornerstone Loire buys.
