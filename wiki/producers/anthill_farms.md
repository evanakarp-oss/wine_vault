# Anthill Farms

_A cool-climate Pinot Noir (and Syrah) house founded by three Williams Selyem alumni, working a roster of far-Sonoma-Coast and Anderson Valley vineyards. Native-yeast ferments, whole-cluster, restrained oak, moderate alcohol — transparent, site-specific Pinot at prices that shame most of the North Coast._
