# Dr. Wehrheim

Birkweiler family estate — neighbors of Rebholz on the Kastanienbusch hill (the porphyry-pink-sandstone GG that is Pfalz's most distinctive grand cru). FASS book covers Kastanienbusch Riesling GG (multiple vintages), Mandelberg Weissburgunder GG, Sonnenschein Spätburgunder GG, and Chardonnay Roisenberg. Quietly biodynamic-leaning.
