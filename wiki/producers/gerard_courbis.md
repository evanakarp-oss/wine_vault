Saint-Joseph grower (no relation to the better-known Domaine Courbis at the southern end of the appellation). The Vieilles Vignes Saint-Joseph rouge anchors the book, with a small Cornas. Père & Fils era at FASS — Ludovic Courbis (his son) bottles his own Cornas separately.
