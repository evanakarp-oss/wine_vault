# Fattoria Fatalone

Organic estate in Taranto, southeastern Apulia, specializing in Primitivo di Manduria and Negroamaro—the signature red varieties of southern Puglia. The winery operates on approximately 25-30 hectares of organically farmed vineyard, certified under European organic standards. Fattoria Fatalone represents the modern quality-focused movement within Apulia, balancing the region's rich varietal heritage with sustainable farming practices and contemporary winemaking approaches.

## Notes

Fattoria Fatalone's commitment to organic viticulture in Apulia reflects the region's broader transition toward sustainable practices. Their Primitivo wines showcase the variety's naturally high-alcohol potential while maintaining freshness and structured tannins through careful harvest timing and extraction management. Negroamaro bottlings display the dark fruit, herbal spice, and elegant structure characteristic of quality southern Italian reds. The estate's focus on minimal-intervention winemaking allows the limestone and clay soils of Taranto to express themselves, offering wines with terroir authenticity and aging potential—a marked departure from the overripe, conventional profiles that once defined mass-produced Apulian wine.
