# Ronco Severo

Uncompromising biodynamic producer in Prepotto, Colli Orientali del Friuli, managed by Stefano Novello (enologist, 1988). Six hectares at 150m elevation on ponca terroir, planted with native Friuli whites (Chardonnay, Ribolla Gialla, Friulano, Picolit, Pinot Grigio) and distinctive reds (Schioppettino, Refosco dal Peduncolo Rosso, Merlot). Minimal-intervention philosophy: spontaneous fermentation, minimal sulfite, no additives, hand harvest, lunar bottling. Distributed in USA by Beaune Imports.

## History & Philosophy

Ronco Severo was established in 1968 when Stefano Novello's father decided to restore the ancient vineyards of the counts of Rieppi, eventually founding the winery. Stefano obtained his **enology degree in 1988** and immediately rejected conventional winemaking in favor of **biodynamic viticulture and minimal-intervention winemaking**.

**Core Philosophy**: Stefano uses **no chemical products in the vineyards** and follows a **non-interventionalist approach** in the cellar. Wines are **macerated on their skins for extended periods**, remain **unfiltered and unfined**, and undergo minimal sulfite additions before bottling. The result is a portfolio of wines that express place and vintage with remarkable immediacy and honesty.

## Vineyard & Terroir

Located in Prepotto (province of Udine), the six-hectare vineyard sits at approximately **150 metres above sea level**, with terroir shaped by **ponca soils** (sandstone marls in local dialect). This distinctive geology imparts siliceous minerality and structural tension to the wines.

## Grape Varieties

**White Varieties**:
- Chardonnay
- Ribolla Gialla
- Friulano (Sauvignonasse)
- Picolit (rare local variety)
- Pinot Grigio

**Red Varieties**:
- Schioppettino (hyperlocal Friuli specialty)
- Refosco dal Peduncolo Rosso
- Merlot

## Winemaking Practice

- **Spontaneous fermentation** using wild vineyard yeasts
- **Minimal sulfite dosage** before bottling only
- **No additives of any kind** in the cellar
- Hand-picking at harvest
- Aging in **large Slavonian oak barrels or barriques** (23–35 months depending on wine type)
- **Bottles under a waning moon** (biodynamic practice)
- **Unfiltered and unfined**

## Master of Schioppettino

Ronco Severo is recognized as a master of **Schioppettino**, one of Friuli's rarest and most distinctive red grapes—a signature expression of Colli Orientali's terroir and Stefano's minimal-intervention philosophy.
