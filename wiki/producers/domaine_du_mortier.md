# Domaine du Mortier

Bourgueil-based organic producer. 9 bottles suggests solid cellar presence. Mortier focuses on traditional, site-driven Cab Franc emphasizing natural expression of Bourgueil's distinctive terroirs.

## Notes

Quality-focused grower-scale producer. Wines show distinctive Bourgueil character—lighter-bodied than Chinon, emphasizing acidity and mineral tension. Built for 10-15 year aging with excellent food-pairing potential. Represents the emerging generation of quality-focused Loire producers.
