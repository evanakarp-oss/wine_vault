# Vodopivec

Small family winery founded 1997 by brothers Paolo and Valter Vodopivec, north Trieste near Sgonico, Carso region bordering Slovenia. Radical focus: Vitovska single-varietal production only. Biodynamic farming (no pesticides, fertilizers, industrial yeasts). Wild fermentation with long skin contact in amphora, zero additions (no sulfur, yeast, stabilization, temperature control). Paolo's pursuit of single-varietal mastery reflects terroir obsession. Vitovska found nowhere else in Italy but Carso region—preservation focus represents territorial stewardship.

## Notes

**Single-varietal philosophy:** Vodopivec's exclusive Vitovska focus contrasts typical multi-wine portfolio approach. Amphora fermentation—ancient technique revival—creates extended skin contact, amber-wine color, complex natural texture. Vitovska rarity outside Carso makes producer crucial to regional identity preservation. Zero-intervention winemaking (no SO2 at any stage) represents radical naturalism.
