# Yann Durieux (Recrue des Sens)

A leading figure in natural Burgundy. Yann Durieux trained in the cellar at
Domaine Prieuré Roch before founding Recrue des Sens in the Hautes-Côtes de
Nuits (around Messanges). Biodynamic/organic farming and minimal-intervention
winemaking — indigenous yeast, no added SO2 — across Aligoté (the cult "Love
and Pif"), Pinot Noir and Chardonnay bottlings. Imported to the US via Wine
Source.
