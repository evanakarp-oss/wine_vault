# François Chidaine

_Reference-point Chenin Blanc grower straddling Montlouis and Vouvray, farming biodynamically (Demeter). Dry, chalk-driven, ageworthy wines — the 'les Argiles' sec is the calling card. A benchmark for terroir-transparent Loire Chenin._
