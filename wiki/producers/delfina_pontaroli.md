# Delfina Pontaroli

Winemaker: **Delfina Pontaroli**. Valle de Uco (Mendoza). Category: **Emerging**.

Emerging winemaker from Mendoza's next generation with a focus on freshness and low-intervention technique. Paz Levinson has included her in the Argentina Reloaded circuit as a producer bringing a distinct new voice to Uco Valley terroir.
