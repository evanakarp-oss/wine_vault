# Jermann

Silvio Jermann made Friuli's most famous modern whites: **Vintage Tunina** (the
pioneering 1975 field-blend of Sauvignon/Chardonnay/Ribolla/Malvasia/Picolit),
**Capo Martino**, and the Chardonnay "W... Dreams". Majority ownership passed to
Antinori in 2021. Polished, rich, internationally styled — historically important
more than terroir-austere. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca
list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]); also the only Friuli producer
with real Raeders shelf presence.
