# Tenute Deperu

Estate winery in northern Sardinia (Sassari province), established in the late 20th century by the Deperu family. The property encompasses approximately 20 hectares of vineyard planted to Cannonau and Vermentino, situated in the granitic and volcanic soils characteristic of northern Sardinia. Tenute Deperu works with sustainable vineyard practices to express the mineral character of the Sassari terroir.

## Notes

Tenute Deperu exemplifies the family-scale Sardinian producer focused on varietal authenticity and regional character. Their Cannonau wines display the structured, mineral-driven profile of northern Sardinia, while Vermentino offerings showcase crisp acidity and coastal-influenced salinity. The estate's positioning in Sassari, historically a hub of Sardinian wine culture, reflects the region's winemaking traditions and the producer's commitment to sustainable practices in preserving indigenous varieties.
