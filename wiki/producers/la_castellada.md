# La Castellada

The third pillar of the Oslavia school alongside Gravner and Radikon. The Bensa
brothers (Giorgio "Jordi" and Nicolò, now Nicolò's sons Stefano and Matteo) farm
~10 ha of Ribolla, Friulano, Chardonnay, Sauvignon and Merlot on Oslavia's ponca
marl, with long macerations for the whites and extended large-oak aging — slightly
more polished and less extreme than Radikon, released late. Surfaced for the vault
via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).

## Notes

**On the 2026-07 Friuli list:** Rosso della Castellada 1997 and 1999 magnums
(€180–190) — mature Merlot/Cab from Oslavia, a rare sight.

**Cellar fit.** Strong — Oslavia natural school with more restraint than its
neighbors; the whites (Ribolla, Bianco della Castellada, Friulano) age 15+ years.
Aged bottles like those listed magnums are exactly the drink-now format for this
school.
