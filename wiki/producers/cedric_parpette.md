# Cédric Parpette

Côte-Rôtie producer bottling independently since 2006 (previously component of Guigal's La Turque blend). Guide Hachette awarded a Coup de Cœur, describing his wine as a "vin rare" and noting: "Already remarkable, this bottle will improve over the next two or three years and can keep for ten years. A domaine to watch. Coup de Cœur."

## Notes

Organic viticulture in Côte-Rôtie. Parpette's independent releases show structured, mineral-driven Syrah emphasizing site character over new oak or high alcohol. Built for 15-20 year aging. Small production and emerging reputation create strong demand.
