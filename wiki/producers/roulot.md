# Domaine Roulot

_The benchmark for saline, chiselled Meursault. Jean-Marc Roulot bottles a celebrated array of village lieux-dits (Les Tessons/Clos de Mon Plaisir, Les Luchets, Les Meix Chavaux, Les Tillets) and premiers crus (Perrières, Charmes, Bouchères) in a taut, low-oxidation, terroir-transparent style._

## Notes

Practicing-organic, precise, tension-driven whites that map the Meursault map lieu-dit by lieu-dit — the antithesis of buttery Meursault, and a cornerstone of the modern white-Burgundy canon. The village Meursault ('23, ~$90) and Clos du Haut Tesson in the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] are the entry points.
