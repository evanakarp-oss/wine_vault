# Domaine de Pallus

Bourgueil-based organic producer with 9 bottles in cellar. Small-scale operation emphasizing natural viticulture and minimal intervention winemaking. Pallus produces distinctive, mineral-driven Cab Franc built for medium-term aging (10-15 years).

## Notes

Emerging organic producer gaining reputation among Loire specialists. Pallus shows the lighter, more elegant Bourgueil style—emphasizing acidity, minerality, and food pairing over power. Consistent quality and organic farming practices make this an attractive addition to Loire-focused cellar.
