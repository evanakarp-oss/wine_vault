# Ronco del Gnemiz

Serena Palazzolo and Christian Patat's hillside estate at San Giovanni al Natisone —
the most Burgundian address in Friuli: parcel-bottled **Chardonnays** (Ronco del
Gnemiz among Italy's best), vibrant Sauvignons (Peri, Salici), Friulano San Zuan,
and the long-lived **Rosso del Gnemiz** (Merlot/Cabernet). Organic farming, small
barrels, low yields. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]), which even offers a 1988 Rosso
magnum.
