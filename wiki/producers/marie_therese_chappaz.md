# Marie-Thérèse Chappaz

Valais-based biodynamic producer known as "the Queen of the Valais." Olivier Poussier declared her Petite Arvines "worthy of the greatest white wines of Europe."

## Notes

Demeter-certified biodynamic viticulture in Fully (Valais), Switzerland's premier white wine region. Chappaz specializes in Petite Arvine—an indigenous variety built for aging. Her wines emphasize mineral expression, herbaceous complexity, and food-pairing versatility. Petite Arvines from top vintages show 10-15 year aging potential comparable to Savoie Jacquière. Unique access to Alpine terroir and biodynamic philosophy makes this a distinctive collector's choice.
