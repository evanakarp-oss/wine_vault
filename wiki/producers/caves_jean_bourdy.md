# Caves Jean Bourdy

Twelfth-generation Jura house in Arlay — the historic cellar of Château-Chalon and Vin Jaune. FASS book: Château-Chalon 2017/2018 ($90–$92), Vin Jaune 2018 ($80–$87). The Bourdy name is one of the Jura's oldest continuously operating domaines and the standard for traditional, oxidative-aged Savagnin.
