# Vignai da Duline

Lorenzo Mocchiutti and Federica Magrini's fiercely ecological estate — old vines
(some pre-phylloxera-era plantings at Duline and Ronco Pitotti), permanent cover
crops, no tillage, no new oak. **Morus Nigra** (old-vine Refosco), **Ronco Pitotti
Chardonnay**, Schioppettino and Malvasia are signatures: taut, saline, deliberately
anti-opulent. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]); also name-checked in a CSW Barolo
piece.
