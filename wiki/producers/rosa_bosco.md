# Rosa Bosco

Rosa Bosco's tiny two-wine estate at Manzano, founded after her years at Abbazia di
Rosazzo (with consultation lineage back to the Felluga orbit): a barrel-fermented
**Sauvignon Blanc** and the **Boscorosso** Merlot. Cult-small, low profile, high
polish. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]), garbled as "Forest Rose".
