# Borgo del Tiglio

Nicola Manferrari — a pharmacist who took over the family estate in Brazzano di
Cormons in 1981 — makes what many consider the most Burgundian, age-worthy whites in
Collio: low yields, native yeasts, fermentation and aging in 250L French oak.
**Ronco della Chiesa** (old-vine Friulano) and **Studio di Bianco**
(Friulano/Riesling/Sauvignon) are the summits; also Rossa della Centa (Merlot).
Chambers profiled his aged wines admiringly. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07
Friuli enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]), garbled there
as "Linden Village" (borgo = village, tiglio = linden).
