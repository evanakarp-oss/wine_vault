# Domaine Prieuré Roch

Cult biodynamic/natural domaine in Nuits-Saint-Georges, founded 1988 by
Henri-Frédéric Roch — co-director of Domaine de la Romanée-Conti. Whole-cluster
fermentation, indigenous yeast, old/neutral oak, and famously minimal sulfur
(little to none until bottling). Holdings span monopoles and premier/grand cru
parcels (e.g. Le Clos des Corvées in Nuits-Saint-Georges, Vosne-Romanée Les
Suchots, Clos de Bèze). Roch died in 2018; the team has continued the
non-interventionist house style. Wines are ethereal, whole-bunch-perfumed and
built for long aging. Imported to the US via Wine Source.
