# Guttarolo

Small-scale family producer in Salento, the heel of Apulia's boot, focusing on traditional Primitivo di Manduria and Negroamaro wines. The winery operates on modest vineyard holdings (approximately 10-15 hectares), practicing conventional viticulture in the warm, Mediterranean climate of southern Puglia. Guttarolo represents the traditional family winemaking model of the Salento region, with roots in multi-generational viticulture.

## Notes

Guttarolo exemplifies the traditional approach to Apulian winemaking, where Primitivo's natural ripeness and Negroamaro's structural tannins define the regional identity. The producer's focus on varietal expression and respect for Salento's hot-climate terroir yields wines with the ripe dark fruit, spice, and mineral undertones characteristic of authentic southern Italian reds. As part of the Salento wine community, Guttarolo contributes to the region's emerging reputation for quality wine production, moving beyond simple bulk-wine production toward curated, site-specific bottlings.
