# Domaine Jean-Baptiste Boudier

Fifth-generation Pernand-Vergelesses grower who launched his own ~6 ha estate in 2015 after apprenticeships with Nicolas Rossignol, Vieux Télégraphe, Gauby and Haut-Brion. Responsible farming and a light hand in the cellar, favouring infusion over extraction across Pernand, Aloxe-Corton and Savigny.
