# Radikon

Oslavia pioneer of extended skin-contact whites and zero-added-sulfur winemaking —
per Chambers "the best of their type - very consistent, fascinating wines, with
finesse." Stanko Radikon bottled the estate's first wines in 1979, began macerating
Ribolla Gialla in 1995 (Oslavje followed in 1997), and settled on ~3-month skin
contact with no sulfites. Children Saša and Ivana continue the estate. In the cellar
already (2008 Ribolla Gialla); page created during the 2026-07 Friuli pass
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]) — Radikon is *not* on that enoteca
list, which is a gap on their side, not ours.

## Notes

**Lineup.** Ribolla Gialla, Oslavje (Chardonnay/Sauvignon), Jakot (Friulano), Slatnik,
plus the "S" line (shorter maceration, lightly sulfured, earlier-drinking). Merlot and
Pignoli in red. Distinctive 500ml/1L bottles with narrow necks, designed for low-sulfur
aging.

**Cellar fit.** Already validated by ownership; the 2008 Ribolla in the cellar is at the
end of its stated window and should be opened soon. CSW carries returning stock
regularly — the natural restock channel.
