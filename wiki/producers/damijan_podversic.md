# Damijan Podversic

Gorizia grower (vineyards on Monte Calvario) and the most prominent disciple of
[[gravner|Josko Gravner]], who mentored him. ~90-day skin macerations in open oak
vats, no temperature control, long aging in big botti, no filtering. Ribolla Gialla,
Malvasia, Friulano, **Kaplja** (Chardonnay/Friulano/Malvasia) and **Prelit**
(Merlot/Cab) are the core. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca
list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
