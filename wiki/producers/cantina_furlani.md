# Cantina Furlani

Fourth-generation biodynamic winemaker Matteo Furlani (700m elevation, Povo area, Trento) operates small plots around Dolomite slopes, specializing in spontaneous-fermentation sparkling wines (frizzante, pet-nat styles). Cultivates local varieties—Lagarino, Verdarbara, Nosiola—plus Pinot Noir and Chardonnay. Uncompromising biodynamic approach; unfined and unfiltered production with minimal added sulfites. Wild-yeast fermentation only.

## Notes

**Sparkling focus:** Frizzante and pet-nat production (ancestral method, wild fermentation) marks departure from Franciacorta-style Metodo Classico. Orange wines (skin contact) and natural complexity from minimal-intervention vinification. Fourth-generation Matteo Furlani stewardship reflects family predisposition toward biodynamic philosophy dating to 1995 organic certification. High-altitude (700m) Dolomite vineyard setting near Trento creates cool-climate natural acidity favoring fresh expressions. Approximately 6 hectares cultivated biodynamically, producing 30 products averaging $10-34 retail (vermouth at $39/750ml), with distribution across USA, Canada, and Europe via Jenny & François Selections. Unfined, unfiltered production with minimal added sulfites and native-yeast fermentation embodies minimal-intervention natural wine ethos while maintaining consistency across sparkling and still wine formats.
