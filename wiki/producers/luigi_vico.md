# Luigi Vico

Tiny certified-organic micro-grower in Serralunga d'Alba — just ~2.1 ha — whose
estate (Serralunga Casa Mia) traces family landholdings back to 1694 but whose
first commercial harvest under Luigi's own name came in 2016. Limited production
of Barolo del Comune di Serralunga d'Alba and a single-cru Barolo Prapò, made
with the sole imperative of expressing the site. Serralunga's structure with a
lighter, more lifted hand — JP's "more elegant but structured." Imported to the
US by North Berkeley Imports.
