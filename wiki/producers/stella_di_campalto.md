# Stella di Campalto

Pioneering organic (1996) and biodynamic (2005) Brunello di Montalcino producer in Castelnuovo dell'Abate, Montalcino DOCG. 7.5–7.7 hectares of vineyard on 13-hectare estate. 100% Sangiovese, organically and biodynamically farmed, uncompromising quality (14,000–15,000 bottles/year); unfiltered and unfined, bottled under waning moon, each plot vinified separately as distinct cru. Imported by North Berkeley Imports (Berkeley, CA; Importer of the Year 2019, 2020, 2022, 2023).

## History & Philosophy

Stella di Campalto established her winery in the mid-1990s in Castelnuovo dell'Abate, the **southeastern subzone of Brunello di Montalcino DOCG**. She achieved **organic certification in 1996** (nearly immediately at founding) and **biodynamic certification in 2005**, placing her among Montalcino's earliest and most committed organic and biodynamic practitioners.

**Core Philosophy**: Stella di Campalto is renowned for her **uncompromising commitment to purity and authenticity**. She proudly asserts that:
- Each vintage will **always be 100% Sangiovese**
- What is in the bottle will **always be 100% from the vintage on the label** (no blending across vintages)
- Wines are **never fined or filtered**, preserving maximum phenolic and aromatic complexity
- Early bottlings were even **filled by hand, one by one**, reflecting the artisanal scale and care

**Regional Distinction**: Among Montalcino's producers, Stella di Campalto stands out for achieving world-class Brunello through organic and biodynamic farming—a technical and philosophical achievement requiring exceptional skill.

## Vineyard & Terroir

The estate encompasses 13 hectares, of which **7.5–7.7 hectares are planted to vineyards**. Located in **Castelnuovo dell'Abate** (the southeastern subzone of Brunello di Montalcino), the vineyard benefits from distinctive terroir shaped by Tuscany's limestone and clay geology.

## Farming & Biodynamic Practice

**Organic Certification (1996)**: The vineyard has been certified organic from near-founding, avoiding synthetic pesticides, herbicides, and fertilizers.

**Biodynamic Certification (2005)**: Stella di Campalto practices **full biodynamic viticulture**, including:
- Following the **phases of the moon** to sow nearly **25 different seed species** that compose the cover crops between vineyard rows
- Producing and utilizing **biodynamic preparations** in collaboration with four other biodynamic producers in Montalcino
- Conducting all operations in alignment with lunar and seasonal rhythms

## Winemaking Practice

- **Each plot treated as a distinct cru**, vinified separately in wooden vats
- **Organic and biodynamic farming** throughout the growing season
- **Hand harvest** at full physiological ripeness
- **Spontaneous fermentation** with native yeasts
- **Minimal sulfite addition** before bottling only
- **Unfiltered and unfined** to preserve phenolic structure and authenticity
- **Bottled under a waning moon** (biodynamic practice)

## Production Scale & Quality

With **annual production around 14,000–15,000 bottles of Brunello**, Stella di Campalto is **among Montalcino's most uncompromising quality-focused producers**. The low production volume reflects her commitment to small-scale, hand-managed viticulture and her refusal to compromise on quality for market demand.

## Key Wines

- **Brunello di Montalcino** (flagship; 100% Sangiovese, unfiltered/unfined, biodynamic)
- **Brunello di Montalcino Bosco** (distinct cru bottling)
- Limited production bottlings from individual vineyard parcels

## US Importer

**North Berkeley Imports** (Berkeley, California) is the exclusive USA importer for Stella di Campalto. North Berkeley Imports has been recognized as "Importer of the Year" by Wine & Spirits Magazine in **2019, 2020, 2022, and 2023**—a testament to their commitment to small-scale, natural, and biodynamic European producers.
