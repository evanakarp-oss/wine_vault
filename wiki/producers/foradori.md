# Foradori

Benchmark Teroldego producer founded 1901, family-owned since 1939, led by Elisabetta Foradori since 1984 (children Theo, Myrtha, Emilio Zierock increasingly active). Operates 24 hectares owned plus 8 hectares leased across multiple small plots around Campo Rotaliano plain (Trento-Alto Adige), with ~75% planted to Teroldego. Biodynamic farmed since 2002, Demeter-certified 2009. Minimal-intervention philosophy: indigenous yeasts, no temperature control, unfiltered bottling.

## Notes

**Alpine terroir:** Dolomites location (100km north of Verona) creates dramatic site expressions. Teroldego—regional red grape with high acid, silica-rich soils—benefits from biodynamic protocols. Low-sulfur winemaking and spontaneous fermentation preserve varietal minerality. Multi-generational family operation reflects long-term terroir commitment.
