# Domaine Takahiko

Cult natural-wine domaine in Yoichi, Hokkaido, founded 2010 by Takahiko Soga
(formerly of Coco Farm & Winery). Specialises in Pinot Noir from the
"Nana-Tsu-Mori" vineyard — whole-cluster, native-yeast, minimal-sulfur
winemaking that has made it the most sought-after red in Japan. Imported to
the US via Wine Source.
