# Bruno Giacosa

Historic Alba-based producer in Piemonte. Bruno Giacosa is a legendary name in Barolo and Barbaresco, known for traditional, structured Nebbiolo-based reds from the Alba region.

## Terroir & Appellation

**Alba, Piemonte** — Piedmont foothills with limestone and clay soils; cool alpine climate producing age-worthy Barolo and elegant Barbaresco.

## Notes

**Vault Status:** Stub profile from Joe Campanale's comprehensive Italian wine book. Listed as an exceptional producer; requires fuller research for current farming methods, key cuvées, and winemaking philosophy.
