# Domaine Georges Roumier

The Chambolle-Musigny reference. Christophe Roumier runs the estate. Top cuvée: **Bonnes Mares**. Also makes **Musigny** (tiny holding), **Chambolle-Musigny 1er Cru Les Amoureuses** (one of the most expensive non-Grand Crus in Burgundy), **Chambolle-Musigny 1er Cru Les Cras**, plus Morey-Saint-Denis Clos de la Bussière (monopole) and Ruchottes-Chambertin.
