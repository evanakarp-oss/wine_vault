# Gilles Berlioz

Savoie's most influential biodynamic vigneron — Berlioz's Chignin-Bergeron (Roussanne) bottlings, Les Christines ($97) and Résilience ($63), are the appellation's reference. Mineral, age-worthy, totally hand-farmed. Adjacent stylistically to Dard & Ribo and the Northern Rhône naturals.
