# Julien Cruchandeau

Côte de Nuits grower based in the Hautes-Côtes — Ladoix Les Ranches, Bouzeron Cuvée Massale (Aligoté), Saint-Aubin 1er Cru L'Amandier, Auxey-Duresses 1er Cru Les Duresses, Nuits-Saint-Georges Aux Saint-Jacques VV. Quietly biodynamic-leaning and almost entirely sub-$70. The kind of grower FASS finds in the Burgundy second tier.
