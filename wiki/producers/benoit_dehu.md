# Champagne Benoît Déhu

Biodynamic grower Champagne in Fossoy, in the western Vallée de la Marne, with
a focus on old-vine Meunier (notably the single-vineyard "La Rue des Noyers").
Whole-cluster pressing, barrel work and low dosage in a vinous, terroir-driven
style. Imported to the US via Wine Source.
