# Champagne Bénoit Déhu

Avize-based grower producing compelling Extra Brut cuvées. Antonio Galloni wrote in Vinous (December 2017): "Benoit Déhu's NV Extra Brut La Rue des Noyers has established itself as one of the most compelling wines in Champagne since it was first released with the 2011 vintage."

## Notes

Organic viticulture in Avize (Grand Cru Chardonnay village). La Rue des Noyers (Extra Brut, no dosage) represents core holding—linear, mineral-driven, food-focused. Consistent quality and compelling pricing make this a standout value in contemporary Champagne.
