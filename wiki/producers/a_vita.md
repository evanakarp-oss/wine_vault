# A Vita

Organic micro-producer in Cosenza, Calabria, specializing in Gaglioppo—Calabria's native red variety. Operating on small vineyard holdings (approximately 8-10 hectares), A Vita practices certified organic viticulture in the mountainous Calabrian interior, where elevation and continental influences temper the Mediterranean climate. The winery exemplifies the emerging quality movement among Calabrian artisan producers committed to sustainable practices and varietal authenticity.

## Notes

A Vita represents the new generation of Calabrian winemakers recovering their region's indigenous grape heritage and terroir potential. Gaglioppo, often undervalued and confused with other southern Italian varieties, displays considerable finesse and aging potential in Calabria's cooler hill zones. Their organic approach preserves the variety's natural structure, yielding wines with dark red fruit, earthy undertones, and mineral tension. As a micro-production estate, A Vita prioritizes quality over volume and direct connections with wine enthusiasts seeking authentic, small-scale Calabrian expressions—part of the broader recovery of southern Italian wine identity beyond commodity production.
