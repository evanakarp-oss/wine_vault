# Château Bel Air-Marquis d'Aligre

Margaux Cru Bourgeois Supérieur (Boissard family) famous for its eccentric release policy — wines are held in cellar for 10–15 years before release. FASS list spans 1996 / 1998 / 2005 / 2009 / 2010 / 2011 / 2013 / 2015, all at $70–$88. Old-school Margaux: predominantly Cabernet Sauvignon, long barrel élevage, no new oak. Accepted as the aged-classic Bordeaux exception in the curation filters (2026-05-26).
