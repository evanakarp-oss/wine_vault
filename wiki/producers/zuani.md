# Zuani

Patrizia Felluga's (Marco Felluga's daughter) focused estate in San Floriano del
Collio, run with her children Antonio and Caterina. Essentially a one-blend house:
**Zuani Vigne** (stainless Collio Bianco — Friulano/Chardonnay/Pinot Grigio/
Sauvignon) and **Zuani Riserva** (the same blend, barrel-fermented), plus a Malvasia.
Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
