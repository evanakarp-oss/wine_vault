# Domaine Arnaud Chopin & Fils

Six-generation family domaine in Comblanchien at the southern edge of the Côte de Nuits, between Beaune and Nuits-Saint-Georges, now run by Arnaud Chopin with his brother Alban. ~14 ha of old-vine Pinot Noir; fresh, fine-boned reds topped by Nuits-Saint-Georges 1er Cru Aux Murgers.
