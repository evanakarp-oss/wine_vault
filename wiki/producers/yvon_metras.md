# Yvon Métras

_Iconic natural Fleurie grower — Gang-of-Four-adjacent, whole-cluster, zero/low-sulfur cru Beaujolais (Fleurie, L'Ultime, Moulin-à-Vent) that ages far better than its gulpable youth suggests. Tiny production, cult demand._
