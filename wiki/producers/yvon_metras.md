# Yvon Métras

Among the elder statesmen of natural Beaujolais — Métras farms Fleurie organically, ferments with native yeast, and ages without added SO2. Allocations are tight; FASS gets the Beaujolais Villages cuvée at sub-$50. The Fleurie cuvées rarely make it stateside in retail.
