# Lis Neris

Alvaro Pecorari's estate at San Lorenzo Isontino on the gravelly right bank of the
Isonzo. Known for serious, partly barrel-raised whites released with age: **Gris**
Pinot Grigio, **Jurosa** Chardonnay, Picol Sauvignon, La Vila Friulano, the Lis
blend, plus a Cabernet-based red and the Tal Lùc sweet Verduzzo. Consistent,
site-argued, mid-scale. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
