# Kante

Edi Kante is the father of the modern Carso — the man who dug a three-story cellar
into the karst limestone above Trieste and proved **Vitovska**, Malvasia, Sauvignon
and Chardonnay could be world-class from this brutal terrain. Mentor (directly or by
example) to Zidarich, Vodopivec and the whole Carso generation. Painter as well;
each vintage carries his artwork. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli
enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
