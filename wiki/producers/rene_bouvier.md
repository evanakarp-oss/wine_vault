# René Bouvier

Marsannay-rooted domaine making **Bourgogne / Marsannay Le Chapitre** (Chenôve — historic terroir, Marsannay AOC from 2019 vintage), **Marsannay Clos du Roy**, **Marsannay Ouzeloy**, plus Gevrey village + 1er Cru (Petite Chapelle), Gevrey "Racines du Temps" 70–80yo vines, and Charmes-Chambertin. CSW: "Bourgogne Chapitre is grown on a parcel in Chenove that defies the urban sprawl of Dijon… sensational value."

## CSW Write-ups

### ★ [Rene Bouvier 2006's, A Producer Under The Radar](https://chambersstwines.com/blogs/articles/rene-bouvier-2006-s-a-producer-under-the-radar)
*2006*

> Their Bourgogne "Chapitre" is lovely in 2006. It is grown on a parcel in Chenove that defies the urban sprawl of Dijon… concentrated with lovely spice and roundness. Great acidity and some prominent florality on the nose…

## Notes
