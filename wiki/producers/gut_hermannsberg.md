# Gut Hermannsberg

One of the historic Nahe estates (formerly state-owned Staatsweingut Niederhausen) — Kupfergrube, Hermannsberg, Felsenberg, and Traiser Bastei are the GG vineyards. FASS carries Kupfergrube GG Reserve 2020 ($109), Hermannsberg GG Reserve 2019, Bastei GG, Felsenberg GG, plus the entry-tier 7 Terroirs Trocken. Geological depth is the calling card — slate, porphyry, melaphyre across one estate.
