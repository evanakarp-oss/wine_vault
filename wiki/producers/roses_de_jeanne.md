# Champagne Roses de Jeanne

Côte des Blancs grower Champagne house producing spectacular, allocation-only cuvées. Antonio Galloni remarked: "These remain some of the most spectacular wines being made anywhere in the world, and are well worth the effort of finding them. Frankly, I can't think of too many wines I would rather drink."

## Notes

Small production, high demand. Biodynamic/organic viticulture on pure chalk. Minimal-intervention approach produces intensely mineral, tensioned Champagnes built for 15+ year aging. Brut Nature bottlings emphasize terroir over commercial dosage. Difficult to source in US; Thomas Kalder Selections represents one of few reliable access points.
