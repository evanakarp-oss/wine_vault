# Vietti

One of Piedmont's reference estates — Vietti's single-vineyard Barolo program (Rocche di Castiglione, Brunate, Lazzarito, Ravera, Cerequio, Villero) helped establish the modern cru framework. Cerequio 2019 at $297 is the FASS book entry — La Morra cru, classical Vietti style. Owned by Krause family since 2016 but maintains the Currado / Vaira winemaking continuity.
