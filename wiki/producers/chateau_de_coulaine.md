# Chateau de Coulaine

Historic Chinon château with 13 bottles in cellar. Biodynamic viticulture on diverse terroirs (clay, limestone, sand). Coulaine emphasizes old-vine Cab Franc (many vines 50+ years) with traditional winemaking—long maceration, natural fermentation, minimal new oak.

## Notes

One of Loire's historic estates now practicing serious biodynamic viticulture. Wines show distinctive power and structure compared to lighter Bourgueil producers—deep minerality, dark cherry, herbal complexity. Built for 15-20 year aging. Older vintages (2000s) show exceptional evolution and secondary development.
