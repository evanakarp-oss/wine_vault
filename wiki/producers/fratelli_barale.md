# Fratelli Barale

Historic estate in the commune of Barolo, sourcing from the Castellero (Barolo)
and Bussia (Monforte) crus. Resolutely traditional winemaking that has never
strayed; Chambers Street describes the wines as elegant and refined, reflecting
vintage and terroir — serious, age-worthy "vino da meditazione" reminiscent of
old Cappellano, not blockbusters. The old bottles age beautifully and recent
vintages have been going from strength to strength. One of the small
craft-driven farms JP flagged to visit.

## CSW Write-ups

### ★ [Piedmont Week - Thursday - De Forville and Barale](https://chambersstwines.com/blogs/articles/piedmont-week-thursday-de-forville-and-barale)
*2015-04*

> 4/9/15 - …We are great fans of their recent vintages (in fact they are going from strength-to-strength, with each new vintage surpassing the last)… As with most of our favorites, Barale has never strayed from resolutely traditional winemaking. The fruit for these older bottles came from Castellero and from Bussia, and… the Barales produced an elegant, refined wine that reflected the vintage and the terroir… their wine reminds us of old Cappellano – not blockbusters, serious in nature, vino da meditazione. — *Jamie Wolff*
