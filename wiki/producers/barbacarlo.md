# Barbacarlo

Legendary minimal-intervention winery in Broni (Oltrepò Pavese) operated by the Maga family for over 150 years. The Barbacarlo vineyard (4 ha, southwest-facing cru) was founded in 1886 on steeply sloped terrain surrounded by forest. Lino Maga produces two single-vineyard reds—Barbacarlo and Montebuono—from indigenous Oltrepò Pavese varieties, fermented in ancient casks with zero additions, bottled in spring with natural refermentation.

## Notes

**Cult producer:** Considered among Italy's finest, Barbacarlo exemplifies hand-worked, chemical-free viticulture. Wines fermented without temperature control, racked by moon cycles, bottled with no dosage or additives. Continental climate and deep limestone-clay soils produce mineral, age-worthy reds with natural complexity. Refermentation in bottle a signature technique.
