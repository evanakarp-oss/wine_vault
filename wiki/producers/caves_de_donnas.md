# Caves de Donnas

Alpine cooperative producer in Donnas, Valle d'Aosta. Caves de Donnas is a key producer of Nebbiolo-based reds from the high-altitude Donnas sub-region, known for structured, mineral-driven wines reflecting alpine terroir.

## Terroir & Appellation

**Donnas, Valle d'Aosta** — Alpine limestone and schist soils at elevation; cool-climate Nebbiolo producing high-acid, mineral-focused reds with aging potential.
