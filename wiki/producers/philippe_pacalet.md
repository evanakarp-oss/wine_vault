# Philippe Pacalet

Beaune-based négociant working in a natural idiom, founded 2001. Pacalet is the
nephew of Beaujolais natural-wine pioneer Marcel Lapierre and trained as an
oenologist; he was the winemaker at Domaine Prieuré Roch through the 1990s.
House style: whole-cluster fermentation, indigenous yeast, no added SO2 until
bottling, neutral oak, sourcing organically/biodynamically farmed fruit across
the Côte de Nuits and Côte de Beaune. Imported to the US via Wine Source.
