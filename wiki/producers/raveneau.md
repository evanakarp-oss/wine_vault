# Domaine Raveneau

_The most coveted name in Chablis. Jean-Marie and Isabelle Raveneau farm grand crus (Les Clos, Valmur, Blanchot) and a roster of top premiers crus (Montée de Tonnerre, Butteaux, Chapelot, Montmains, Forêts) into powerful, mineral, barrel-raised Chablis that ages for decades._

## Notes

Along with Dauvissat, the reference for age-worthy, terroir-driven Chablis — oak-raised but never oaky, built on Kimmeridgian tension. Allocation-only and chronically scarce. The village Chablis ('19/'21, $150–160) and Butteaux in the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] are the (relative) value entry; the grand crus are trophy-priced.
