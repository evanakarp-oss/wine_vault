# Domaine Marshall

Nuits-Saint-Georges estate with roots in the 1960s English négociant Tim Marshall, returned to family hands in 2022 under Jean Marshall after two decades leased to Méo-Camuzet. Organic farming and a distinctive oak-free élevage in low-permeability ceramic vessels; one of the most talked-about Burgundy newcomers.
