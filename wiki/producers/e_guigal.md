# E. Guigal

The Ampuis house that built the modern Côte-Rôtie market. Guigal's single-vineyard La-Las — La Mouline (1966, Côte Blonde, with Viognier), La Landonne (1978, Côte Brune, pure Syrah), and La Turque (1985, replanted Côte Brune) — are among the world's most collected Syrahs, traditionally released in 3-packs at FASS. Outside the icons, the Brune et Blonde, Château d'Ampuis, and the Hermitage Ex-Voto round out the serious tier. Off-style for the natural-leaning vault but a Rhône completist's anchor.
