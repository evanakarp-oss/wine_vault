# Enrico Togni Rebaioli

Natural winemaker in Erbanno, Val Camonica, founded 2001 when Enrico abandoned law school to farm inherited vineyards (1.5 ha from grandfather's 1960s plantings) plus acquired 1.5 ha on limestone terraces. Organically farmed 5-hectare estate (3 ha in production) on deep sandy soils with volcanic proximity. Cultivates Merlot, Nebbiolo, Barbera, Marzemino, and Erbanno (a rare native Lombard variety rescued from near-extinction by Togni).

## Notes

**Rare heritage:** Sole contemporary cultivator of Erbanno varietal. Terroir combines glacial valley position with volcanic influences and limestone geology. Minimal-intervention ethos; hand harvesting and natural fermentation. Wines reflect territorial character in high-altitude continental climate. Represents emerging wave of natural winemakers in underappreciated Val Camonica.
