# Philip Togni Vineyard

_A high-elevation Spring Mountain estate making a single, ageless Bordeaux-blend Cabernet in a deliberately classical style. Philip Togni — a Bordeaux-trained veteran of mid-century Napa — planted the estate at ~2,000 ft; daughter Lisa Togni now runs it. Structured, cool-toned, and built to age 20+ years._

Named in the CLAUDE.md aged-old-guard note as an on-taste sibling to Beringer PR / Mondavi Reserve. One wine, made the same way for decades: mountain fruit, firm tannin, restraint. The 2019 nine-pack and 2009 in the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] ($77–90/btl) are a case-depth entry to a classicist that rarely trades cheaply.
