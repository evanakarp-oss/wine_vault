# Paolo Rodaro

Family estate at Spessa di Cividale (Rodaro since 1846), ~60 ha. Known for
Schioppettino and for the **Romain** line of partially air-dried (appassimento)
reds, plus the full FCO white range and Picolit. Solid regional standard-bearer at
scale. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
