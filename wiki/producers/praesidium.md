# Praesidium

Small family-owned organic estate in Prezza (Abruzzo), established 1988 by Enzo and Lucia Pasquale. Today run by Enzo alongside children Antonia and Ottaviano. Praesidium specializes exclusively in Montepulciano d'Abruzzo, farming five hectares in an ideal microclimate suited to this ancient variety.

## Vineyard & Farming

Five hectares of organically farmed vineyards cultivated according to principles inspired by traditional Abruzzo methods. All work done by hand, including green harvest to control crop size. In spring, cover crop of fava beans adds nitrogen to soil naturally. The specific microclimate of Prezza — a valley particularly suited to Montepulciano — focuses the estate's entire production on this single varietal.

## Winemaking Philosophy

Low yields per hectare maintained through careful vineyard management. Minimal interventions in the cellar; no added chemicals. Fermentation conducted exclusively with autochthonous (wild) yeasts, honoring the vineyard's natural microbiology. Sulfur dioxide use kept well below organic certification limits, reflecting a preference for minimal additives. Wines bottle unfined and unfiltered, with only light rackings for clarification, preserving the wine's authentic character and connection to terroir.

## Key Wines

- **Montepulciano d'Abruzzo** — the estate's single offering, in various designated bottlings and ages
- **Montepulciano d'Abruzzo Riserva** — aged expression

## Philosophy

Praesidium wines are authentic expressions of land and tradition. The range includes bottles of striking personality and genuine character, capable of telling in the glass the story of both the winery and its special terroir. The family's commitment to organic farming and minimal intervention reflects a philosophy that great wine emerges from the vineyard, not the cellar.

## Notes

Praesidium represents the small-scale, terroir-focused approach to Montepulciano d'Abruzzo production. The singular focus on one varietal, combined with organic farming and minimal winemaking intervention, makes these wines direct expressions of Prezza's unique character. The estate exemplifies the Abruzzo tradition of family continuity (now spanning multiple generations) and the region's capacity for authenticity at human scale.
