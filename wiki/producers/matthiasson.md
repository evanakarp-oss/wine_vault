# Matthiasson

_Steve and Jill Matthiasson's farming-first Napa project — restrained, lower-alcohol, and stylistically closer to Europe than to cult Napa. A respected viticultural consultant, Matthiasson makes a linear Napa Cabernet, a Ribolla Gialla, a Friuli-inspired white field blend, and a rotating cast of Refosco, Cabernet Franc, and old-vine oddities._

## Notes

Squarely on the cellar's 'sense of place + tension' axis and its Friuli/skin-contact leanings. Organic farming, native ferments, early picking. The Napa Cab is the antithesis of the opulent style; the whites (Ribolla Gialla, the Napa white blend) and the Zinfandel/Refosco are the sleeper value. Appears across both the red and white sections of the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] (Ribolla case at ~$15/btl).
