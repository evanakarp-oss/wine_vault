# Kelley Fox Wines

_Kelley Fox makes ethereal, perfumed, low-intervention Willamette Pinot Noir, most famously from the old-vine Maresh Vineyard in the Dundee Hills and the biodynamic Momtazi Vineyard. Whole-cluster-inflected, gently extracted, energetic wines with a devoted following._
