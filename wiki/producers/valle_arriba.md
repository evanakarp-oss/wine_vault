# Valle Arriba

Winemaker: **Valle Arriba team**. Valle de Pucarà / Valle de Uco (Mendoza). Category: **Artisan / Terroir-Driven**.

## Approach

Valle Arriba focuses on the Valle de Pucarà, one of Mendoza's newest and most promising sub-zones at extreme altitude in San Carlos. The El Pucareño Malbec was cited by James Suckling 2025 as showing 'singular character and sense of place, with darker fruit and greater intensity.' Featured at Argentina Reloaded Rio de Janeiro 2024 and Buenos Aires 2025.

## Grapes

Malbec, Cabernet Franc, Semillon
