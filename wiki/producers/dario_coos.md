# Dario Coos

The reference house of **Ramandolo** (Nimis, the northern hills) — Italy's first
DOCG-recognized cru for sweet Verduzzo Friulano. Generations of Coos family
production; also dry Friulano, Ribolla, Malvasia and Schioppettino from the steep
Nimis amphitheater. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
