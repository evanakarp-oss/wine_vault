# Jean-Michel Stéphan

One of Côte-Rôtie's natural-wine anchors — Jean-Michel Stéphan farms whole-cluster, indigenous yeast, zero SO2 regimes on parcels in Coteaux du Bassenon and Coteaux du Tupin (both $146–$155 at FASS). Adjacent stylistically to Dard & Ribo and the Beaujolais Gang of Four — perfumed, lifted, sometimes vintage-variable. Le Grand Blanc VDF appears under 'Maison Stephan' in the data.
