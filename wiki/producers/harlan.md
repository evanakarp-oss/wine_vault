# Harlan Estate

_Bill Harlan's western-Oakville-hills estate — the archetypal Napa 'first growth,' a dense, opulent, oak-framed Cabernet blend, with The Maiden as the second wine. Allocation-only and among the most sought (and priciest) wines in California._
