# Puiatti

Historic Friuli name: Vittorio Puiatti was a mid-century apostle of pure,
**oak-free** white winemaking ("steel, not wood") and long-aged metodo classico from
Ribolla and Chardonnay. The brand has since changed hands (now under larger corporate
ownership) and is a shadow of its editorial-era self, though the sparkling program
persists. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli enoteca list
([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
