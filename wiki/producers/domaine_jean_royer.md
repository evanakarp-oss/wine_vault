# Domaine Jean Royer

Châteauneuf-du-Pape producer known for elegant, Burgundy-friendly styling. Josh Raynolds (Vinous, December 2016) noted: "Always among the most elegant examples of Châteauneuf-du-Pape…these are Châtauneufs for Burgundy lovers."

## Notes

Sustainable viticulture in Châteauneuf-du-Pape. Royer's wines buck the region's fruit-forward stereotype—emphasizing restraint, mineral tension, and food pairing. Grenache-based blends built for 15-20 year aging. Consistent quality and distinctive house style appeal to Burgundy-trained palates seeking serious Rhône expression.
