# Dominio del Águila

_The most exciting traditional address in Ribera del Duero. Jorge Monzón (trained at DRC and Vega Sicilia) and Isabel Rodero farm ancient, high-altitude co-planted field-blend vineyards around La Aguilera, making structured, savory, low-intervention reds (Canta la Perdiz, Peñas Aladas Gran Reserva) and a serious old-vine Albillo white._

## Notes

Old-vine (some pre-phylloxera) field blends of Tempranillo with Albillo, Bobal and others, farmed organically and vinified traditionally — the antithesis of modern extracted Ribera. The Peñas Aladas Gran Reserva '19 6-pack ($100/btl), Canta la Perdiz, and Albillo VV in the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] were the standout Spanish arrival.
