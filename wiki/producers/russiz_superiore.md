# Russiz Superiore

The Collio cru estate of the Marco Felluga family (Marco was Livio Felluga's brother;
now grandaughter Ilaria Felluga's generation), on the historic Russiz hill at Capriva
next to [[villa_russiz|Villa Russiz]]. ~50 ha of estate vineyard: Friulano, Pinot
Bianco, Sauvignon, Ribolla, plus the Col Disôre Collio Bianco blend and Cabernet
Franc. Polished, reliable, mid-scale classic Collio. Surfaced for the vault via the
2026-07 Friuli enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
