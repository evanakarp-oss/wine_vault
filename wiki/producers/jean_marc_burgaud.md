# Jean-Marc Burgaud

Morgon-based organic producer representing Beaujolais' younger generation. Michel Bettane described these wines as "part of the young generation which contributes to the dynamism of Morgon." David Schildknecht said Jean-Marc is "as usual brimming with ideas about how he is going to make what are among his region's handful of most exciting wines even better." Josh Raynolds (Vinous) remarked: "Burgaud is a serious player in the region ... and he has few peers when it comes to value."

## Notes

Organic viticulture in Morgon (schist/granite soils) producing compelling Gamay-based reds. Burgaud's approach emphasizes terroir expression over commercial fruit—linear, mineral-driven, age-worthy by Beaujolais standards (5-10 year potential). Rare example of serious, ambitious winemaking in the region; excellent value proposition.
