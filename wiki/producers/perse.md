# PerSe

Winemaker: **Edy del Popolo & David Bonomi**. Multi-region — Mendoza / Luján de Cuyo / Uco Valley (Mendoza). Category: **Artisan / Terroir-Driven**.

## Approach

PerSe was founded after Edy del Popolo and David Bonomi travelled every Argentine wine region with the specific goal of finding parcels whose character would shine through with minimal interference. Each wine expresses a single parcel exactly as it is — no oak obsession, no technological correction. Del Popolo is also General Manager at Susana Balbo (overseeing BenMarco); Bonomi is technical director at Bodega Norton. Their joint natural/low-intervention side project is among the most discussed in the new Argentine wave. Featured at Argentina Reloaded Buenos Aires 2025.

## Grapes

Malbec, Semillon, Cabernet Franc, Torrontés
