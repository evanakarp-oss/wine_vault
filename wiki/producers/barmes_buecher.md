# Barmès Buecher

Biodynamic Alsatian producer recognized for mineral-driven, age-worthy Gewürztraminer and Pinot Gris. Named a "Révélation" in the 2010 Gault Millau guide for commitment to terroir expression over fruit-forward commercial styling.

## Notes

Wettolsheim-based estate farming Grand Cru Pfersigberg and surrounding parcels with rigorous biodynamic viticulture. Known for structured, complex Gewürztraminer built for 10-15 year aging—Stephen Tanzer noted of the 2007 Pfersigberg that "these are wines that will age into masterpieces; it's Gewürztraminer gone Picasso." 2019 Vinous tasting scored 12 of 14 cuvées between 90-93+ points, confirming consistent quality across the range. Natural fermentations and minimal sulfur produce distinctive mineral tension and food-pairing versatility.
