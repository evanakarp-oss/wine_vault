# Thibaud Boudignon

Vouvray-based organic producer of exceptional dry Chenin Blancs. Michel Bettane declared: "[these] should be in the cellar of all connoisseurs of great dry chenins. The three wines of Boudignon were the best dry chenins that I tasted at the Salon of Angers, we are at the level of 1er Cru and Grand Cru Burgundy."

## Notes

Small-scale organic viticulture in Vouvray producing mineral-driven, age-worthy dry Chenins. Boudignon's wines represent Vouvray's serious, terroir-focused potential—high acidity, limestone tension, and complexity built for 10-20 year evolution. Consistently ranked among Loire's finest white wine producers.
