# L'Acino

Organic grower-producer in Catanzaro, Calabria, dedicated to Gaglioppo and traditional Calabrian viticulture on hillside vineyards. L'Acino operates on approximately 12-18 hectares of organically certified vineyard, emphasizing low-intervention farming that respects the region's volcanic and mineral-rich soils. The name "L'Acino" (the grape) reflects the producer's focus on fruit quality and varietal purity as the foundation of winemaking.

## Notes

L'Acino exemplifies the quality-focused, organic-farming model gaining traction among emerging Calabrian producers. Their emphasis on Gaglioppo showcases the variety's considerable potential for structure, elegance, and aging complexity—particularly when grown in Calabria's hill zones where cooler temperatures slow ripening and preserve natural acidity. The winery's organic certification underscores commitment to sustainable viticulture, reflecting broader movement among serious Calabrian producers to move beyond the region's historical reputation for simple, high-alcohol wines. L'Acino's grower model, where the family tends vineyard and winery, ensures quality control and terroir authenticity at every stage. Their wines represent Calabria's emerging identity as a source of distinctive, age-worthy Mediterranean reds.
