# 1701 Franciacorta

Historic biodynamic estate founded in 2011 by Federico and Silvia Stefini in Cazzago San Martino. First and only Demeter-certified biodynamic producer in the Franciacorta DOCG, operating 10 hectares entirely under biodynamic protocols since inception. Under winemaker Giulio Salti's direction, 1701 produces approximately 60,000 bottles annually of non-vintage sparkling wines using only Chardonnay (8 ha) and Pinot Noir (2 ha), with zero dosage philosophy.

## Notes

Founded 2011; fifth generation winemaking family. Represents the biodynamic frontier in Franciacorta, rare for a region dominated by conventional producers. All wines fermented naturally without dosing. No temperature control in vinification. Emphasis on terroir, minimal additions, and lunar-calendar guidance for key vineyard operations.
