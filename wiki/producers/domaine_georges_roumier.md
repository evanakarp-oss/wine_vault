# Domaine Georges Roumier

Benchmark Chambolle-Musigny domaine run by Christophe Roumier, among the most sought-after addresses in the Côte de Nuits. Holdings span village Chambolle through grands crus Bonnes-Mares and Musigny plus the monopole Clos de la Bussière in Morey-Saint-Denis; the wines are a reference for transparent, age-worthy red Burgundy.
