# Domaine des Ardoisières

## Notes

**Domaine des Ardoisières** is a biodynamic estate in the Savoie alpine region (Cevins), specializing in distinctive, mineral-driven white and red wines that express the terroir and microclimate influence of the French Alps—William Kelley describes it as "probably my favorite address for Savoie whites" for its "purity and transparency" qualities. The domaine's vineyards sit in the shadow of Mont Blanc in Savoie's high-altitude, cool-climate region, where slate soils (ardoises) give the wines their characteristic mineral salinity and herbal lift. Biodynamic farming emphasizes natural pest management, minimal chemical inputs, and careful attention to vineyard health through soil development and natural vigor management; yields are modest to concentrate the small berries' aromatic complexity and phenolic development. Winemaking focuses on low-alcohol, fresh expressions that capture the alpine terroir: native yeast fermentation in neutral vessels, minimal oak or stainless steel aging to preserve aromatic purity, and bottling with minimal SO2 to let the wine's natural mineral character emerge. Signature expressions include **Silice Blanc** and **Silice Rouge** (a low-alcohol red made from Mondeuse grape, described by CSW as capturing the Mondeuse's characteristic peppery profile while maintaining freshness; current release $29.99 per Raeders), plus white varieties reflecting the region's rare grapes and old-vine selections. CSW describes the house style as capturing "alpine influence" in vineyards "in the shadow of Mont Blanc," with the Ardoisières wines being "favorites of our staff and clientele" for their distinctive regional character. Current-release pricing estimated $25–40 makes these excellent value for biodynamic, mineral-focused alpine whites. The estate's biodynamic commitment, focus on mineral terroir expression, and small-scale alpine viticulture align directly with Evan's documented French terroir-driven taste filters (biodynamic-leaning, grower-scale, mineral-focused).
