# Meroi

Venerable family winery in Buttrio, Colli Orientali, established 1920 and now managed by Paolo Meroi. Nearly 50 acres farmed organically with ponca terroir (marl and sandstone from ancient Eocene seas), producing refined whites and reds from flagship vineyard sites Zitelle and Dominin.

## History & Philosophy

Founded by Domenico Meroi in 1920, Meroi has passed through generations to his great-grandson Paolo Meroi, who continues the estate's winemaking tradition. Paolo oversees expansion to 19 hectares across the high hills of Buttrio in the heart of Colli Orientali. 

The winery practices **organic viticulture without formal certification**, refusing synthetic pesticides and herbicides. Vines are fertilized with manure and treated only with copper sulphate and sulphur. The family encourages natural biological balance in the vineyard, promoting the natural predation between insects rather than relying on chemical pest control.

## Vineyard & Terroir

The distinctive terroir of Meroi's vineyards is shaped by **ponca soil**—a blend of marl and sandstone dating from ancient Eocene seas. This imparts minerality and complexity to the wines. Two flagship vineyard sites define Meroi's portfolio:

- **Zitelle vineyard**: Acquired in 2004; considered a "Grand Cru" site within Colli Orientali
- **Dominin vineyard**: Named after Paolo's father; also recognized as a "Grand Cru" within the appellation
