# Costadilà

Natural-wine pioneer founded 2006 by Ernesto Cattel in Tarzo, Treviso province, above Conegliano in Prosecco hills. Ernesto pioneered coupling "Col Fondo" (ancestral sparkling method) with skin-contact fermentation. Organically farmed; native-yeast fermentation; zero SO2 in vinification. Wines named after vineyard altitudes (450 s.l.m., etc.). Produces sparkling wines (OX and MOZ labels) using ancestral method plus red table wine from native Veneto varieties. Polyculture farm: vines, fruits, vegetables, cereals, livestock coexist. Arno Cattel (son) now leads after Ernesto's 2018 passing, with family-friend stewardship circle.

## Notes

**Ancestral-method pioneer:** Col Fondo + skin-contact combination rare even in natural-wine circles. Ernesto's legacy continues through communal stewardship model. Altitude-based wine naming reflects terroir obsession. Orange-wine (skin-contact) production demonstrates experimental minimalism. Polyculture approach reflects sustainable-agriculture commitment beyond viticulture.
