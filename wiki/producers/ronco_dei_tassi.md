# Ronco dei Tassi

Fabio Coser's family estate above Cormons (founded 1989, sons Matteo and Enrico now
active), ~18 ha amid woodland — named for the badgers (*tassi*). Known for **Fosarin**
(Collio Bianco: Friulano/Pinot Bianco/Malvasia, partly in wood) and a strong Malvasia;
consistent Tre Bicchieri track record at fair prices. Surfaced for the vault via the
2026-07 Friuli enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
