# Vie di Romans

Gianfranco Gallo's estate at Mariano del Friuli — the producer who put the gravelly
Isonzo plain on the fine-wine map. Site-named bottlings held back for late release:
**Chardonnay Vie di Romans**, **Sauvignon Piere** (unoaked) and **Vieris** (oaked),
**Flors di Uis** blend, **Dessimis** Pinot Grigio, Dolee Friulano, Dis Cumieris
Malvasia; Longorucis Pinot Nero in red. Dense, structured, long-lived whites —
Isonzo's answer to grand-cru thinking. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli
enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]) as "Streets of Romans".
