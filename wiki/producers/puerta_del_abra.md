# Puerta del Abra

Winemaker: **Jorge Pérez Companc family**. Balcarce, Buenos Aires Province (Patagonia). Category: **Artisan / Terroir-Driven**.

## Approach

One of Argentina's most unusual and compelling wine projects: 12 hectares in Balcarce in the province of Buenos Aires — 115–137m altitude, Atlantic coastal influence, entirely different from the Andean model. Riesling planted 2014 from massal selection SO4 rootstock. Jorge Pérez Companc's personal project since 2013. The coastal Atlantic climate, loamy soils, and relatively low altitude produce wines with crisp natural acidity and a distinctly European sensibility. The Riesling is among Argentina's most distinctive whites — cool, mineral, and unlike anything from Mendoza.

## Grapes

Riesling, Pinot Noir, Chardonnay
