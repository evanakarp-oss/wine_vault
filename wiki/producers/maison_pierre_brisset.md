# Maison Pierre Brisset

Micro-négociant founded 2014 in the Château de Bligny, Bligny-lès-Beaune,
by a second-career vigneron: Pierre Brisset studied finance, co-founded the
Paris travel site VoyagerMoinsCher.com, sold it to Rakuten in 2010, and
pivoted to Burgundy. He owns a single 0.5 ha estate parcel — Chassagne-
Montrachet 1er Cru Abbaye de Morgeot (Chardonnay), bought 2013 — and buys
fruit for everything else, deliberately skewed to 1er and Grand Cru across
both Côtes (Meursault, Puligny, Chassagne, Vosne, Chambolle, Gevrey,
Échezeaux, Corton-Charlemagne). He has no winery of his own: the wines are
made at the "Wine Studio" custom-crush cellar at Château de Bligny —
Burgundy's only California-style shared facility, run since 2015 around
Dominique Lafon (Comtes Lafon) and Pierre Meurgey. Cellar regime per the
Japanese importer Firadis: gentle pneumatic press, whites barrel-fermented
and raised in barrique, reds steel-fermented then barrique, bottled
unfiltered. Fass's house benchmark — he uses "tastes like Brisset" as the
compliment for other producers' wines.

## Notes

**French critic record** (validates the Fass narrative): RVF June 2017
"plus beaux vins de l'année" — Chassagne 1er Cru **18/20**, Chambolle 1er Cru
16/20; Guide Hachette 2022 — 1 star each for Saint-Aubin 1er Sous Roche
Dumay 2019 and Chassagne 1er 2019; Guide Hachette 2024 — **2 stars** for
Chassagne 1er Abbaye de Morgeot; Bettane & Desseauve coverage (90/100 for
the 2020 Bourgogne Pinot Noir); Gault&Millau winery entry. Jancis Robinson
has tasted every release since the debut vintage; Allen Meadows has praised
the texture. Japan import channel: Firadis.

**Cuvée name decoder**: *Gabrius* = ancient name of Gevrey (declassified
Gevrey-Chambertin Pinot); *Cassaneas* = ancient name of Chassagne (vines in
Chassagne, Meursault, Volnay just outside the AOC; 14 months in barrel,
~900 bottles).

**Taste-filter placement**: no farming certification or organic claim —
purchased fruit, shared custom-crush facility. Earns his slot on the
terroir-transparency + Lafon-adjacent-polish axis, not the
biodynamic-grower axis (accepted as such, 2026-06-11). Full sourcing in
the linked research view.
