# Marie Courtin

_Cult single-parcel grower Champagne from Dominique Moreau in Polisot (Aube / Côte des Bar), farmed biodynamically and bottled with zero dosage. Each cuvée (Résonance, Efflorescence, Concordance) is one vintage, one plot, mostly Pinot Noir — among the most sought-after grower fizz._
