# Domaine des Pierres Sèches

Family-run Saint-Joseph grower based in the northern Ardèche hills. FASS imports the Cuvée 1930 (old-vine, both rouge and blanc, $63–$77 tier), the Revirand lieu-dit ($52), and basic Saint-Joseph blanc + rouge at the $30-ish entry level. Granite terraces, traditional élevage, no new oak.
