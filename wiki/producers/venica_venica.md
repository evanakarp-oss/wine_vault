# Venica & Venica

Family estate in Dolegna del Collio (founded 1930; Gianni and Giorgio Venica's
generation, now the next). ~40 ha in the cooler northern Collio. **Ronco delle Mele
Sauvignon** is the flagship — arguably Italy's most decorated Sauvignon — plus Ronco
delle Cime Friulano, Malvasia Petris, Pinot Bianco Talis, Ribolla l'Adelchi.
Aromatic, precise, single-vineyard-driven. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07
Friuli enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]), where it has
the deepest bench of any Collio producer.
