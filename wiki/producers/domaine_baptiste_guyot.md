# Domaine Baptiste Guyot

Beaune domaine on the former Abbaye Saint-Martin farm; Baptiste Guyot took over in 2010 and works ~9 ha of Pinot Noir, Chardonnay and Aligoté across Beaune, Pernand-Vergelesses, Volnay, Pommard and Monthélie. Ploughing and organic treatments, HVE-certified since 2020.
