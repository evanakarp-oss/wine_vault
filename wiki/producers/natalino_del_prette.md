# Natalino del Prette

Historic producer in Lecce province, southern Salento, with roots in traditional Apulian viticulture. The family-operated winery tends approximately 15-20 hectares of vineyard, focusing on regional varieties Primitivo and Negroamaro planted in the limestone-rich soils characteristic of Lecce. Natalino del Prette practices sustainable viticulture methods while maintaining the traditional methods that have defined Salento winemaking for generations.

## Notes

Natalino del Prette embodies the heritage of Lecce's wine culture, a region historically central to Apulian viticulture but often overshadowed by commercial bulk-wine production. Their Primitivo di Manduria wines express the full ripeness potential of the variety, balanced with aging-worthiness through careful harvest selections. Negroamaro bottlings showcase the structured tannins and dark, sophisticated fruit profile the Lecce zone is known for. As a multi-generational family producer, Del Prette contributes to the recovery of Salento's artisanal wine traditions, emphasizing fruit quality and terroir expression over quantity. The estate's commitment to sustainable practices reflects the broader Apulian quality movement gaining momentum in the early 21st century.
