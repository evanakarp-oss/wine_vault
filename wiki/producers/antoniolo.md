# Antoniolo

Alto Piemonte-based producer known for Gattinara and other northern Piedmont wines. Antoniolo represents the traditional Alto Piemonte approach to Nebbiolo.

## Terroir & Appellation

**Alto Piemonte** — Northern Piedmont Alpine foothills; distinctive terroir producing elegant, mineral-driven Nebbiolo-based reds (Gattinara DOCG).
