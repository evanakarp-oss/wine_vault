# Georges & Vincent Laval

Tiny, highly sought organic grower Champagne in Cumières, in the Vallée de la
Marne, farmed organically since the 1970s (one of the region's organic
pioneers) and now run by Vincent Laval. Single-vineyard, low/zero-dosage cuvées
("Les Chênes", "Les Hautes Chèvres") in a vinous, transparent style. Imported
to the US via Wine Source.

> Note: the same house appears as "George Laval" (Cumières) in the Roscioli
> Wine Club rollup — cross-reference if a Roscioli producer page is ever
> created.
