# Zorzal Wines

Winemaker: **Michelini Brothers (JP, Matías, Gerardo)**. Tupungato (Mendoza). Category: **Biodynamic**.

## Approach

Founded 2008. Concrete eggs, no oak in terroir line, native yeasts, respectful enology. One of the most influential projects in the Uco Valley. Biodynamic practices throughout.

## Grapes

Malbec, Pinot Noir, Cab Franc, Torrontés

## Key Wines

- Terroir Único
- Eggo Franco
- Lecciones de Vuelo
