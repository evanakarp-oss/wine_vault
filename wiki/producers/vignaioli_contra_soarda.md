# Vignaioli Contra Soarda

Winery established 2002 by Mirco and Gloria Gottardi in Asiago plateau hills near Bassano del Grappa (Veneto), now led by second generation Eleonora and Marcello Gottardi. "Contra' Soarda" means "hamlet on the slope." Operates 60,000+ vines and 1,000 olive trees with strong environmental-sustainability commitment. Hilly volcanic-soil terroir with significant temperature variation enhancing grape quality. Cultivates Garganega, Pinot Noir, Carmenere, Merlot, Vespaiola. Produces red, white, rosé, sparkling wines from Breganze DOC. Limited-edition Musso wine line (Veneto dialect for donkey).

## Notes

**Polyculture philosophy:** Vines, olives, grains, vegetables, livestock coexist on same plots. Second-generation transition underway. Asiago plateau microclimate—cooler nights, ample sunlight, valley breezes—creates quality fruit. Vespaiolo focus reflects Breganze regional specialty. Musso label introduces whimsy to premium positioning.
