## Fass Notes — Arguably the Greatest Grand Cru Burgundy Value on Earth – 2022 Remi Poisot Grand Cru Corton Bressandes

> I'm a huge fan of Beethoven. I love his pia no concertos. I love his sonatas. And, of course his symphonies. The first 2 are lovely examples of what they are. The Eroica is where he really started to break the rules. And the 5th is a work of genius, exploring what a master can do with 4 notes in the forst movement. The 6th is gorgeous. But for me, the 9th is just a level above. The last movement seems like it was composed by angels. There is one part ( 1:12:58 in this recording ) where the sopranos have a segment alone that just floats like weightless beauty.
