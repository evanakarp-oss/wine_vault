# G. Montisci

Producer from the Nuoro region of central Sardinia, working with native Sardinian vineyard varieties on hillside terrain. The estate focuses on Cannonau (Grenache), which thrives in Sardinia's continental Mediterranean climate at elevation. Montisci's vineyard work emphasizes sustainable practices and natural expression of the Nuoro terroir, known for its mineral-rich soils and cooler, higher-altitude conditions.

## Notes

G. Montisci represents the small-scale, terroir-focused producer model in Sardinia's interior wine regions. Nuoro's hillside vineyards produce Cannonau with more structure and freshness than lower-altitude coastal zones, often showing wild red and dark berry notes with mineral grip. The producer's commitment to sustainable practices reflects the broader Sardinian quality movement toward expressing place through thoughtful vineyard management and minimal winemaking intervention.
