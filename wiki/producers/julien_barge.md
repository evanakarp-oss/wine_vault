# Julien Barge

Julien Barge took over the family domaine in Ampuis from his father Gilles, who in turn worked under his grandfather Pierre. The estate sits on roughly six hectares of granite-and-mica schist across the heart of Côte-Rôtie, with a stake in the Côte Brune-adjacent lieu-dit Le Combard. Old-school élevage in barrels of varied age, low new oak, stems used selectively — the wines fall in the structured, savory, slow-aging camp rather than the modern fruit-forward style. FASS carries the domaine's full vertical of the Coeur de Combard parcel back to 2015, the basic Les Côtes, and a Saint-Joseph Blanc.
