# Domaine Marquis d'Angerville

The Volnay benchmark. 16.5ha, of which 11.5ha is Volnay 1er Cru (about 12% of the appellation's 1er Crus). Holdings: **Clos des Ducs** (2.15ha monopole — jewel in the crown), Caillerets, Champans, Taillepieds, Fremiet, Mitans, Pitures, Clos des Angles. Biodynamic.
