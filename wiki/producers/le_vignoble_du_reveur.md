# Le Vignoble du Rêveur

Biodynamic, low-intervention Alsace estate based in Bennwihr, the project of
Mathieu Deiss (of the Marcel Deiss family) with Emmanuelle Milan. Co-planted
field blends, skin-contact whites and experimental cuvées ("Singulier",
"Pierres Sauvages", "Un Instant sur Terre") in a natural idiom. Imported to
the US via Wine Source.
