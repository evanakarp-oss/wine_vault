# Lamé Delisle Boucard

Touraine producer in Ingrandes-de-Touraine, located downstream of the Chinon/Bourgueil heartland. Lamé Delisle Boucard represents the broader Touraine appellation tier, which produces lighter, earlier-drinking Cabernet Francs than the prestige Chinon and Bourgueil appellations.

## Terroir & Appellation

**Ingrandes-de-Touraine, Touraine** — Regional-level appellation encompassing diverse terroirs. Touraine Cabernet Francs are typically lighter in body, lower in tannin, and earlier-drinking (2–6 year window) than their Chinon/Bourgueil counterparts, reflecting different soil and climate profiles.
