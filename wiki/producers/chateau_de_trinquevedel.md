# Château de Trinquevedel

Tavel rosé producer — 'Les Vignes d'Eugène' 2023 at $37–$41. Tavel is the only French AOC dedicated entirely to rosé; Trinquevedel is a long-tenured estate making the structured, food-oriented, dark-pink style.
