# Charlopin Tissier

Domaine founded 2013 by Yann Charlopin (son of Philippe Charlopin of Gevrey-Chambertin) + Justine Tissier. Based in Morey-Saint-Denis. 0.15ha **Marsannay Le Chapitre** between Clos du Roy and Montre Cul in Chenôve. Organic farming, red stony clay over Comblanchien limestone. 18-month élevage in barrique with 20–30% new oak (mostly François Frères).
