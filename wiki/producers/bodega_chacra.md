# Bodega Chacra

Winemaker: **Piero Incisa della Rocchetta**. Mainqué, Alto Valle del Río Negro (Patagonia). Category: **Biodynamic**.

## Approach

Biodynamic certified from founding (2004). Grandson of the creator of Sassicaia. Ungrafted vines planted in 1932. Indigenous yeasts, unfiltered, natural decantation only. Chardonnay collaboration with Jean-Marc Roulot. The defining reference for Argentine Pinot Noir.

## Grapes

Pinot Noir, Chardonnay

## Key Wines

- Treinta y Dos
- Cincuenta y Cinco
- Barda
- Chardonnay (with Roulot)
