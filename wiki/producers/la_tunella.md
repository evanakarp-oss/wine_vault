# La Tunella

The Zorzettig family's (Livia branch) polished estate at Premariacco — ~70 ha,
modern cellar, consistent awards. Full FCO range; the **Schioppettino** and Refosco
are the reds of note, whites led by Friulano and the Biancosesto blend. Commercial-
plus tier with real consistency. Surfaced for the vault via the 2026-07 Friuli
enoteca list ([[../_views/friuli_producer_map_2026_07|view]]).
