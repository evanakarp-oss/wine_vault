# Domaine Maume-Siblas

Rebirth of the classic Gevrey-Chambertin Domaine Maume: after a decade leased to Tawse/Pascal Marchand (2012–2021), the vines returned to family hands as Maume-Siblas, with Thomas Maume in the cellar alongside mother Patricia Siblas. Biodynamically farmed (a legacy of the Tawse era), aiming to recapture unadorned, structured Gevrey typicity.
