# Domaine Simon Bize

_A benchmark Savigny-lès-Beaune estate — fresh, transparent, terroir-driven reds (and fine whites) from a domaine that has quietly made some of the Côte de Beaune's best value for decades. Run since Patrick Bize's death by his widow Chisa Bize._

## Notes

Organic-leaning, cool and precise wines from the Savigny premiers crus (Aux Vergelesses, Les Guettes, Aux Marconnets, Les Fournaux) plus a serious Latricières-Chambertin. The 2012 Latricières and aged Savigny Vergelesses ('06/'10, $50–80) in the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] are the on-taste picks — grower Côte de Beaune with tension.
