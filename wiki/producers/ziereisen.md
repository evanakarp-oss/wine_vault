# Hanspeter Ziereisen

Markgräflerland's reference estate — Hanspeter Ziereisen farms on the south-facing slopes facing Switzerland, in the shadow of the Black Forest. The Jaspis line is the top tier: Spätburgunder Alte Reben 10-4 ($182), Spätburgunder Zipsin, Gutedel Alte Reben 10-4, Chardonnay Nägelin, Grauburgunder Würmlin, Syrah Daublin. Below that, Talrain / Rhini / Schulen Spätburgunder + Gestad Syrah at $30–$45. One of the most ambitious German Pinot Noir estates.
