# Cédric Bouchard

_A cult grower in the Aube whose Roses de Jeanne cuvées are each single-parcel, single-vintage, single-variety, made from low yields with minimal or zero dosage. Purist, vinous, low-pressure Champagnes (Les Ursules, Côte de Béchalin, Le Creux d'Enfer, La Haute-Lemblé) that read more like wine than fizz._
