# Domaine Vincent Dauvissat

_Vincent Dauvissat farms biodynamically and makes what many consider the co-benchmark (with Raveneau) of great Chablis — grand crus Les Clos and Les Preuses, premiers crus La Forest, Séchet, Vaillons, plus a serious village. Barrel-raised, deep, mineral, and profoundly age-worthy._

## Notes

Biodynamic-leaning, own-domaine, tiny production; the wines combine Chablis cut with real density. A Kermit Lynch import in the US. The La Forest '22 ($110) in the [[auction_261W_week30_wheelhouse_2026_07|Week-30 261W sale]] is the on-taste pick; Les Preuses and Les Clos are trophy-tier.
