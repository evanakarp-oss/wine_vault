# Casa Caterina

Natural winemaking estate in Monticelli Brusati, Franciacorta, run by brothers Aurelio and Emilio del Bono. Casa Caterina specializes in low-sulfite sparkling wines using Chardonnay and Pinot Noir, employing organic viticulture with minimal intervention philosophy. Known for emphasis on terroir expression and natural fermentation in Franciacorta.

## Notes

Founded as a natural winemaking project by the del Bono brothers, Casa Caterina represents the emerging wave of artisan and minimal-intervention producers in the Franciacorta region. The winery focuses on organic certification and produces both classic brut and rosé sparkling wines with sustainable farming practices.
