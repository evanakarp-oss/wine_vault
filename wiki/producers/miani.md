# Miani

Founded 1985 by Enzo Pontoni in Buttrio village, Colli Orientali del Friuli, producing <700 cases annually from 10 hectares densely planted with high Sauvignon Blanc and Merlot proportion, plus Refosco, Friulano, Malvasia, Chardonnay, Ribolla Gialla. Mineral-rich ponca soils (calcareous clay and sandstone layers). Particularly known for Sauvignon Blanc expression; also produces quality reds and whites from Friuli native varieties.

## Notes

**Scarcity focused:** Limited annual production reflects quality-over-volume philosophy. Ponca terroir creates distinctive mineral expression common to Colli Orientali. Sauvignon Blanc—international variety—combined with native Friuli varieties (Friulano, Ribolla, Refosco) reflects balanced portfolio. Proximity to Slovenia border influences regional identity.
