# La Grenouille

**La Grenouille Wine Imports** — a small California importer of curated French
grower wines (owner Jérôme Brenot; based in South Pasadena / greater LA;
distributes in California and New York). The book is almost entirely French,
skewing terroir-driven and natural-leaning, with an unusually deep
**grower-Champagne** bench plus a strong **Northern Rhône** and **Loire/Jura**
spine — squarely in Evan's wheelhouse. Roster pasted from the site's *Growers*
page on 2026-07-09.
