# Veritas Wines

California-based fine-wine importer + distributor, founded 1989. Portfolio
across France, Italy, Germany, Switzerland and the USA, with a signature
focus on family-run estates farming organically, biodynamically or
naturally. Trade distribution in CA, NV, CO, AZ, NM; partner network
covers NY.

Lines deep on Burgundy (Domaine Leroy, Arnoux-Lachaux, Bruno Clair,
Michel Lafarge, de Montille, de la Vougeraie, Comte Armand, Sylvain
Pataille) and grower Champagne (Pierre Gerbais, Hugues Godmé, Camille
Savès, José Dhondt, Le Brun Servenay, Vincey), with notable depth in
Loire (Guiberteau, Vincent Pinard, Bretaudeau, Ogereau, Maunoury),
Languedoc-Roussillon (Montcalmès, Danjou-Banessy, Pedres-Blanques) and
boundary-pushing Italy (Vino di Anna, La Visciola, Vaira Aurelj).

Strong Evan-curation alignment: biodynamic, terroir-driven, grower-scale,
boutique. Multiple producers overlap with the Chambers Street editorial
universe (Sylvain Pataille, Lafarge-Vial, Bret Brothers) — see CSW counts
in the rollup below.
