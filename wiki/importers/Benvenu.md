# Benvenu

**★ Focus distributor** (Evan's pick, 2026-07-07 — *"I really like the wines I've
had from this portfolio"*). Benvenu (benvenusa.com) imports **a small portfolio
of artisanal, micro producers who are defining the next era of their regions.**
Every producer farms **under ~10 hectares, harvests by hand,** and is driven to
make serious, terroir-transparent wine. The book straddles the
Friuli–Slovenia border hills (Collio, Colli Orientali, Brda, Vipava), volcanic
Etna, high-altitude Chianti Classico + Cortona in Tuscany, Mamoiada in Sardinia,
and one Langhe grower in Piedmont.

This is dead-center in Evan's taste: **terroir-driven, biodynamic-leaning,
grower-scale** — the Italian Friuli / Piedmont vein of the curation filter, plus
the border-Slovenia orange/white scene and volcanic Etna whites. Roster pasted
from Evan's list on 2026-07-07; regions verified against the importer's producer
pages + Wine Enthusiast / retailer listings (see per-row notes).
