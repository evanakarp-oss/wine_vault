# Roscioli Wine Club

Italian-only artisan-focused wine club + editorial site run out of Rome by the Roscioli family (the deli/restaurant). Treat as a **preferred Italian importer/curator** alongside Kermit Lynch, Neal Rosenthal etc. Especially strong for Piedmont, Tuscany, Sicily, Friuli and lesser-trod regions (Calabria, Sardinia, Molise).

Each producer profile here also exists as its own page at `https://www.roscioliwineclub.com/<slug>/` with a winemaker video — see `roscioli.profile_url` in each producer page's frontmatter.
