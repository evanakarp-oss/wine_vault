# Henderson Selections

Texas-based (Austin) artisanal wine distributor/importer, founded 2012. Tagline
**"Place Over Process."** Low-intervention wines from small vignerons — one of the
more stylistically diverse US portfolios, spanning the US West Coast and nine
countries. Distributes in Texas (via Classified Wines & Spirits) and is expanding
into the NY/NJ market. Strong overlap with Evan's taste: deep grower-Champagne
bench, terroir Beaujolais, Piedmont classicists, Sonoma-Coast/North-Coast
California, plus Friuli/Carso skin-contact. Roster pasted from
`hendersonselections.com/wine-producers` on 2026-06-19 (full list in
`raw/henderson/producers_2026-06-19.md`).
