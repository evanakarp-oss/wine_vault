## Methodology

1. Source landed at `raw/polaner/portfolio_2026-05-21.md` (323 producers across 9 countries, hierarchical country → region → producer structure preserved).
2. Cross-check script: normalize name (NFKD ASCII, lowercase, hyphens→spaces, strip prefixes like *Domaine/Château/Cave/Estate/Cascina/Tenuta*), drop generic words and given names, require **all** remaining tokens ≥3 chars to appear as whole words in Raeder's `producer` field.
3. Hand-audit each match against the Polaner region tag and Raeder's cuvée — false positives noted above.
