# LPV (La Passion du Vin) — where the signal is strongest, by French region (2026-06)

LPV is the serious French-amateur forum (≈400k posts, ≈6k members, founded 2002). Its
signal is uneven: most of it is chat, but a few **structural pockets carry consensus-grade
signal** — blind panels, long per-producer megathreads, and curated best-of polls. This
maps those pockets to the French regions in [[../../CLAUDE|Evan's taste profile]] so a
future read (or an LPV ingest pipeline, à la Berserkers) targets the gold and skips the noise.

## The 5 signal types (ranked by strength)

1. **Per-producer "fil" megathreads** — the single highest-value unit. One pinned thread
   per domaine accumulates multi-vintage CRs from many tasters over 10–44+ pages
   (e.g. Chave runs ~44 pages, Jamet/Clape/Vincent Paris deep). This is the closest thing
   to a structured critic database the forum has. **Start here for any in-scope producer.**
2. **Organized blind horizontals / verticals** (mostly in `/degustations-eclectiques`).
   Blind + multi-taster ⇒ the most reliable *relative* ranking within a vintage/appellation.
   These are the panels you trust over any single post.
3. **Curated "best-of" / poll threads** — distilled community consensus
   ("Top 10 domaines les plus lus", "Le meilleur du Rhône", "Vos champagnes de vignerons
   préférés", "Top 3 Bourgogne rouge", "bon rapport qualité-prix"). Great for a fast read
   of who the hive rates.
4. **Regional circles (cercles régionaux LPV)** — LPV Bordeaux / Paname / Versailles /
   Belgique etc. run recurring semi-plénière dinners with structured CRs. High signal,
   geographically clustered tasters.
5. **Annual-event coverage** — Grands Jours de Bourgogne (barrel reports, biennial),
   Salon des Vins de Loire, Salon Vignerons Indépendants. Good for young-vintage reads.

## How to mine it (practical)
- **For a specific producer:** find its `fil` first (the megathread), read the last 2–3 pages
  for current-vintage consensus, then the early pages for aged-bottle track record.
- **For "is this vintage/appellation any good":** find the blind horizontal in
  `/degustations-eclectiques` — trust the panel ranking over any single CR.
- **For discovery in your lane:** the "best-of / top 3 / vigneron préférés / rapport
  qualité-prix" poll threads are the fastest consensus read.
- **Trust weighting:** blind panel > long producer fil (many tasters) > regional-circle CR
  > single enthusiast post. Reputations matter — a handful of prolific tasters carry the
  forum; weight named, consistent contributors over one-offs.
