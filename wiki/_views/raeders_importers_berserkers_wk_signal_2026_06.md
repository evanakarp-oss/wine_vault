(`raeders_x_importers_master_2026_06.md`) and overlays two independent demand
signals:

- **WB** = rank + mention count in the Wine Berserkers *"Top 10 producers in
  your cellar"* megathread (collector consensus — the community favorites).

## Reading the result

- **The strongest buys** sit in Tier 1 plus the on-taste names in Tier 2:
  Giacomo Conterno, Burlotto, Ridge (reference set), Mugnier, Roumier, Chave,
  Rousseau, Beaucastel, López de Heredia, Mount Eden — terroir-driven,
  grower-scale, and community-validated. Most aren't yet in the cellar.
- **Tier 3 is mostly classed-growth Bordeaux via BNP.** Kelley covers them, but
  per Evan's taste these are *drink-now aged bottles only* — so weight toward
  back-vintages in the Raeders listings, not current releases.
- **Roagna** is the one name carrying a signal across *both* importer lists
  (Polaner + Roscioli) and already in the cellar — a natural vertical to deepen.
- **Caveat:** signals attach to producer pages that exist in the vault; a
  Raeders producer with no page (or no ingested signal) shows as blank here even
  if collectors love it. WB names are matched from short thread surnames, so this
  is a curated floor, not an exhaustive ceiling.
