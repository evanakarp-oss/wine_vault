# R Squared Selections — Top Picks (2026-06-17)

R Squared is a natural/terroir importer whose book *is* Evan's wheelhouse: deep
**Jura (25), Savoie, Friuli**, traditional Piemonte, grower Champagne, Cornas, and
grower-Aligoté white Burgundy. Refreshes the [[r_squared_picks_2026_05_27|May-27
screen]]; **61 wines are flagged new**. Prices are retail (real buys, not auction
estimates). Already owned (from cellar): [[jean_francois_ganevat|Ganevat]] (Madelon),
[[elvio_cogno|Cogno]] (×3), Foillard (Morgon '18).

## Obscure regions first (the live interest)

### Jura — the natural grail region, missing from Acker, deep here
- **[[jean_francois_ganevat|Ganevat]]** (own the Madelon): négoce cuvées 'Poulprix' 2024
  ($42, 51 avail), 'La Croix des Bataille' 2024 ($42), and the aged macerated
  **'L'Eau d'Range' Chard/Savagnin 2018 ($59)** — the orange-leaning one.
- **Domaine des Cavarodes** (Étienne Thiébaud): Chardonnay 'Les Lumachelles' 2020–2023
  ($75), Pinot/Trousseau 'Lumachelles' ($59) — cult Franche-Comté grower, **vault gap**.
- **[[domaine_marnes_blanches|Marnes Blanches]]** (vault page): Chardonnay 'Au Rettin' /
  'Empreinte' 2022 ($59); macerated 'En Quatre Vis' 2023 ($45) — the skin-contact entry.
- **[[stephane_tissot|Tissot]]** (vault page): single-vineyard Chardonnays 'En Barberon' /
  'Les Bruyères' / 'Les Gravières' 2023 ($69).
- **Zeroine 'Ju Sa Ox' Savagnin Sous Voile 2018 ($79)** — oxidative; Labet Macvin NV
  ($119); La Bouchaude 'La Vague' Savagnin Macération 2022 ($35, cheap orange flier).

### Savoie — Belluard (the closing Gringet category)
- **Belluard 'Monsieur Gringet' 2020 ($54)** and 'Les Grandes Jorasses' Altesse 2020 ($52).
  Dominique Belluard passed in 2021; biodynamic alpine Gringet is a category winding down —
  library availability won't repeat. Sparkling 'Perles du Mont Blanc' '18/'19 ($26–32).

### Friuli — Miani (the cult gap from your Acker question)
- **Miani** (Enzo Pontoni, tiny production) — exactly the Friuli profile you asked about:
  **Ribolla Gialla 'Pettarin' 2023 ($95)**, Malvasia 2023 ($85), Sauvignon 'Saurint' /
  'Zitelle' 2023 ($135); reds Rosso 2022 ($99), Merlot 2022 ($119). **Vault gap.**
