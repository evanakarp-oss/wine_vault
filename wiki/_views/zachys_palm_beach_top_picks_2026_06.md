# Zachys zCollections (Palm Beach) — Top Picks (2026-06)

1,742 lots. The collection's bulk is cult California (**Schrader alone is 116
lots**; Marcassin 29, Bond 23, Realm/Dana/Memento Mori/Colgin/Hundred
Acre/Harlan — all skipped per the taste filter) and big-name Bordeaux. What's
left over is genuinely strong: deep **traditionalist Piedmont**, aged
**classed-growth Bordeaux at lower estimates than catalog 261W**, and a short
but real natural list. Estimates here run noticeably below market — but
**read the condition strings**: pre-1990 bottles often have shoulder fills;
"bottom neck (BN) or better / into neck" is what you want.
