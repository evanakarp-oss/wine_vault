## Methodology

- **CSW signal:** dedicated article count (★) parsed from `wiki/producers/<slug>.md` frontmatter `retailers.chambers.dedicated_count`. 143 producers had ≥1 dedicated CSW article and weren't owned per `cellar/*.md`. (Final count after re-resolving owned set: 138.)
- **WK signal:** post count from `raw/berserkers/William_Kelley/_post_index.csv` (4,729 posts scraped from wineberserkers.com/u/william_kelley), ingested via `scripts/compile_wb_signals.py` into `retailers.berserkers_kelley.post_count` per producer page.
- **Owned set:** distinct `producer_slug` values across all `cellar/*.md` frontmatter.
- **Cross-retailer:** D=DTE, R=Raeders, F=FASS — flagged from `retailers.<r>.in_portfolio` so you can see when CSW isn't your only path.

## Caveats

- WK posts ≠ WK ratings. The Berserkers post is a proxy for "Kelley talks about this producer publicly" — could be enthusiasm or critique. The actual Wine Advocate (Kelley) clipping ingest (`raw/clippings/wine_advocate/`) is wired but empty; that would be the score-bearing source. Open follow-up.
- WK posts on Berserkers heavily skew Burgundy / Champagne / Bordeaux (his Wine Advocate beats). Mosel-grower / Loire-CF / Beaujolais lacunae in §4 partly reflect WK's coverage scope, not those producers' quality.
