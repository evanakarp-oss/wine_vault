## What to skip on these pages

- **Mass Italian PG**: Cavit, Riff, Santa Margherita, Ruffino, Santi, Santa Marina, Antica Corte
- **12-year-old aromatic white**: Villa Medoro Passerina 2012 at $5.99 — too old to be fresh
- **Kosher mass-market**: Bartenura, Cantina Gabriele
- **Co-op orange wines**: Borgo Savaian, Fazi Battaglia (unless you specifically want orange)
- **Sweet bottles** (great wines but not house whites): Vietti Moscato, Michele Chiarlo Nivole Moscato, La Spinetta Moscato, Bartenura Moscato
- **Sub-$10 mass-market Bordeaux Blanc**: Recougne, Antonins, Roquefort
