## False positives caught and excluded

- *Champagne Michel Gonet* (Avize) ≠ Gonet-Médeville (Bisseuil) — same family branch, different estate
- *Savart Champagne* (Écueil) → "Dremont & Savart Éphémère 017 Bouzy GC" at Raeder's — looks like a joint cuvée, **possibly related to the GCS "Ephemeral Champagne" entry**. Flag for verification.
- *The Horées* / *The Round Stone* — these are English-translated GCS labels; without the original French names the matcher fell back on the generic "the" token, producing noise
- *Cassiopeia Estate* — no Raeder's match (no false positive surfaced)
- *Icy Liu* — unique enough name, no match

## Methodology

1. Source landed at `raw/grand_cru/portfolio_2026-05-21.md` (72 producers across 12 region sections).
2. Cross-check script: normalize name (NFKD ASCII, lowercase, hyphens→spaces, strip prefixes like *Domaine/Château/Cave/Estate*), drop generic words and given names, require **all** remaining tokens ≥3 chars to appear as whole words in the Raeder's `producer` field.
3. Hand-audit each match against the GCS region tag and Raeder's cuvée — false positives noted above.
