# Acker + Blacksmith Retail Top Picks (2026-06)

Two retail lists, screened together. **Acker** (Fine & Rare stocklist, dated
6/8/26): 1,661 items, Burgundy-dominated (944), trophy-heavy — DRC, Coche,
Leroy, Harlan — but with a real grower/value seam underneath. **Blacksmith
Wines** (sale catalog, 6/12/26): 159 items with markdowns of 10–45%, mostly
Bordeaux/Napa/port, a few aged-Bandol and Burgundy sleepers. Cult-hedonist
Napa (Harlan, Colgin, SQN, Hundred Acre, Screaming Eagle, Bryant, Scarecrow,
Abreu, Sloan, Bond, Promontory — hundreds of slots on both lists) skipped
per the taste filter.

## Skip / fails the filter

- Both lists' cult-Napa walls (Harlan, Colgin, SQN, Hundred Acre, Screaming
  Eagle, Schrader, Bryant, Scarecrow, Abreu, Sloan, Bond, Promontory,
  Maybach, Tusk, Vérité, Caymus, Paul Hobbs, Marcassin) — opulence tier,
  not site-transparent.
- Acker trophy Burgundy (DRC, Coche ×57, Leroy, Rousseau, Roumier, Liger-
  Belair, Prieuré Roch ×26) — collector-priced, no value angle.
- Generic NV Champagne (Ruinart, Armand de Brignac), generic-tier Tuscany
  (Tignanello/Sassicaia walls, Ornellaia), Blacksmith whiskey/armagnac/port
  blocks, Pavie/Pavie-Decesse modernist St-Émilion.

## Watch-outs

- Acker list is dated 6/8/26 with no condition notes — fine-and-rare stock
  is mixed-provenance; ask for fill/label photos on anything pre-2000.
- Blacksmith flags condition inline (label issues, shoulder fill, stained
  labels) — priced accordingly, confirm before buying.
- Blacksmith Laroche Chablis Vaudevey 2022 shows $57 with no sale price —
  listing error, ignore.
