# Ceritas Auction Watch (2026-06)

Cellar context: 14 Ceritas cuvée-vintages owned (~$59–85 paid for the
standard chardonnays/pinots; $98–215 for Occidental, Porter-Bass and the
PMR Cab). Near-$50 at auction means buying the entry/mid cuvées *below*
mailing-list price — very doable; the upper-tier single vineyards are not
$50 wines anywhere.

## Where Ceritas trades

Only two venues, both retail-adjacent weekly auctions:

- **[WineBid](https://www.winebid.com/BuyWine/Producer/Ceritas/13164)** —
  the main marketplace. Producer page reported ~4 active items at search
  time. Factor the buyer's premium (~20%): a **$41 hammer ≈ $50 all-in**.
- **[K&L Auctions](https://www.klwines.com/Auctions)** — occasional
  consignment lots, no buyer's premium.

The classic houses don't carry it: nothing at HDH, Acker, Zachys or
Spectrum, and zero Ceritas lots in either catalog already landed in
`raw/auctions/` (261W's 1,965 lots; Zachys Palm Beach's 1,742 lots).
Small-production mailing-list California just doesn't reach that channel.
